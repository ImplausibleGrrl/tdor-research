===== Man in Skirt Stabbed To Death on L.A. Street =====

A man dressed as a woman was stabbed to death early today on a Los Angeles street.

Newton St. detectives said the victim lived as Clara Johnson at 986 E. 51st St. 

His body was discovered at 2 a.m. on 51st about 50 feet east of Wadsworth Ave.

Police at first believed Johnson was a traffic victim until an examination disclosed he was stabbed in the neck.

Police said Johnson's legal first name was Eugene. He was about 50 .

His body whs discovered by motorist not long after the victim left a Central Ave bar.

Det. L. L. Hightower said Johnson wore a pink blouse, flowered skirt and stockings. There were no shoes on the feet. 

Det. Hightower- said Johnson had a 14-year-record of masquerading as a woman.  He was arrested more than a dozen times.
