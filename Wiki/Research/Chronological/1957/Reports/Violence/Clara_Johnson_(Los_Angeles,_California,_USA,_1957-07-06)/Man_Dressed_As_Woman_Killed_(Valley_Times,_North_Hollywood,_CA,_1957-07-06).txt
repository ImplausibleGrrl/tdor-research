===== Man Dressed As Woman Killed =====

A man dressed as a woman was stabbed to death early today on a Los Angeles street.

Newton street detectives said the victim lived as Clara Johnson at 986 E. 51st St.

His body was discovered at 2 am. on 51st street east of Wadsworth avenue.

Police at first believed Johnson was a traffic victim until an examination disclosed he was stabbed in the neck.
