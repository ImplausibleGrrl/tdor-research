===== HUB MAN FATALLY SHOT =====

BOSTON (AP) —James Smith, 28, was shot and killed early today in his apartment at 29 Wyoming St. in the Roxbury district.

Police said Smith was dead on arrival at Boston City Hospital. Smith had three bullet wounds in his stomach.

Police said Smith apparently was shot by another man after a quarrel witnessed by two women.
