Police Investigate Transsexual Death

Cause of death is still uncertain. Case was registered as suspicious death
By Marcus Pontes - by Suzano 12 JUN 2018 - 1:13 pm

Victim was taken to the Municipal Emergency Room (PS) of Suzano, but died in the hospital unit Photo: Sabrina Silva/Disclosure

Police are investigating the death of 31-year-old transsexual Isabelly Cândido de Mello. She died last Saturday, the 9th. Information about the case was released this Tuesday (12). 

According to the investigation, Isabelly was taken to the Emergency Room (PS) in Suzano, but died in hospital. In the bulletin, the family member said he hadn't seen her for days. And that in the last contact, the hairdresser said she was starting to have pneumonia.

Isabelly's family also noted that she had bodily injuries. Excoriations on the hand, chest and face, but they do not know what the circumstances of the injuries would be. They also said that the victim was separating from his current partner.

An inquiry was launched to investigate the case. Police ordered an examination to determine the cause of death.
