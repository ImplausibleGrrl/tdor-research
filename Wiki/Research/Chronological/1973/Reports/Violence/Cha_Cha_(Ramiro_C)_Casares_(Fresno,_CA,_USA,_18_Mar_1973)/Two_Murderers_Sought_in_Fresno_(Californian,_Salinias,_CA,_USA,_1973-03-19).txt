Two Murderers Sought in Fresno

FRESNO (UPI) — Police today investigated two weekend murders, one a shooting death and the other a beating.

Officers said the body of Terry Lee Austin, 25, was found late Saturday night in a parking lot behind a Fresno bar. He had been shot once In the heart. No suspects have yet been apprehended.

Sunday morning, the body of Ramiro C Casares, 29, was found in a room at a west Fresno hotel.

Police said Casares had suffered extensive injuries to the back of his head apparently incurred in a beating.

Officers said Casares was believed to be a transvestite and when found was wearing womens undergarments.
