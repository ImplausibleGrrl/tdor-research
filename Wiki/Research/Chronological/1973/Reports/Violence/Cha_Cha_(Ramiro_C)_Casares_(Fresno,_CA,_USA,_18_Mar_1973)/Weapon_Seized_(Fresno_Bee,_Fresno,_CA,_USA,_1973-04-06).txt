=== Weapon Seized ===

Investigators said what is believed to have been weapon used in the shooting also was found in the house.

Sheriffs deputies said the case has been turned over to Fresno police because the killing took place in the city Saldivar had been questioned by Fresno police in connection with the unsolved beating death of Ramiro C Casares 29 whose body was found March 18 in a room in the Hotel Walters at 564 F St.

=== Women’s Clothing ===

Casares, also known as Cha Cha, was dressed in women’s clothing when his body was found officers said.

Police said Saldivar was not a suspect in the killing but that he was questioned on Saturday afternoon
