Police are also investigating the apparent bludgeon slaying of a 29-year-old man whose body was found yesterday morning in a room in the Hotel Walters at 564 F St.

Police said Ramiro C Casares was apparently killed some time between 3 a.m. and 10 a.m. yesterday. Jennie Hernandez, 47, who lives at the hotel told police she discovered the victim lying on the foot of the bed of her hotel room.

She said Casares had been staying in her room off and on in recent weeks. Police said the victim was struck in the head, possibly with a metal chair which was in the room.

Garfield Morgan Jr, 22, the night manager of the hotel told police he saw the victim at about 3 am with three men in the hotal hallway. lHe did not detect anything unusual at the time police said When the man was found he was wearing women's clothing including a long-sleeved blouse, panty hose and a yellow mini skirt, police reported.
