> Trans woman from Pará dies in Italy and family asks for help in transferring the body
> | MAY 26, 2023,
> 
> Bruna Cancio dos Santos, from Pará, aged 36, died after falling from the sixth floor of a building on the afternoon of Thursday, 25, in the city of Rimini, Italy. Bruna was a transsexual woman who had lived in the European country for just over a year, but before moving, Bruna lived in Marituba, in the metropolitan region of Belém.
> 
> To the Italian news, the police reported that in the investigations, all possibilities are being considered, from homicide to suicide.
> 
> Still according to police information, when the agents arrived at the apartment where the fall occurred, there was no one there besides Bruna.
> 
> At the scene, the police found broken window glass and Bruna had cuts on her arm. It is worth noting that the woman from Pará lived with a friend, but it is not known whether the place where the death occurred was Bruna's house.
> 
> What Bruna's family says:
> 
> At Portal Roma News , Bruna Cancio's niece, Beatriz Dória, said that the family knows almost nothing about the case, just what the police reported to the news in Italy.
> 
> “We contacted the Brazilian consulate, who promised us more information on the morning of Friday the 26th”, said Beatriz.
> 
> The niece asks for help to transfer her aunt's body as soon as possible.
