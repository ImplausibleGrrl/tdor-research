> Ben Amics urges the administrations to investigate the suicide of a young trans woman in Lloseta
> Europa Press Balearic Islands
> Published: Monday, June 26, 2023 1:39 PM
> @epbaleares
> PALMA, June 26 (EUROPA PRESS) -
> 
> The Ben Amics association has lamented the suicide of a 15-year-old transgender girl on Thursday June 22 in Lloseta and has urged the relevant administrations to clarify the causes of the suicide.
> 
> In a statement published on Twitter this Monday, the entity has ensured that "the profiles on the victim's social networks show that she was an openly LGTBI+ young woman" and that "she identified with the female gender, which does not correspond to the assigned sex at birth.
> 
> Thus, Ben Amics has urged the relevant administrations to clarify the causes that led the young woman, who was of school age, to take her own life.
> 
> The association recalled that the LGTBI Comprehensive Care Service of the Consell de Mallorca offers social, psychological and legal support to LGTBI+ people, especially victims of LGTBI-phobia. In addition, 024 is a hotline for suicidal behavior of the Ministry of Health.
