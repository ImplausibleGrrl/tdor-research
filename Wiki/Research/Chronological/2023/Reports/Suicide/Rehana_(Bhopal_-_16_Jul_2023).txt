> Ailing woman dies by suicide in Piplani
> TNN / Jul 18, 2023, 08:14 IST
> 
> Bhopal: A 39-year-old woman allegedly died by suicide at her home in Piplani locality on Sunday. Police recovered a suicide note in which she mentioned that she was fed-up of her illness and took the extreme step. Piplani police registered a case and started investigations. Police said that deceased Leela Bai, a resident of 50-Quarters Chandmari in Piplani, was a housewife while her husband Vijay Ahirwar is a daily wager. Vijay found Leela hanging and rushed her to a hospital where she was declared dead on arrival.
> 
> In another case, a 23-year-old transgender died by suicide at her home in Chhola locality on Sunday. No suicide note was recovered but police suspect that she took the extreme step after a dispute with her live-in-partner. Chhola police, who registered a case, said that the deceased had underwent a sex change surgery and was residing in a live-in relationship with an auto-driver. When the auto-driver returned home around 10am on Sunday, Rehana was found hanging from the ceiling fan. Acting on information, Chhola police reached the spot. It was revealed that a dispute took place between the couple on Saturday after which the auto-driver left home. 
>
> Further investigations were on.
