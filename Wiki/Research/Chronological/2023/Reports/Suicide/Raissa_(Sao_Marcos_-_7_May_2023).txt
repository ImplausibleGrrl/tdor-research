> The body of a young transsexual, identified only as Raíssa, was found this Sunday, 7, in a sports court in the village of São Marcos, in Major Izidoro, in the Sertão of Alagoas. The 20-year-old was located without vital signs, inside the locker room on the court.
> 
> The Legal Medical Institute (IML) of Arapiraca was triggered to collect the body. According to the examination report, the cause of death would have been by hanging. There is a possibility that Raíssa committed suicide, but the police will investigate a possible homicide.
> 
> The victim's mother has already been to the IML awaiting the victim's release for burial.
