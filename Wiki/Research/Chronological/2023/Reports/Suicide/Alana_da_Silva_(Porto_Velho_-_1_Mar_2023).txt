> TRAGEDY: Transvestite found dead in the east of Porto Velho
> Photo: Rondoniaovivo
> 
> A transvestite identified as Ivanilson da Silva, 27, known as "Alana" was found dead this Wednesday afternoon (01) in a residence on Rua Plutão, Planalto neighborhood, east zone of Porto Velho (RO).
> 
> The Military Police, Criminal Expertise and the Legal Medical Institute (IML) were called by the victim's sister.
> Alana was found dead in one of the rooms of the residence. A rope was seized next to her body.
> No one at the scene could say why the transvestite had committed such a desperate act. 
> After the expertise works, the body was removed to the IML.
