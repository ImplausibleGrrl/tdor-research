> GABRIEL GODOY'S (ANE) BODY IS FOUND NEAR CANAL BRIDGE
> The Jaú Fire Department found this afternoon, around 17h30, the body of Gabriel Godoy (Ane) who had thrown himself from the Ponte do Açúcar bridge on the morning of last Tuesday. The body was found near Ponte Victorino Didon, the canal bridge.
>
> Gabriel, who adopted the social name since July last year, is from the city of São Manuel, and for reasons not yet revealed, came with his car, a Goal, Botucatu plate, stood on the bridge and played. Fact confirmed and witnessed by a fisherman.
>
> Since then, the Fire Department had been looking for the body of the 29-year-old boy.
>
> The funeral car waits in the Marina Tietê region for the arrival of the IML - Legal Medical Institute - to release the body.
