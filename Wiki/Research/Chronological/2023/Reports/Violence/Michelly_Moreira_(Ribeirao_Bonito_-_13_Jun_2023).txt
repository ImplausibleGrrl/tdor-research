> Transvestite is beaten to death in Ribeirão Bonito
> June 13, 2023 - 2:09 pm June 13, 2023
> 
> Transvestite is beaten to death in Ribeirão Bonito
> A homicide marked the beginning of the morning of this Tuesday, 13, when Fabiano Trigo Bonani, 45 years old, who adopted the social name of Michelly Moreira, was killed with clubs on Rua João GP Ferraz, in Jardim Helena, in Ribeirão Bonito. The crime allegedly took place around 1:37 am.
> 
> A neighbor saw the body lying in the middle of the street and called the Military Police who, upon arriving at the scene, witnessed the first assistance given by rescuers, who went to the city hospital.
> 
> Professionals who assisted the victim noticed injuries to the face that would be bloodied, in addition to injuries to the left leg and hands. There are indications that they would be fractured.
> 
> It is also said that the victim's body would be without clothes from the waist down and the PMs, in search of information, even obtained it from a security guard who would have seen the victim still alive screaming for help and would be drunk. The possible murder weapon was not located nearby. The Scientific Police began the work that now follows for investigators who will try to locate the author of the crime of death. The fact registered in the CPJ.
> 
> The president of the MDB in the city issued a note of repudiation and asked for the identification of the author of the crime. Check it out in full:
> 
> NOTE OF REPUDIATION AND CONGRESS
> This Tuesday (13), we woke up under the impact of an unacceptable brutality that occurred this morning in Ribeirão Bonito and which resulted in the cruel murder of FABIANO TRIGO BONANI, at the age of 45.
> A member of the LGBT+ community, he adopted MICHELLY MOREIRA as his social name and, that night, he was found seriously injured on a street in the Jardim Heliana I neighborhood with signs of beatings.
> We demand from the local authorities the greatest celerity in the investigations and the proper identification of the author of this violence with the strict accountability and application of the applicable punishments, according to the Law.
> We would like to express our deep solidarity with their parents, Noemi Trigo and Claudinei Bonani, and with the other bereaved family members, relatives and friends.
