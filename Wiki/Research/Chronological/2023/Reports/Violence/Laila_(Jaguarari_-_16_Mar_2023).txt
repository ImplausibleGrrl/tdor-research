> A trans woman, known as Laila, 27 years old, was shot dead inside a house, on Thursday night (16), in the city of Jaguarari, in the north of Bahia.
>
> According to the Civil Police of the municipality, the motivation for the crime is not yet known, since she was found alone in the property.
>
> Laila was rescued by teams from the Mobile Emergency Care Service (Samu) and taken to the Municipal Hospital of Jaguarari, but did not resist her injuries.
>
> The police are investigating the authorship of the murder.
