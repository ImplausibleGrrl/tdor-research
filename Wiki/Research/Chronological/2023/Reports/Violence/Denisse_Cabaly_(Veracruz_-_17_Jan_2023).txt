> The young trans woman Denisse Cerón Mendoza, alias Denisse Cabaly, was beaten to death in the market area of ​​the city of Veracruz. It is a lonely area, associated with crime. According to her witnesses, two subjects viciously beat her until she was unconscious. Denisse, she was 29 years old and was transferred to the Veracruz High Specialty Hospital where she died due to the injuries she had. Nobody intervened. Denisse was passing through the city and she was afraid for her life, because in 2018 she witnessed the murder of [Alaska](https://tdor.translivesmatter.info/reports/2018/07/25/alaska-bout_martinez-de-la-torre-veracruz-mexico_92714271), her roommate in Tlapacoyan.
> 
> Originally from Las Libres (Puebla), she had lived in Teziutlán (Puebla) since the murder of Alaska, where she did sex work and provided community services.
> 
> The activist Jazz Bustamante told Presentes that at some point Denisse went to ask her for help because she was afraid.
> 
> "A very attentive, helpful, empathetic person and I think it should be noted that she asked me for help on one occasion, I summoned her to my lawyer's office (...) she said that she was afraid because she had been one of the witnesses that the Prosecutor's Office He pulled because he lived a lot with Alaska Contreras (…) she was upset, with a lot of fear and a certain paranoia, you asked her about the murder of Alaska and she was upset because she said that her murderer was free,” said Bustamante.



> LIBRES, Pue.- A transsexual woman named Denisse , originally from this municipality of Libres , was brutally murdered on Tuesday night, on the streets of the Port of Veracruz , where, according to witnesses, she was attacked by at least two individuals who they gave him a beating, causing him serious injuries that caused his death minutes later.
> 
> According to close sources, they confirmed that said woman, also known as Denisse Caballi, 29 years old , was originally from the municipality of Libres, with current residence in the city of Martínez de la Torre Veracruz , where she carried out sex work, undertaking trips constant to the port of Veracruz and other cities, to also practice their trade.
> 
> In this sense, it was informed that the woman was attacked on the central streets of said Veracruz city, whose attackers beat her repeatedly until she was left unconscious and lying on the pavement, causing a severe contusion to her head, which led to her being injured. his death.
> 
> In the same way, these sources close to the family confirmed that, despite the fact that Denisse received the attention of the emergency forces, who gave her first aid, and then transferred her to a hospital in the area, she lost her life in the way to said hospital.
> 
> 
> In this way, his remains were transferred to the Forensic Medical Service of said city, where hours later they were identified by their relatives, who traveled from the city of Libres to be able to claim the body , which, until Wednesday night, did not it had been handed over by the Veracruz State Attorney General's Office.
> 
> It should be noted that Denisse Caballi would have stood out in this northeastern region, for having participated in various beauty contests , occupying the title of Nuestra Belleza Gay Trayectoria Teziutlán Puebla 2018 , among other participations, with which she achieved the sympathy and friendships of the community LGBTTIQ and more, within this part of the entity.
> 
> For this reason and after an unfortunate event, it has been the members of said community, at least from this northeastern region, who, through social networks, have shown their discontent around said murder, demanding justice from the Veracruz authorities.




> In the middle of a pool of blood, Denisse Cabaly, a  35-year-old trans woman  asked for help trying to survive, after the brutal beating that some men gave her on Abasolo street, between Díaz Mirón and González Pagés in the city of Veracruz.
> 
> After total silence, Denisse became unconscious and was never able to wake up. People around her who noticed the fact, during the first hours of the day, called the police and an ambulance to attend to what had happened.
> 
> The paramedics checked her vital signs and observed that the woman required urgent care, so they transferred her to the Veracruz Regional Hospital, however, the doctors could no longer do anything to save her life.
> 
> According to the witnesses, the woman who worked as a sex worker in the market area was with some men who, after starting an argument with her, brutally beat her until she was left lying on the ground.
> 
> Realizing the seriousness of what happened, those responsible fled and Denisse was not able to get up, it was until dawn this Wednesday, when merchants in the area, who were beginning to open their stores, noticed that a person was lying there with serious injuries. .
> 
> In the first news that was disseminated in the media, information was requested about the victim's relatives, it was then that people from the municipality of Libres, belonging to the state of Puebla, confirmed that Denisse was her relative.
> 
> The trans woman was a sex worker and although she was in an area where nightlife allows this type of activity, so far, no one has identified those responsible.
> 
> Members and activists of the LGBT + community assure that Denisse's death should be investigated as a hate crime, however, so far the details of the investigation carried out by the Veracruz State Attorney General's Office are unknown.





> VERACRUZ, VER.- Denisse, a 35-year-old trans woman who worked as a sex worker, died in the Regional Hospital of the city of Veracruz due to a beating by two individuals in the market area of this municipality.
> 
> The attack occurred during the night of this Tuesday, on calle Abasolo, between Salvador Díaz Mirón and González Pagés avenues.
> 
> Passersby who located her when she was dying gave the report to the Red Cross paramedics.
> 
> The victim had blows to various parts of the body and bruises to the head. She was immediately taken to the hospital, where she died minutes later in the emergency room.
> 
> Denisse, it was reported, was originally from the municipality of Libres, in Puebla, and lived in Tlapacoyan, Veracruz.



> Veracruz, Ver. (Veracruz Vanguard).- A trans woman was deprived of her life on Tuesday night when she was in the Abasolo alley, between Salvador Díaz Mirón avenue and González Pagés street in the Centro neighborhood.
