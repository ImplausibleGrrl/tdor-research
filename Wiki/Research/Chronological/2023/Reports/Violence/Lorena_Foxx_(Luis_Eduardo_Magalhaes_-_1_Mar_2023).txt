> On the afternoon of this Wednesday, the 1st, shortly after 3 pm, a mower operator found the body of the transsexual Lorena Fox, who disappeared for almost eight days, after getting into an unidentified vehicle on Avenida Enedino Alves da Paixão, close to the old Luís Eduardo Magalhães bus station.
> 
> The body was found in an area of ??grass at the end of Avenida Tancredo Neves, next to a sector of condominiums in the Jardim Paraíso neighborhood. A black shoe was found next to the body, which was already in an advanced state of decomposition. Lorena's family even offered a reward to whoever informs the whereabouts of the transsexual.
> 
> The Civil Police continues to listen to witnesses, family members, acquaintances of Lorena and looking for images from security cameras to arrive at the authorship and motivation of the homicide. The Civil and Military Police, in addition to the Municipal Guard, are at the scene of the crime to carry out expertise. // Sigi Vilares

> Wednesday, March 1, 2023 - Last updated 8 hours ago
> 
> Around 4 pm this Wednesday (01), a mower machine operator found a body in a bush area, on Avenida Tancredo Neves, in the Jardim Paraíso phase II neighborhood, in Luis Eduardo Magalhães.
> 
> According to information gathered, the operator was skimming the area when he saw a corpse on the spot. Immediately, he made contact with the Military Police of the 85th CIPM, which went to the indicated place.
> 
> The Chief of Police, Dr. Joaquim Rodrigues, together with the investigators, were at the scene collecting information and found a pair of women's sneakers supposedly belonging to the victim.
> 
> Then, the Technical Police Department (DPT) was called, carried out the expertise and removed the body to the DPT in Barreiras, in order to perform a necropsy examination.
> 
> The body found is the 27-year-old transsexual Thallyson Daniel Alves de Lima, known as Lorena Foxx, (self-declaration of gender: transvestite) born in Alvorada do Norte/GO. The case will be investigated by the Civil Police.
