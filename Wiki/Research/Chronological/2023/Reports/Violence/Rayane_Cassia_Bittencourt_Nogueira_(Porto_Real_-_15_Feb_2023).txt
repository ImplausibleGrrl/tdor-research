> A 21-year-old transsexual girl was found dead at home with signs of strangulation in Porto Real. The case took place on Wednesday (15) in the Vila Real neighborhood.
> According to the Military Police (PM), neighbors noticed a strong smell coming from inside the property. The young woman's uncle managed to jump out the window of the house and find the body.
> 
> In addition to the strangulation, Civil Police experts also found signs of blows to the head. The victim's body was taken by the Fire Department to the Legal Medical Institute (IML) of Três Poços, in Volta Redonda.
> 
> The case is being investigated by the 100th Police Station of Porto Real as a homicide. Witnesses and family members have already been summoned to testify.
> 
> In a statement, the Quatis LGBTI Citizenship Center, which covers the Agulhas Negras region, said it is providing assistance to the family with social and psychological care, as well as legal advice if necessary.
