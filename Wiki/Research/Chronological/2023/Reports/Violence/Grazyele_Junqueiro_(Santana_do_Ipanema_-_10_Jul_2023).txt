> Trans woman beaten in Santana do Ipanema dies after seven days in hospital
> The family reported that Grazi was in a coma and her health was serious; Two people have already been arrested by the Alagoas Civil Police
> 7/10/2023 2:17 pm - Updated on 7/10/2023 2:35 pm
> Grazy was brutally beaten indoors because of a debt
> 
> The trans woman Grazi, aged 38, could not resist the injuries and died this Monday (10), at the Hospital de Emergência do Agreste (HEA), in Arapiraca. A week ago, she was beaten inside her house, in Santana do Ipanema, a motivated crime, according to the police, because of a debt of R$900.
> 
> According to information from the Scientific Police, a team from the Legal Medical Institute of Arapiraca (IML) was called in around 12 noon today to collect the victim's body from the hospital. The victim's brother confirmed to Gazetaweb that Grazy died at 9:47 am today and that he and his sisters are in Arapiraca to arrange for the release of the body.
> 
> Two suspects in the crime have already been arrested by the Civil Police of Alagoas (PC/AL). The first was Kayque Silva dos Santos, who was found with the victim's cell phone. Genilson de Melo Viana, 30, the second accused of the attacks, said in a statement that he was called by Kayque to collect the victim's debt. In the end, he would receive R$ 100 for the service.




> A transsexual, identified as Grazy, was seriously injured after being beaten up. The case was registered on the morning of this Monday, 03, inside Grazy's house, in the Lajeiro Grande neighborhood, in Santana do Ipanema, Sertão de Alagoas.
> 
> According to information passed on to the newsroom, Grazy's house was invaded and she was severely beaten. She was found with a cut on her forehead and several marks of beatings, later, Grazy was rescued by an ambulance from the Mobile Emergency Care Service (SAMU), in serious condition and taken to a hospital unit.
> 
> Military Police and Civil Police agents were called to the scene and the case will be investigated.
