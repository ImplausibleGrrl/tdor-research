> Transvestite is shot dead in the Nossa Senhora Aparecida neighborhood in Patos de Minas
> The crime would have occurred after a disagreement with a couple of boyfriends. Suspects remain at large
> 
> NEWS | COPS February 12, 2023 0
> 
> Transvestite is shot dead in the Nossa Senhora Aparecida neighborhood in Patos de Minas
> 
> A 23-year-old transvestite was killed early this Sunday (12/02) in a bar on Rua Joaquim Fubá, neighborhood Nossa Senhora Aparecida, in Patos de Minas. A couple of lovers, suspected of the crime, are still on the run.
> 
> According to the incident report, the military already found Sofia lying on the sidewalk and unconscious. She was shot in the face, arm and chest. SAMU and Fire Department units tried to revive her, but she died at the scene.
> 
> Witnesses told the police that Sofia was at the bar, when the author arrived at the scene accompanied by his girlfriend. The woman allegedly had a disagreement with the transvestite and the young man intervened and began to threaten Sofia.
> 
> Nervous, the boy pulled out a gun and shot the transvestite in the face. She ran, but was chased and hit by more shots.
> 
> After the crime, the boy took his motorcycle and fled, taking the woman, towards Ponte do Arco.
> 
> The technical expertise of the Civil Police carried out the usual work and sent Sofia's body, civil name Patrick Gonçalves de Oliveira, to the Legal Medical Institute (IML). The case will be investigated by the Civil Police.



> The Military Police are looking for a couple of lovers suspected of being involved in the murder of a 23-year-old transvestite in Patos de Minas. The couple would have argued with her in a bar and the man would have fired several shots at her. Sofhia Gonçalves even ran from the scene after being hit in the face, but she was hit by more shots and could not resist her injuries.
> 
> The crime took place during the early hours of this Sunday (12), on Avenida Joaquim Fubá, in the Nossa Senhora Aparecida neighborhood. According to the occurrence of the Military Police, the corporation was activated informing that there had been several firearm shots in the bar and Patrick Gonçalves de Oliveira, known as Sofhia, had been shot and was lying on the sidewalk.
> 
> Police officers went to the scene and identified that she had suffered punctures in her face, arm and chest. SAMU and the Fire Department went to the scene, but the doctor ended up noting the death. An informant told the Military Police that the crime would have happened after the arrival of a couple of lovers in the trade.
> 
> For passionate reasons, the woman would have started an argument with Sofhia, when her boyfriend intervened and started threatening the victim with a firearm. At one point, he shot her in the face. The victim ran away and the shooter followed, firing more shots. At the intersection with Rua José Cassiano Caixeta, she fell and ended up dying.
> 
> The shooter fled on a motorcycle towards the Paranaíba River bridge. Civil Police technical expertise was on site to check how everything happened. 5 rounds of .32 caliber ammunition were collected. The police went to where the shooter would be living, close to the crime scene, but he was not found. Searches continue to locate him. He and his girlfriend have already been identified.
> 
> On Instagram, Sofhia called herself a blogger, dancer, hairdresser and eyebrow designer. She had nearly 3,000 followers. In addition to her moments of fun, Sofhia also showed in her profile a little of her work with eyebrows and hairdressing.
