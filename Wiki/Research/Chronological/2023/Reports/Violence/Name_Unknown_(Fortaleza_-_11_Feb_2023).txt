> Transvestite murdered in Osório de Paiva: residents point out that it was "announced tragedy"
> On February 11, 2022 and February 11, 2023, two transvestites were killed on land on Avenida Osório de Paiva. Residents highlight security, public lighting and drug trafficking problems
> 19:02 | Feb. 13, 2023
> A 27-year-old transvestite was stabbed to death on Avenida Osório de Paiva, in the Siqueira neighborhood, in Fortaleza. The case was registered on Saturday, the 11th. For residents of the region, the crime was an "announced tragedy". As O POVO found out, the victim was stabbed inside a plot of land that works as a prostitution spot. In addition to darkness and abandonment, the neighborhood's complaint also relates to the illegal sale of narcotics.
> 
> The crime on Avenida Osório de Paiva took place exactly on the day that completed one year since the death of transvestite Sofia Gisely, the victim of a homicide with stones on the same avenue.  
> 
> Those who need to go through the place daily fear robberies due to the lack of lighting. According to residents, whose names have been preserved, the motel located next to the land is used by people who stay nearby to carry out sexual programs. And the land is also used for the same purposes.
> 
> In Saturday's case, as O POVO found out, the victim was stabbed inside the lot and fled to the entrance of the motel, but did not resist. The knife used in the crime was stuck in his chest.
> 
> Motivation was not informed, but the neighborhood suspects a conflict over a sex program or dispute over a prostitution point. The Secretariat for Public Security and Social Defense (SSPDS), however, reported that investigations are carried out by the Department of Homicide and Personal Protection (DHPP). According to the SSPDS, the victim had a criminal record for illegally carrying a firearm and drug trafficking. The victim's name has not been released.
>
> One year after the death of Sofia Giselle
> On February 11th of this year, Sofia Giselle's death was one year old. A 23-year-old transvestite who was killed with stones, also on a piece of land on Avenida Osório de Paiva. The two crimes happened on the same date, with a difference of one year. According to family members, Sofia prostituted herself on Avenida Osório de Paiva.
>
> On February 15, 2017, Dandara dos Santos was beaten, stoned and shot to death. She was taken in a wheelbarrow and the crime was filmed. The atrocity that killed Dandara and Sofia was also committed against the transvestite, whose name has yet to be announced, who was killed on Saturday. She had a knife stuck in her chest. A video shows the victim on the ground with dozens of people around, the slipper off the foot and the flashing light illuminating the crime scene.
>
> By means of a note, the Ceará Military Police (PMCE) reported that patrolling in the Siqueira neighborhood, in Fortaleza, where Gen. Osório de Paiva, is the responsibility of the 17th Battalion and is carried out by General Ostensive Policing (POG) and Tactical Force (FT) vehicles. Also, teams from the Policing Command for Intensive and Ostensive Action Rounds (CPRaio) and the Shock Policing Command (CPShoque) reinforce policing in the area.
> 
> “We reaffirm that the PMCE is always available and can be called at any time of the day by calling 190, in case of any suspicious action. that a citizen is the victim of a crime, as this is one of the relevant instruments on which the public security forces of the state rely to guide their preventive and repressive actions against crime", describes the note.
