> A trans woman was murdered with clubs around 3 am this Friday, 10, on Avenida Afonso Nunes, on the other side of the lagoon, in Grussaí, district of São João da Barra.
> 
> According to police information, the victim identified as Amanda and with civil registration in the name of Fábio Freire Rodrigues Ribeiro, 39, was found dead inside a residence.
> 
> Populars informed the military that Amanda would be running down the street with men who would be with a piece of wood from a hoe in their hands carrying out aggressions against the trans woman. Amanda even screamed at the residents' door asking for water, but she couldn't resist the injuries and died on the balcony of a residence.
> 
> Amanda's body showed blood marks and signs of beatings. Police are looking for suspects in the crime. Security camera footage will be used to identify the perpetrators.
> 
> The case is still being investigated by the 145th Police Station of São João da Barra. The body was sent to the Legal Medical Institute (IML), in Campos dos Goytacazes, where it will undergo an autopsy and will soon be released for burial.



> A trans woman, known as Amanda, 39 years old, was found dead on the balcony of a residence last Thursday night (09) on Avenida Afonso Nunes, on the other side of the lagoon, in Grussaí, district of São João da Barra. .
> 
> According to information from the Military Police, the victim was found with blood marks on his legs, with signs of beating. She would have entered a house asking for water and help, then fell to the ground. 
> 
> An ambulance was called, but the victim could not resist and died at the scene. There is still no information about the motivation and authorship of the crime. The body was taken to the Legal Medical Institute (IML) in Campos. 
> 
> Police officers carried out searches in the region, but no one was detained as of this writing. The case was registered at the 145th Police Station.
