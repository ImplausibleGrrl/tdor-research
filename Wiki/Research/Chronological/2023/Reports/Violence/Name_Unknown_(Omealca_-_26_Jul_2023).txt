An unidentified trans woman was shot dead on a road in Omealca, Veracruz (Mexico) on 26 Jul 2023.


> Transvestite person was executed with bullets, in Omealca
> Crime occurred on the San Pablo Ojo de Agua-Mata Tenatito road
> Benito Juárez Ramírez Omealca, Ver. 07/26/2023
> alcalorpolitico.com
> 
> A transvestite was murdered from several firearm shots in the early hours of this Wednesday morning, on the San Pablo Ojo de Agua-Mata Tenatito road.
> 
> Residents who noticed the discovery immediately alerted the police authorities through the emergency number 911.
> 
> Elements of the Mexican Navy Secretariat arrived at the scene to cordon off the place and preserve the scene following the chain of custody protocol. .
> 
> The staff of the Regional Prosecutor of Justice took notice and integrated an investigation folder in this regard.
> 
> Criminalistics experts arrived to carry out an ocular inspection and the assurance of any type of indication to later make way for the removal of the body that was transferred to SEMEFO in Córdoba.
