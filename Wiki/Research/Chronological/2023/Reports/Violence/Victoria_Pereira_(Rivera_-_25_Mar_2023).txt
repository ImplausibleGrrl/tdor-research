> Victoria Pereira, a trans woman murdered in Rivera, was one of the protagonists of Naturaleza Trans, a play that premiered at the Solís Theater
> Posted onMarch 25, 2023
> 
> Marianella Morena, director of the work, recalled the creation process and the essence of the murdered woman; the work was at the FIBA in Buenos Aires and had an invitation to exhibit it in Spain.
> Victoria Pereira was murdered this Saturday morning in Rivera . She was a 22-year-old trans woman, who was born in Brazil. She was stabbed in the Villa Sonia neighborhood. It is being investigated whether her case was a transfemicide or if the homicide had another motive.
> 
> Victoria was one of the protagonists of Naturaleza trans , a documentary work directed by Marianella Morena, in which three trans women share their life stories, with the violations, marginalities and challenges of experiencing their gender identities in Rivera. Nicole Casaravilla and Alisson Sánchez also performed in the play.





> A trans woman was stabbed to death
> The Rivera police work to clarify the authority and motives of the homicide
> 
> Camilo dos Santos
> March 25, 2023 at 5:31 pm
> A 22-year-old trans woman was stabbed to death this Saturday morning in Rivera, sources from the Police Headquarters of that department confirmed to  El Observador .
> 
> It happened on calle Manuel Meléndez in the Villa Sonia neighborhood in the north of the city of Rivera, near the border with Brazil.
> 
> Rivera police are working to clarify the authority and motives for the homicide. The Scientific Police and the Prosecutor's Office are also working on the case.
