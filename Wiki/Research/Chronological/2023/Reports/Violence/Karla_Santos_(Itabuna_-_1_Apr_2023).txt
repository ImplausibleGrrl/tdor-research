burned


> A transsexual woman, whose identity has not been disclosed, was the victim of a cowardly attack, this Saturday morning (11), in Itabuna, in the south of Bahia. According to the Military Police, she is intubated in a hospital.
> 
> A man committed the crime around 8 am. At the time, the victim was accompanying his father during a surgical procedure at a hospital, when he began talking to the suspect by cell phone and arranged a meeting at the back of the health unit.
> 
> Soon after, both started a fight and the suspect threw alcohol and set the victim on fire. She entered the hospital with her body on fire and the security team helped the victim. The woman had to be intubated and had part of her face, head, abdomen, arms and back burned.
> 
> As reported by the victim's mother, both had a relationship of about 22 years. In addition, she claimed that the relationship was aggressive and the man was a drug user.
