> Last Wednesday, April 5, at around 11:45 p.m., a murder was recorded in the 13 block of calle Portugal in the El Porvenir district.
> 
> It is about a transgender stylist identified as Tania Viviana Ramírez, who was attacked with a knife by a foreign citizen inside her beauty shop, leaving her badly injured with deep wounds to her head, neck and arms. The attacker fled, leaving the victim mortally wounded, who agonized for several minutes until death.
> 
> The motive for the crime is still unknown, the first investigations indicate that it would be a settling of accounts for a debt that the victim had with foreign citizens.
> 
> Meanwhile, the criminal was arrested after locating him through video surveillance cameras. The victim was from Piura and lived in the CP El Milagro. Also, she was a member of the LGBT community.
> 
> It should be noted that a trans woman identified as Cristal, who was murdered under the same circumstances, was also murdered three years ago at that same address.


> Tania Viviana Ramirez was killed early this Thursday from several stabbings by a foreign citizen.
> Tania is born from Piura, district Twenty-six de Octubre, A-H Nueva Esperanza, currently based in Trujillo, district of Milagro, was a stylist and works in Porvenir.
> She integrated the LGBT community, from where activists demand justice and that this death does not go unpunished. So far the motives of his cruel murder are unknown
> According to some witnesses, the perpetrator would have been arrested following surveillance cameras
