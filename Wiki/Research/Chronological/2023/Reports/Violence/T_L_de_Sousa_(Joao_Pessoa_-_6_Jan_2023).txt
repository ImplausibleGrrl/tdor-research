> Transsexual is shot dead in Mandacaru jet wash, in João Pessoa
> January 6, 2023
> Around 1 am this Friday (6) a 24-year-old, identified as Thiago Lopes de Sousa, was shot dead near a car wash in the Mandacaru neighborhood, in João Pessoa.
> 
> Firearm shots were heard by residents who called the police and the body was found on the ground.
> 
> According to the expertise, four injuries caused by gunshots were detected on the body. According to police, the victim would be homeless and drug user.
> 
> The Homicide Police Department is investigating the case.
