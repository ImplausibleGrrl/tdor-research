> Jenny, Jesús and Carlos, victims of the attack in a Papantla bar
> Two men and a woman from the LGBT community were murdered inside the El Indido bar in Papantla. this is known
> Written in VERACRUZ on5/19/2023 10:27am
> 
> PAPANTLA, VER.- During the early hours of this Friday, May 19, 2023, the identification of the 3 victims of the attack that took place on Thursday night at the " El Indio" bar , located in the municipal capital of Papantla , was carried out. It was confirmed that the victims were two men and a transsexual woman, in charge of the establishment.
> 
> On Thursday night, an attack was recorded inside a bar located in the downtown area of ??Papantla , near the municipal town hall. The event was recorded and was uploaded to social networks by the attackers , alleged members of a criminal group that operates in the northern part of the state of Veracruz.
> 
> It was announced that two of the victims were murdered near the bar counter, one of them, identified as Jesús SH, who was left on the floor of the establishment and the second victim, who responded to the name of Carlos "N", was attacked from behind while sitting on a chair.
> 
> The member of the LGBTQ+ community who was identified as Jenny and manager of the El Indio bar, was murdered inside one of the local bathrooms, the woman was wearing a red blouse and dark pants. Relatives are expected to come to claim her remains of her.
> 
> So far, no authority from the Ministry of Public Security (SSP), the Veracruz State Attorney General's Office (FGE), Governor Cuitláhuac García Jiménez or the municipal mayor Eric Domínguez Vázquez , have issued a statement in this regard.
> 
> It is expected that in the next few hours more information will continue to be revealed about this event, which was recorded minutes after 8:00 pm this Thursday, a few meters from the Municipal Town Hall, in the heart of the city. So far, no arrests have been made of the attackers.
