> Oaxaca de Juarez. — After the murder of Paris Alexsandry , a member of the muxe community of Juchitán, who lost her life at the age of 39 after being shot by a firearm in a house in this town, the Oaxaca State Attorney General's Office opened the investigation file 3957/FIST/JUCHITAN/2023 for the crime of homicide .
> 
> In the case of Paris, so far there are no detainees. Meanwhile, the agency reported that the case is handled under the Protocol for the Care of People from the Lesbian, Gay, Transsexual, Transgender, Transsexual, Transvestite, and Intersex Community.
> 
> According to data from the report "Documentation of cases of violence and crimes against muxhe and trans people in the Isthmus of Tehuantepec", by Mexfam and Fundación Arcus, in the last fifteen years there have been 38 cases of violence against muxe and trans people , in nine municipalities. of this region.
> 
> Most of these episodes occurred in Juchitán, which had 13 attacks, followed by Unión Hidalgo with eight, Santo Domingo Tehuantepec with six, Santa María Mixtequilla with four, Santa María Xadani with three, and Chahuites, Santiago Niltepec, San Pedro Huilotepec and Santiago Ixtaltepec. with one in each of them.
> 
> The document details that of the 38 attacks, 25 were classified as hate crimes, the average age of the victims was 35 years old and more than half identified themselves as trans women or muxe'-gunaa, and 21% as trans men or muxe'nguiiu, coupled with the fact that 97.4% belonged to Zapotec peoples.
> 
> The victims were merchants, waitresses, housekeepers, motorcycle taxi drivers, artisans, stylists, cooks, seamstresses, teachers, people engaged in sex work, and employees of Petróleos Mexicanos.
> 
> Among these cases, that of Óscar Cazorla , activist and founder of the Auténticas Intrépidas Buscadoras del Perilo collective, stands out, who was assassinated on February 9, 2019 and whose crime remains unpunished.
> 
> This civil organization that works in favor of sexual diversity was jointly founded by Cazorla López and other muxes 44 years ago to make visible the inclusion of this vulnerable sector and, at the same time, eradicate homophobia and discrimination .




> Oaxaca de Juárez.- The Oaxaca State Attorney General's Office (FGEO) reported that it initiated an investigation folder for the crime of homicide , committed against París , a person belonging to the community of sexual diversity , in Juchitán de Zaragoza .
> 
> Investigation folder 3957/FIST/JUCHITAN/2023 was addressed under the Protocol for the Care of People from the Lesbian, Gay, Transsexual, Transgender, Transsexual, Transvestite, and Intersex Community, the prosecutor's office reported.
> 
> "The events occurred this Thursday in the Tomás Aquino neighborhood, located in the municipality of Juchitán de Zaragoza, in the region of the Isthmus of Tehuantepec , where the body of HLJ was located, inside a private home," he explained.
> 
> After receiving the report on the commission of the crime, the prosecution explains that it deployed its multidisciplinary team to start the inquiries, including the Specialized Unit for Attention to Crimes Committed by Sexual Diversity .
> 
> "From this initial work, the FGEO established several lines of investigation that are followed to find the whereabouts of the person who is responsible."
> 
> According to local news media, París was dedicated to selling tamales, which is why she was known in the community.


> This Thursday, Paris Alexsandry, a 37-year-old member of the muxe community of Juchitán, Oaxaca, died after being shot by a firearm in a house in this town and so far there have been no detainees.
