> Friday, June 23, 2023 00:07
> León, Guanajuato - A trans woman was shot to death next to a hotel in the Vista Hermosa neighborhood.
> 
> Authorities did not report the motive for the attack or the identity of the attackers. 
> 
> The attack occurred minutes before 11 pm on bulevar San Juan Bosco almost on the corner with Córdoba. 
> 
> Pamela, as she was known in the place, was outside the Star Hotel, which she commonly attended. 
> 
> She works there, she was engaged in prostitution, she also collected recyclable garbage," said a preventative who was at the scene. 
> 
> At that time a man arrived and approached Pamela, being behind her, he took out a firearm and fired a single shot. 
> 
> "He shot him only once in the never, he never saw his assailant arrive," the authorities reported. 
> 
> Seconds after the attack, the man fled on San Juan Bosco boulevard. 
> 
> The aggression was reported to the Single Emergency System 911, elements of the Municipal Police carried out a search operation, but were unsuccessful. 
> 
> Paramedics came and gave her first aid, but Pamela no longer had vital signs due to the injury she had. 
> 
> Authorities reported that the victim only had an entry and exit shot to the head. 
> 
> The place was guarded by agents of the State Attorney General's Office to collect data on the event and begin investigations into the attack.
