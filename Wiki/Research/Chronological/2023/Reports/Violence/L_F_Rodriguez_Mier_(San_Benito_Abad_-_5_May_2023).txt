He fought 6 days! Young transsexual died from a stab wound to the neck
> After fighting for his life for six days, Luis Fernando Rodríguez Mier, a member of the LGBTI community, died this Friday in a private clinic in Sincelejo, who had been injured in the municipality of San Benito Abad.
> 
> Rodríguez Mier was stabbed in the early hours of last Sunday in the middle of a pick-up party that was held on calle La Concepción in the aforementioned town.
> 
> The young transsexual died in the intensive care unit of the Santa María de Sincelejo clinic, where he had been transferred due to the seriousness of the injuries suffered.
> 
> According to people close to the victim, the attack with a knife would have been for revenge. Luis Fernando would have been attacked from behind when he was enjoying the celebration.
> 
> Relatives of the victim demand that the corresponding authorities drop the full weight of the law on the person responsible for the death of the 28-year-old citizen.
