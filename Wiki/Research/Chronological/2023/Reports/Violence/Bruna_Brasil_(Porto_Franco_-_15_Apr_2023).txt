> Transvestite found dead with signs of violence
> The body of the transvestite Bruna Brasil was found by residents of the Jardim Europa neighborhood, in the municipality of Porto Franco.
> Imirante.com
> 04/15/2023 at 5:04 pm - Updated on 04/15/2023 at 6:13 pm
> PORTO FRANCO - This Saturday morning (15) a transvestite, identified as Bruna Brasil, was found dead with signs of violence. The victim's body was found in the Jardim Europa neighborhood in the city of Porto Franco , in the interior of the state.
> 
> Bruna Brasil's body was found in a thicket by residents of the neighborhood, who called the Maranhão Military Police (PMMA). The transvestite, who was born in the State of Pará, had marks of injuries on her body her, caused by sharp objects.
> 
> Her body was taken to the Instituto Médico Legal (IML) in Imperatriz, inland Maranhão, where it will undergo examinations.
> 
> In a note, the Maranhão Civil Police (PC-MA) informs that as soon as it became aware of the case, a team from the Imperatriz Regional Police Station went to the location of the fact, where it carried out the first surveys.
> 
> Also according to the Civil Police, that teams from ICRIM and IML were also called for expertise and removal of the body, respectively. Initially, the case is being investigated by the Imperatriz Regional Police Station, which has already begun hearing the testimony of witnesses and the victim's family members, in order to identify the circumstances of the case.
