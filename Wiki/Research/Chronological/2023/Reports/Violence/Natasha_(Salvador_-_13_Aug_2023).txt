> Initial information indicates that after murdering the transvestite, the group fled along a path that gives access to the Cosme de Farias neighborhood.
> Published on 08/13/2023, at 5:50 pm
> 
> A transvestite, who has not yet been formally identified, was murdered on the first footbridge of Avenida Bonocô, towards Pituba, in Salvador , this Sunday afternoon (13). The young woman was tied up and was the victim of several shots.
> 
> Initial information indicates that she was taken to the location by several criminals, who after the shooting would have fled to a path that gives access to the neighborhood of Cosme de Farias. A group of about eight armed men was seen passing by.
> 
> Soldiers from the 58th Independent Military Police Company (CIPM) surrounded the site for the arrival of teams from the Department of Homicide and Personal Protection (DHPP), who are already carrying out investigative work. According to the Civil Police, the expertise and removal guides have already been issued, for the work of the Technical Police Department (DPT).



> Transvestite is victim of homicide on catwalk on Avenida Bonocô
> Sunday, 08/13/2023 - 6:20 Pm
> 
> The victim was captured by armed criminals and later murdered on the first footbridge on Avenida Bonocô, towards Iguatemi. According to the first information released by the 58th Independent Military Police Company (CIPM), traffickers took the young woman tied up to the scene and fired several shots.
> 
> According to information, Military Police vehicles are present in the area, awaiting the arrival of the Department of Homicide and Personal Protection (DHPP). The criminals managed to escape. Many passers-by were passing by the scene at the time of the shots and dispersed in fright. Residents reported that eight armed men were seen in the area leading to the Cosme de Farias neighborhood.
