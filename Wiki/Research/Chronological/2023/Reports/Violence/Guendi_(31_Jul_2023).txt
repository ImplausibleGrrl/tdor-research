> A 42-year-old trans woman was murdered inside her home in Babahoyo, Los Ríos province. The crime was denounced by the "Silhouette X" association on Wednesday, August 2.
> "Guendi, a 42-year-old trans woman, was shot by four heavily armed individuals aboard two motorcycles," a statement said.
> 
> According to the association, she was at her home when the four subjects entered "and opened fire on her" in the Malaria sector.
> 
> After that, the National Police went to the scene to carry out the respective removal of the corpse .
> 
> The letter adds that "in addition to the Guendi case, his partner would have been murdered days ago in the province of Los Ríos and his body abandoned on a highway."
> 
> The Quito Trans Psycho Center of the Silueta X Association, members of the Trans Revolution National Platform (which covers rural, community and periphery areas with its collectives) and the Ecuadorian Federation of LGBT+ Organizations called on the authorities.
> 
> They asked the State Attorney General, Diana Salazar, the Police Commander, Fausto Salinas, the Minister of the Ministry of Women and Human Rights, Paola Flores, the Undersecretary for Diversities, Alexander Guano, the Ombudsman of Ecuador ( e), Cesar Córdoba, "carry out the necessary coordination to find those guilty of this act that puts our community in mourning."
> 
> The Runa Sipiy Report of the Silueta X Association, on transfemicides, LGBT+ murders, unresolved violent deaths or suspected criminality, records this case and that of her partner as the eighteenth of this 2023, against their populations in Ecuadorian territory .



> Association "Silueta X" denounces the murder of a trans person in Ecuador
> 02 August 2023 - 14:18 1 minute
> Quito, Aug 2 (EFE).- The association for the defense of the rights of people with a gender identity other than the norms "Silueta X" denounced this Wednesday the shooting murder of a trans person in the Ecuadorian province of Los Ríos, the eighteenth violent death recorded so far this year in the LGTBI community.
> 
> "Guendi, a 42-year-old trans woman, was shot by four heavily armed subjects," the NGO said in a statement, reporting that she was in her home when the four subjects entered "and opened fire on her" in the Malaria sector.
> 
> The National Police went to the scene of the events to remove the body, the letter indicates, adding that "in addition to the Guendi case, his partner was murdered days ago in the province of Los Ríos and his body was abandoned on a road" .
> 
> "Silueta X" appealed to the State Attorney General, Diana Salazar, and to the Police Commander, Fausto Salinas, among other authorities, to make the necessary arrangements to find those guilty of the murder.
> 
> According to the statistics managed by Silueta X, it would be the eighteenth case, so far in 2023, of transfemicide, LGTBI murder and violent death that was not clarified or suspected of crime in that community. EFE
