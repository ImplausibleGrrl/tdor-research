Tina was shot dead near her home by assailants on a motorcycle.

> Tina, a 19-year-old trans woman , was shot to death on the night of Monday, June 19, 2023. This violent death occurred in the Paraíso de la Flor sector, in the north of Guayaquil.
> 
> Preliminarily it is known that subjects aboard a motorcycle intercepted the victim, while she was near her home, in that same area of the city. Another citizen who was at the scene also died [in the attack].
> 
> At the moment, the case is being investigated. Police agents arrived at the point to remove the bodies, but they did not give statements in this regard.
