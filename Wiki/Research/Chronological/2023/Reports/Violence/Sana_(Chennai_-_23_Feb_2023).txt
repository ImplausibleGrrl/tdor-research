> Transgender woman murdered in Chennai
> CHENNAI: A transgender woman was found dead with strangulation marks on her neck near Madhavaram in Chennai on Thursday night.The deceased has been identified as Saravanan alias Sana, 29, of Tsunami Quarters. Sana went missing on Wednesday, police said. Her body was found lying at the back of a petrol bunk on the 200 Feet Road. Based on information from passersby, the Madhavaram Milk Colony police recovered the body and sent it for postmortem. They registered a case and launched a hunt for the murderers.
