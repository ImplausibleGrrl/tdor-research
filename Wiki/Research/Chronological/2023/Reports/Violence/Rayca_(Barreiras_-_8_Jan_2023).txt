Late in the morning today, Sunday the 8th, around 5am, a homicide took place on Rua Machado de Assis, in the Santa Luzia neighborhood of Barreiras.

According to information, the victim identified by his first name de Luan and popularly known as Rayca, was shot with a firearm by an unidentified individual aboard a motorcycle.

The Military Police registered the case and the DPT removed the body to the IML where it will be necropsied. Motivation will be investigated by the Civil Police. // Sigi Vilares
