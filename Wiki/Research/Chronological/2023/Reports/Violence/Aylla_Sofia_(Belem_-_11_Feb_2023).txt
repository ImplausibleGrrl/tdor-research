> This Saturday (11), a man stabbed to death a transvestite, identified as Aylla Sofia, in the district of Icoaraci, in Belém.
> 
> According to police, the crime suspect would be the victim's companion, who was arrested shortly after trying to flee.
> 
> The case was registered by the Civil Police in the Sectional of Icoaraci. The author of the crime was presented by military police and arrested in flagrante delicto for the crime of homicide, according to the PC.
> 
> The case is being investigated.





> A transvestite was stabbed to death this Saturday (11), on Rua Siqueira Mendes, in the Cruzeiro neighborhood, in the Icoaraci district, in Belém.
> 
> The victim, whose social name was not informed, was better known as "Bananinha".
> 
> According to preliminary information, the crime would have occurred at the end of the morning, near the Cruzeiro warehouse. Military Police officers were triggered and when they arrived at the scene they found the victim dead.
> 
> Experts from the Pará Scientific Police were also at the scene of the homicide and confirmed the cause of death. Then, the Center for Scientific Expertise was triggered to carry out the removal of the body.
> 
> Witnesses said the victim would have received the stab wound after a misunderstanding with a companion. The information, however, has not yet been confirmed.
> 
> At the time of publication of this article, however, the name of the suspect had not been disclosed and neither had the reason for the disagreement.


> A transvestite woman was murdered with a knife late Saturday morning (11), on Rua Siqueira Mendes , between Travessa Pimenta Bueno and Avenida Doutor Lopo de Castro, in the Cruzeiro neighborhood , in the Icoaraci district , in Belém . Preliminary information says the two would have gotten into an argument after the woman threw away a pot of beans, then the man stabbed her.The Scientific Police of Pará (PCPA) confirmed the call at around 1:30 pm. The author of the crime, whose name has not been released, was presented by military police and arrested in flagrante delicto for the crime of homicide.In a note sent to Oliberal.com, the Military Police reported that the victim was found near the warehouse and had injuries, possibly from a knife. The military searched the area to locate the suspect in the crime, who was captured on Cruzeiro beach. The man was arrested and taken to the 8th Urban Sectional of Icoaraci.
>
> The crime area was isolated by the Military Police of Pará (PMPA) for the investigation work. The Civil Police of Pará (PCPA) also attended the accident site and recorded the incident report. The victim's body was sent for necropsy at the Legal Medical Institute (IML), of the Scientific Police of the State of Pará (PCEPA), and will later be released to the family members. The investigation to determine all the circumstances of the accident is ongoing at the Civil Police.Also in a note, the Civil Police reported that “the case was registered at the Icoaraci Sectional. The author of the crime was presented by military police and arrested in flagrante delicto for the crime of homicide”.
