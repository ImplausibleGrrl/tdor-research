> PUBLIC COMMUNITY: Another trans woman and her partner are murdered in La Troncal, Cañar, Ecuador
> 
> The Trans Psycho Center of Quito of the Silueta X Association, members of the National Trans Revolution Platform (which covers rural, community and periphery areas with its collectives) and the Ecuadorian Federation of LGBT+ Organizations, make the following fact publicly known.
> 
> Bella (Sipiy Rune Coding CPTSX00231978), a 35-year-old trans woman, who lived in the United States and had been in Ecuador on vacation for the past 2 months, was shot to death along with her new partner. The murder of both occurred when they returned from bathing in a river in the Troncal, at the entrance to the coliseum in the Pancho Negro sector, a nearby place where they lived.
