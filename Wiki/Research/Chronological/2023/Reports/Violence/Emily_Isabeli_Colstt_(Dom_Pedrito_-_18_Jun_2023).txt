> Transsexual found shot dead in Dom Pedrito
> 
> This Monday morning (19), the Military Brigade was called on Rua Hermes Ernesto da Fonseca, a location that leads to the Passo Real beach resort, where people found a body. Skills. SAMU was at the scene and confirmed the death.
> 
> The victim is a transsexual identified as Emily Isabeli Colstt, 28 years old. She was killed by multiple gunshots. There are no suspects as to the authorship of the crime and the Civil Police have started investigations.
> 
> Witnesses told police they heard gunshots on Sunday night (18), hours before the body was found.
