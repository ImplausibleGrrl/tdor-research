Mimi was shot multiple times whie in her car in Santa Cruz do Rio Pardo, São Paulo (Brazil) on 18 Apr 2023.

She was 45.



> A 45-year-old transsexual known as “Mimi”, with the registered name of José Antônio Pereira da Silva Filho, was shot dead on Tuesday night (18), in Santa Cruz do Rio Pardo (120 kilometers from Marília). .
> 
> The case took place on Avenida Rosa Pereira Nantes in Jardim São João, around 10:50 pm. The victim was found inside a vehicle with license plates from Ourinhos (95 kilometers away from Marília).
> 
> Mimi was shot five times in the back. Samu's team was triggered and confirmed the death at the scene. The case is still under investigation by the Civil Police.


> A 45-year-old transvestite was shot dead on Tuesday night (18) in the Vila Gonzaga neighborhood, in Santa Cruz do Rio Pardo (SP). The transvestite was found dead inside her vehicle, located with the door open.
> 
> According to the Military Police, who were called around 11 pm to respond to the incident registered as a homicide, the victim was shot at least five times with a firearm.
> 
> The Civil Police investigates the case, but until the beginning of the morning of this Wednesday (19) no one had been arrested. The victim's identity has not been confirmed.
