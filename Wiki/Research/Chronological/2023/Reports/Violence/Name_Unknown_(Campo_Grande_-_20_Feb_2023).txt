> Transvestite is shot dead at the Central Fair of Campina Grande/PB
> February 20, 2023
> 
> A transvestite was shot dead in the early hours of the morning of this Monday (20), at the Central Fair of Campina Grande, Agreste da Paraíba.
> 
> The Central Fair is not working due to Carnival, but according to the Civil Police, merchants who were at the scene heard gunshots. Shortly afterwards, a body was found.
> 
> Civil Police have been triggered and are at the scene carrying out an expertise. According to the first findings, the victim is a man who was killed with several shots, including a fatal shot that would have hit the back of the head.
> 
> Circuit footage from cameras at the fair establishments must be analyzed by the police to identify the author of the crime. The case remains under investigation.
