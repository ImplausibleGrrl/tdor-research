> Gabriel Henrique Lucas Aguiar, 22, confessed to the death of a trans woman in Colombo, in the Metropolitan Region of Curitiba. He gave a statement to the police shortly after being arrested, this Monday (22), and explained his motivation.
> 
> The young man was the victim's companion, identified as Raynanda Minieiro , 34 years old. In the statement, Gabriel stated that he had an 'unhealthy jealousy' for his girlfriend.
> 
> “I was insanely jealous of her, that really complicated jealousy. If I saw a guy close to her, I was already jealous, I already fought with her”.
> 
> Raynanda was stabbed to death and was found by neighbors inside the house. Gabriel confirmed that he hit the victim after an argument, with blows to the chest and head. He further claimed that he ended up accidentally hitting his own leg and then left the scene.
> 
> “I was afraid of doing worse things. I just didn't know it was going to lead to this situation."
> 
> Gabriel Henrique Lucas Aguiar's mother made an outburst on social networks about the crime committed by her son.
> 
> In the report, the young man's mother stated that she loved her daughter-in-law, that she spent Christmas and New Years with the family, and that her son is "a shame for the family".
> 
> “Shame on my family Gabriel, why did you do that [...], now you've become a murderer and now you have to pay [...]. "I'm sorry for the word of a mother, but I'm angry because I didn't raise a murderous son".



> The Military Police arrested this Monday (22) a young man suspected of killing his girlfriend, a trans woman, in Colombo , in the Metropolitan Region of Curitiba (RMC). According to the police, he was detained at the Cajuru Hospital, which is located in the capital, because he sought medical attention after committing the crime.
> 
> The g1 tries to contact his defense. According to the PM, the suspect went to the health unit because he would have injured his leg while striking his girlfriend.
> 
> According to the PM, neighbors of the victim heard cries for help and, when the police arrived at the house, they found the woman dead and with several injuries to her body caused by a knife.
> 
> Police said they received information on the young man's suspected involvement in the crime and located him in the hospital, where he allegedly confessed to the murder after an argument motivated by jealousy.
> 
> According to the Civil Police, the suspect was taken to the police station and charged with femicide.




> Raynanda Mineiro, 34 years old, was found dead inside the house, on the morning of this Monday (22), in a residence on Rua Rafael de Queiroz, in the Jardim Guarani neighborhood, in Colombo, in the Metropolitan Region of Curitiba (RMC). The victim would have been murdered with several stab wounds.
> 
> At home, she allegedly argued with her partner. Neighbors would have heard a lot of screaming during the night and early in the morning they heard the trans young woman's distress calls. Upon going to the scene, witnesses came across the victim's body lying on the ground. The main suspect is her boyfriend.
> 
> Until the closing of this text, at 9:30 am, this Monday, the 22nd, no suspect had been arrested.

> Young trans woman is found dead at home; partner is the main suspect in the crime
> May 22, 2023 at 7:52 am
> 
> The body of a trans woman was found inside the house on the morning of this Monday (22). The case took place in the Guarani neighborhood, in Colombo , in the Metropolitan Region of Curitiba.
> 
> In the house lived a girl, her two children and a couple. The woman, who works in a bar, would have come to the house to leave the young trans woman after she had completed her work shift at the establishment. After leaving her colleague, the woman returned to the bar. The couple's children were not at home at the time of the crime.
> 
> At home, the young trans woman allegedly accused with her husband. Neighbors would have heard a lot of screaming during the early morning. Early in the morning they heard cries for help from the young trans woman. Upon going to the scene, witnesses came across the victim's body lying on the ground. The young woman would have been killed with knife strikes by her companion.
> 
> The young woman's body was collected and sent to the Legal Medical Institute. The killer has not yet been found. The police are investigating the case.




> Neighbors hear 'help' screams and find trans woman stabbed to death inside her home in Colombo
> By Francielly Azevedo on May 22, 2023 at 7:36 am .
> 
> A 34-year-old trans woman was found dead inside her house, on the morning of this Monday (22), in the Jardim Guarani neighborhood, in Colombo, in the Metropolitan Region of Curitiba (RMC). Neighbors reported that they heard screams for 'help' before finding the victim lying on the ground, with multiple injuries and a lot of blood.
> 
> The crime took place at a residence on Rua Rafael de Queiroz. The woman would have been killed with several stab wounds.
> 
> A friend, who has lived with the victim for four years and was not at home, reported that the neighbors called her about the noise.
>  
> “The neighbors heard the screams and told me. I called my sister so she could see what was going on. My sister came and found her dead. I arrived later and didn't want to go in. My sister said there was a lot of blood,” she told Banda B.
> 
> The friend said that the woman had been in a relationship for two years, but she cannot say whether it was her boyfriend who committed the crime.
> 
> “I have no idea. She never complained to people about violence. It is very sad,” he lamented.
> 
> Until the publication of this report, no suspect had been detained.
> 
> Banda B already knows the identity of the victim, but as the crime is recent, and out of respect for the family, it will not publish it at this time.
