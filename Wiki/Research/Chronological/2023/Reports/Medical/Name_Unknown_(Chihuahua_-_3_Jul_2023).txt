> Senior transgender woman found dead
> Monday, July 3, 2023 - 5:24:59 pm
>

> 
> Apparently the woman would have died days ago, because after attending the report by municipal police officers, they confirmed the death and reported the decomposing body. 
> 
> Neighbors in the area mentioned that the person's first name responded to Óscar Sánchez, who years ago identified himself as Angélica, alias La Chata.  
> 
>  The causes of death are unknown, however the settlers who knew her commented that she had been suffering from an illness for some time.
> 
> At the scene, the presence of agents of the Specialized Prosecutor for Women was requested to remove the body.
