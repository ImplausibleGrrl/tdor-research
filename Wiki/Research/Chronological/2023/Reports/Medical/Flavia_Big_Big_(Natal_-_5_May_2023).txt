> Flávia Big Big, known on social media for her memes, dies
> The trans influencer had been hospitalized since February to treat cancer
> 
> Flavia Big Big
> Flávia Big Big was hospitalized to treat cancer (Photo: Reproduction/Instagram)
> The 26-year-old trans influencer and comedian Flávia Big Big, known on social media for her memes, died this Monday (5/8). She had been hospitalized since February 1st to treat cancer. Several netizens were shocked by the news and mourned her death.
> 
> On social networks, Flávia shared a photo lying in the hospital bed telling about her diagnosis.
> “On February 1st, my body started to feel very tired and had a lot of fever and I went to the hospital. I was diagnosed with cancer, and I went to another hospital and found out that I had nothing to do with these symptoms and I'm telling you that I'm hospitalized with anemia and lung problems. Jaja I'm better to make people happy, I love you. God will bless us all,” he wrote, on April 8.
> 
> In the same month, Flávia's situation worsened and she had to be intubated. The family even asked on social media that fans of the influencer say prayers for her.
> The news of her death was shared by her sister, Fabiola Gomes, on social media. “Our Big's suffering is over, she is now with her daddy from heaven. Thanks to everyone who was praying for our sister,” she wrote.
>
