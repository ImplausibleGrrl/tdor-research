> Abby Moreira, the first trans woman to be a municipal guard, dies at age 46 in Pernambuco
> Written by Publisher , 21:31 / June 24, 2023.
> She had a sudden illness in her sleep; Abby was an activist for LGBTQIA+ rights
> 
> Abby Silva Moreira died at the age of 46 due to a sudden illness this Saturday (24), according to the city hall of Jaboat達o dos Guararapes. In 2017, she became the first trans woman to assume the position of municipal guard in Brazil. She died while sleeping at her home in the Candeias neighborhood. The information is from g1 .
> 
> The death of the activist for the rights of the LGBTQIA+ population will be investigated by the Death Verification Service (SVO). During the night, she got up to drink water, went back to sleep, but did not wake up. 
> 
> The City Hall was informed about the death at 8:30 am today. Abby was a member of the Municipal Guard and was assigned to the Human Rights Department of Jaboat達o dos Guararapes. 
> 
> The agent left a son and his wife, with whom he had been married for 28 years. 
> 
> Being the first and only trans woman in the position of municipal guard in her municipality, Abby reported to g1 , in 2022, that she suffered several cases of transphobia. 
> 
> She was even removed from street activities in February last year and had salary bonuses cut , despite being a public servant.
> 
> In 2021, he faced a strong depression due to transphobia. She also required two psychiatric hospitalizations .
> 
> At the time, the then commander of the Jaboat達o Municipal Guard, Admilson Silva de Freitas, was exonerated.
