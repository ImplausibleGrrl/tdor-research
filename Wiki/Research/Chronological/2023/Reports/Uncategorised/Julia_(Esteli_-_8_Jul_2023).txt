> Transsexual was found dead on the sidewalk of a disco in Estelí
> Saturday, July 8, 2023 11:22 AM
> The transsexual Julio César Almanza Jarquín, about 23 years old, popularly known as "La Julia", was found dead on the sidewalk of a nightclub located in front of Casa Pellas in the city of Estelí, this Saturday morning.
> 
> The lifeless body of "La Julia" was found by workers from the amusement center who immediately reported the case to the National Police.
> 
> Shortly after, the departmental head of the Police, Senior Commissioner Alejandro Ruiz Martínez, appeared at the scene, accompanied by a technical and criminalistics team to investigate the causes and circumstances of the death of Julio César Almanza.
> 
> Julio César Almanza, better known as "La Julia", lived in the El Paraíso neighborhood, and presumably at night he went out in search of adventure and passion in the lively area of ??Diamante de Las Segovias.
