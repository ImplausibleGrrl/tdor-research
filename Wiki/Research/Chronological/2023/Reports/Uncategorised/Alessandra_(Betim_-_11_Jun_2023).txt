====== Alessandra (Betim - 11 Jun 2023) ======
Created Monday 12 June 2023
@uncategorised



Alessandra
22
11 Jun 2023
Bairro Alterosa
Betim
Minas Gerais
not reported



> The body of a 22-year-old transvestite was found on the sidelines of the railway line in Bairro Alterosa, in Betim, in the Metropolitan Region of Belo Horizonte, this Sunday morning (6/11). The suspect is that the victim was hit by the train. However, the cause of death has not yet been confirmed, given the condition in which the body was found. 
> 
> The victim was recognized by her partner who learned from people that a body had been found at the scene and, already concerned about the disappearance of her partner since last night, sought the police. Alessandra was recognized by a tattoo she had on one of her arms. 
> 
> According to the incident report, people passing by saw the body and called the Mobile Emergency Service (Samu). The call was for a person who had fallen on the railway line, but the death was confirmed by the medical team. 
> 
> Civil Police expertise was triggered. The victim's limbs were torn apart and scattered across the railway line, so it was not possible to check promptly whether there were any signs of violence. Next to the body was a cell phone and a credit card, but there is still no confirmation that they belong to Alessandra. 
> 
> The body was sent to the Legal Medical Institute of Betim.
