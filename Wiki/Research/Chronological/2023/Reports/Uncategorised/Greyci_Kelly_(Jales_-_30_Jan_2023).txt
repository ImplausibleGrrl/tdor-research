> A  transsexual woman living in Dolcinópolis was found dead in Jales on the morning of Monday, January 30. Greyci Kelly, 43 years old, had been missing since the previous Thursday, when she would have left that city to come to Jales. Her body was in a state of decomposition in a vacant lot in Jardim Romero, and was found by employees of the Secretary of Agriculture and Environment, who were called to investigate the bad smell in the place. The suspicion was that there was some dead domestic animal, but when they arrived at the land, the servers found the body. There were already people there. They took videos and photos of the corpse and shared it on social media. 
> 
> The Civil Police reported that the Cause of Death will be investigated by the expert, but there is a suspicion that she has suffered a sudden illness due to drug use. Apparently, there were no signs of violence. She wore a dress and sunglasses. There is information that she was a chemical dependent and her body was close to a place known as a drug sales point. 
> 
> Greyci Kelly was well liked and had many friends who mourned her death through social media. 



> Missing trans woman found dead in vacant lot
> Woman who was missing was found dead on land in Jales (SP) – Photo: TVC Interior/Disclosure
> 
> A 43-year-old transsexual woman was found dead in a vacant lot of Vila União, in Jales (SP), on Monday (30). The body was located the day after Trans Day of Visibility, celebrated on January 29. The information is from TV Tem/g1 .
> 
> According to the Civil Police, Greyci Kelly, born in Dolcinópolis (SP), was missing from this Thursday (26). Military police, civilians and experts were called and attended the scene after locating the body.
> 
> It is not known whether the body showed signs of violence. No suspects have been identified and the case will be investigated. After the victim's identity was confirmed, friends mourned Greyci Kelly's death on social media.
> 
> “Today we say goodbye to this person named Greyci Kelly. An amazing person I had the privilege of meeting a few years ago. I leave here my words of affection for you, Greyci. For the incredible person you were, regardless of anything and any situation,” wrote a friend.
