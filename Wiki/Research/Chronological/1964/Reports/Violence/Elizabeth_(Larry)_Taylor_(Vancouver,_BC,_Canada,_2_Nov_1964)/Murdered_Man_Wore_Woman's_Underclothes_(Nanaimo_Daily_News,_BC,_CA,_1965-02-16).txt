===== MURDERED MAN WORE  WOMAN'S UNDERCLOTHES =====

VANCOUVER (CP)—A man known as "Elizabeth Taylor" was wearing feminine undergarments, nylon stockings, a gold blouse and opera gloves when police found his body, a murder trial was told here Monday.

Duncan Beaudry, charged with the non-capital murder of Larry Taylor, known to police as a female impersonator, is on trial in assize court before a jury of 10 men and two women.

Sgt. Gordon McQueen of Vancouver police said Beaudry pointed out Taylor's body to him under a verandah Nov. 2, after telling the sergeant "there's something fishy going on around here."

The sergeant said three coins totalling 18 cents lay near th body.

Vancouver city pathologist Dr. T. R. Harmon testified he believed Taylor was strangled by someone who came up behind him. He said the man's body showed a number of bruises.
