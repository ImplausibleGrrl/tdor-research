===== STRANGULATION =====

==== Four Held After Man Found Dead ====

A 40-year-old man was found strangled in an East End lane early today.

Three men and a woman found in a nearby apartment were arrested following discovery of the body and are being held for questioning.

Homicide detectives have identified the victim as Larry Taylor, 40, of no fixed address, His body was found shortly before 6 a.m. in a lane near an outside stairway at the rear of an apartment building at 874 East Georgia.

An autopsy died from strangulation.

Police said Taylor had suffered a cut on the forehead and some bruising. He had been bleeding from the mouth.

The victim was dressed in woman's clothing, complete with long red opera gloves, nylon stockings, and a dress. He also wore make-up, police said.
