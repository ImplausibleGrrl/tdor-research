James Smith, about 40 years old, and supposed to be a terra-cotta worker, was found dead in an Illinois Central freight car at Paxton, Nov. 30, with his throat cut from ear to ear. The car had been on a side-track there for several days. There is no clew to the murderes. Smith lived in Litchfield.
