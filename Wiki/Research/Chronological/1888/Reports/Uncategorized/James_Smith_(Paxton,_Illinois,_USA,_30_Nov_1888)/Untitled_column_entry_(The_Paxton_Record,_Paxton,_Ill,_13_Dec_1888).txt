Paul Hull, a newspaper correspondent from Chicago, was in the city Tuesday looking up evidence in the James Smith case. He thought from the information he obtained that the case looked more like murder than suicide. Mr. Hull is a newspaper man of considerable note. During the last session of the legistlature, he was the Springfield correspondent of the Chicago News.
