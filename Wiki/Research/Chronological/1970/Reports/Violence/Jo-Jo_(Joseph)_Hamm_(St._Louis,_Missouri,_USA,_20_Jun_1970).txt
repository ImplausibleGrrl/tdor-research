At 7:30 am on the 20th of June, 1970, the body of Jo-Jo Hamm, 30,  was discovered slumped in a car by a passer-by on the 1800 block of Piggot Avenue in East St. Lous.

Jo-Jo, who was wearing women's clothing, had been shot three times. The police determined that they had been dead for several hours.
