Found dead this morning (9/29), in the Buriti Lagoa stream, Bairro São Conrado, in Campo Grande, the 28-year-old actor and dancer Stelio Constantino.

He had a degree in Performing Arts from UFGD (Federal University of Grande Dourados).

According to Campo Grande, the body was found in the region of the stream, hanging from a tree, by residents who were hiking along the site.

The police are investigating the case and the main suspicion is that the victim committed suicide by hanging. The body was sent to Imol.

Currently the actor lived in the capital, where he started to have a trans identity, using the social name of Sucia Labasura Constantino Barbosa.


The actress and dancer Sucia Labasura Constantino Barbosa, 28, a former resident of Dourados and graduated in Performing Arts at UFGD, was found dead on Tuesday morning (29), in Campo Grande.

She currently lived in the capital, where she started to have a trans identity and used the social name of Sucia Labasura Constantino Barbosa.

The body was found in the Buriti Lagoa stream, in the São Conrado neighborhood. People on the trail saw the body hanging from a tree and called the authorities.

The suspicion is that the victim has committed suicide.


The body found on Rua Praia Grande, in Jardim São Conrado, in Campo Grande (MS), was identified as 38-year-old Stélio Constantino Barboza. The case was registered as suicide. That was her registration name, not her social name. The report did not get the social name used by the trans woman.
