====== Name Unknown (Santo Antonio de Jesus - 4 Aug 2020) ======

not reported

The body of a trans person was found in a house in Santo Antonio de Jesus, Bahia (Brazil) on the night of 4 Aug 2020.

Their identity and cause of death are unknown.



A transvestite was found dead in a residence on Tuesday night (04), on Avenida Heitor Lírio Melo, Irmã Dulce neighborhood, in Santo Antonio de Jesus. According to an image circulating on social networks, the body of the transvestite, whose identity has not yet been released, is lying on the floor of a house, along with blankets.

In the image it is possible to notice that next to the victim's body there are containers used for food and a bottle of content not yet identified. The Body will be collected for the Technical Police Department to perform an autopsy and the authorship and motivation of the crime will be investigated by the Civil Police of Santo Antonio de Jesus.


//04 - Without identification, Santo Antônio de Jesus, Bahia (Brazil), 04/08/2020//

//On the night of Tuesday, the 4th, a dead person was found inside a building that functioned as a school between Travessa da Urbis 1 and Av. Heitor Lírio de Melo in Santo Antônio de Jesus. According to information that she is a transsexual and that her body would probably have been in place since the previous day and was only found today. Police were at the scene and cordoned off the area until the DPT team arrived for cadaveric surveys and removal of the body for IML.//
