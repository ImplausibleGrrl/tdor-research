: Transvestite found dead in bush
July 31, 2020 at 22h 34min, by Visão NotíciasPhoto by Disclosure

Transvestite Raimundo Nonato Rodrigues dos Reis, 46, was found dead in the scrub on Estrada Ruti, in the Industrial Sector near the Tapajos farms. According to a source, a person who passed by passed by and found the victim lying dead and lifeless.

According to a Visão Notícias source, Raiane (stage name) was admitted to the Emergency Department and left the unit last Saturday (25) for some unknown reason and was no longer seen.

Friends mobilized on social networks and the group Falando de Sinop, when on Friday night (31) their location was reported, but without life.

The Military Police were called and the forensics were called in to carry out the procedures and forward the body to the Institute of Legal Medicine (IML). At first it would be natural death.

The case remains under investigation.
