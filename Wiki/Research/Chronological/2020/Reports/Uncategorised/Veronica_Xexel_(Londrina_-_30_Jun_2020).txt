The body of a transvestite was found inside a house in Jardim do Sol, west of Londrina on Tuesday (30). She was identified only as Veronica and had been missing since Sunday (28). The house is in an alley on rua Netuno and according to neighbors, the stench caught the attention. They also found the door of the house strange, and when they went to the scene they saw the body and called the Military Police. 

Criminalistics went to the scene, along with Civil Police from the Homicide Police. It is not yet known what happened at the scene and it was a murder. The causes of death have not yet been defined.
