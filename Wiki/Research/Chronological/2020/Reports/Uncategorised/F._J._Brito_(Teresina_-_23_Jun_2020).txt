====== F. J. Brito (Teresina - 23 Jun 2020) ======
Created Wednesday 24 June 2020
@uncategorised


F. J. Brito
[Fernando José Brito]
45
23 Jun 2020
Teresina
Brazil
possible suicide

Uma travesti, de identidade ainda não revelada, foi encontrada morta na tarde dessa terça-feira (23) no Conjunto Taquari, localizado na zona leste de Teresina. De acordo com a coronel Joseline, comandante do 5 Batalhão da Polícia Militar, a vítima foi encontrada sentada e morta, e foi encontrado alguns compridos no lado do corpo. A principal suspeita é que a vítima tirou a própria vida. O Instituto de Medicina Legal (IML) foi acionado para remover o corpo.

A transvestite, whose identity has not yet been revealed, was found dead on Tuesday afternoon (23) at Conjunto Taquari, located on the east side of Teresina. According to Colonel Joseline, commander of the 5th Battalion of the Military Police, the victim was found sitting and dead, and some [medication] was found on the side of the body. The main suspicion is that the victim took their own life.


A transvestite was found dead inside her home on Tuesday (23) at the Taquari complex, in the Vale Quem Tem neighborhood, east of Teresina. The 45-year-old victim, identified as Fernando José Brito, was found by family members. According to the police, the body has no evidence of violence.

Family members were surprised because she spent about 48 hours without keeping in touch by phone and went to check in at her house when they found her dead on the couch in the living room and with medication next to her body.

The PM isolated the place until the arrival of the expertise of the Civil Police. The body was collected by the Institute of Forensic Medicine (IML). The report will identify the cause of death that will be investigated.
