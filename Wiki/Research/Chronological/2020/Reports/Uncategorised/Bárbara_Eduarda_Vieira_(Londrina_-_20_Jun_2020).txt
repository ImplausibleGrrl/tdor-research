====== Bárbara Eduarda Vieira (Londrina - 20 Jun 2020) ======

Bárbara Vieira, 40, died after being run over on Saturday night (20), from Avenida Brasília, an urban stretch of highway BR-369, in Londrina. The victim was hit while crossing the road and the driver of the vehicle, whose name was not disclosed, had no injuries.

Londrina invested almost R $ 9 million in the expansion of beds 
Rescuers from the Integrated Emergency Trauma Care Service (Siate) and a team from the Mobile Emergency Care Service (Samu) were called, however, the victim did not resist. The body was sent to the Legal Medical Institute (IML) of Londrina.

After being run over, another accident was recorded at the scene. A motorcyclist dodged the accident and hit the highway. He had no injuries and was subsequently released.

Transvestite dies in a hit and run in Londrina


A 40-year-old transvestite died in a hit-and-run on BR-369 on Saturday night (20) in Londrina .

According to the press report of the Fire Brigade, the accident would have happened around 8:00 pm, near number 151 of Avenida Brasília, north of the city.



She was allegedly hit by a silver Hyundai / HB20 and died inside the ambulance of Samu (Mobile Emergency Service), while receiving care.

Her body was sent to the IML (Medical-Legal Institute) of Londrina , which identified her as Bárbara Eduarda Vieira.
