An unidentified #trans woman was found dead in a hotel room in Boituva, São Paulo (Brazil) on 19 Dec 2020. #SayHerName #TransLivesMatter #TDoR


The victim was found dead in a hotel room after a young man with her reported she was feeling ill.

An autopsy is awaited.

> A [trans woman] died inside a motel located on Vicente Palma Highway (SP-129), early on Saturday (19), in Boituva (SP). The case will be registered at the city police station as a suspected death.
> 
> According to the Civil Police, a boy was with the transvestite in the motel room. He called the police when he realized she was feeling sick.
> 
> Military police arrived at the scene, but have already found the transvestite lifeless. According to police, this man confessed to having used drugs with the victim.
> 
> According to the police, the transvestite had no apparent signs of aggression. The body was sent to the Instituto Médico Legal (IML) of Itapetininga.
