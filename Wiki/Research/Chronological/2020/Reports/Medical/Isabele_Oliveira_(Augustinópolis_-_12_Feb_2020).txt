Transsexual nursing technician dies inside hospital in upstate
Isabele Oliveira, as she was known, was 36 years old. She would be asleep during the rest period and didn't wake up anymore; case was at the Augustinópolis Regional Hospital.

Nursing technician Isabele Oliveira died inside the hospital - Photo: Personal Archive

A transsexual nursing technician was found dead inside the Regional Hospital of Augustinópolis, in northern Tocantins, this Wednesday morning (12). Isabele Oliveira, as she was known, was 36 years old. Coworkers told the Military Police that she was asleep at rest and did not wake up.

Death was noticed early in the morning, when colleagues tried to wake up the nursing technician and found that he had no vital signs. The Military Police was called around 7 am and the case was registered as a death to be clarified.

The delegate of the Civil Police was called and the expertise was also at the place. The body was taken to the IML of Tocantinópolis.

The State Department of Health (SES) said it deeply regrets the death of the servant and reported that the cause of death is under investigation by the competent authorities.

The Association of Transvestites and Transsexuals of the State of Tocantins (ATRATO) issued a note of condolence and stated that Isabelle Oliveira was a prominent militant in the fight against LGBT discrimination. "He led several fights and flags, with important achievements, mainly in the job market."
