====== Patrícia (Porto Velho - 18 Mar 2020) ======
Created Wednesday 17 June 2020


Patrícia
58
18 Mar 2020
Porto Velho
Brazil
cardiac arrest

According to the information, the transvestite and hairdresser identified Antônio Valdernison Castro Calixto, 58, better known as Patrícia, was found by a relative of the victim found her lifeless on a bed in a residence at Rua Vasco da Gama, neighborhood Três Marias on the east side of the capital, and called the Military Police and Samu, who after verifying the situation, confirmed the death.

The hairdresser died of a heart attack, and had been having a stroke for about a year. Patrícia lived in Italy for a few years and was well known in Porto Velho.

The funeral will take place from 11 pm on Wednesday (18) at the São Cristóvão funeral home, and the funeral will take place tomorrow (19), at the Santo Antônio cemetery.
