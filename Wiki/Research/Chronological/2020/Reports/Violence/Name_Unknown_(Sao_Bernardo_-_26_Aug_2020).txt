The burnt body of an unidentified trans woman was found in a motel room in São Bernardo do Campo, São Paulo (Brazil) on 26 Aug 2020.

Her killer was arrested.



The body of a [woman], whose identity has not been revealed, was found charred on the bed of a motel room, at Estrada Martim Afonso de Souza 380, Botujuro, São Bernardo.

The Fire Department was called in to respond to the occurrence of a fire and when the team arrived at the scene, they found the room on fire and the body. The PM (Military Police) was called and the expertise requested.

Preliminary information shows that a married man and a transvestite were in the suite and had a fight. The man reportedly killed the transvestite and set his body on fire. He would have tried to escape, but the police managed to arrest him.
