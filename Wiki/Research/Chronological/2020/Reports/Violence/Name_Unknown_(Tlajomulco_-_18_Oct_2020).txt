The body of an unidentified #trans woman was found in undergrowth alongside a road in Tlajomulco de Zúñiga, Jalisco (Mexico) on 18 Oct 2020.

She appeared to have been between 20 and 25 years of age, and had been stabbed in the neck. #SayHerName #TransLivesMatter #TDoR



The victim's body was found amongst undergrowth alongside a road. She had a neck injury and appeared to have been between 20 and 25 years of age.

> The body of a person was found on Adolfo B. Horn, almost at the intersection with Avenida Los Cántaros, in Tlajomulco de Zúñiga.
> 
> A municipal policeman reported that the victim was face up, and had an injury on the right side of the neck, apparently caused by a sharp object.
> 
> He said the body had been there for several hours.
> 
> The person appeared between 20 and 25 years old. She was dark-skinned, slim, tall, long hair streaked with gold, and wore blue jeans with a black blouse. She also had a pink and blue backpack.
