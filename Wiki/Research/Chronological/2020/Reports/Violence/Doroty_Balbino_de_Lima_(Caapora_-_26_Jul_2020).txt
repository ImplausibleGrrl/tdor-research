Doroty de Lima was shot in Caaporã, Paraíba (Brazil) on 26 Jul 2020.

She was 40.
 
https://www.portalt5.com.br/noticias/policia/2020/7/352714-travesti-e-assassinada-a-tiros-no-litoral-sul-da-paraiba



Doroty was shot multiple times in the head in a closed bar (named "Bar do Charel") by three masked men in what appears to have been a targetted assassination. Police reported finding narcotics on or near her body.

> A 40-year-old [trans woman] was murdered, early on Sunday evening (26), in the city of Caaporã, on the south coast of Paraíba. According to the Civil Police, the victim was known as “Doroty” and the crime took place around 6 pm.
> 
> The Civil Police informed PortalT5 that she was in a closed bar, with two other people. when three masked men arrived and fired several shots at the victim.
> 
> She died at the scene from her injuries. The victim was identified as [deadname] Balbino de Lima. Police also reported that they found narcotics with the victim and said she was involved in drugs.
