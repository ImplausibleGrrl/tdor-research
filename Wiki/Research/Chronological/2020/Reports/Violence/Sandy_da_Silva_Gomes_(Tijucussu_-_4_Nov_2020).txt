Transvestite is shot to death while selling raffle tickets in rural Cascavel
Nov 05, 2020 at 06:30

A 29-year-old transvestite, identified as Sancle da Silva Gomes and known as Sandy, 29, was shot dead on Wednesday night (4) in the town of Tijucussu, in the rural area of ​​Cascavel, in Greater Fortaleza. She sold raffles at the time of the crime. It is the second transvestite victim of violence in Ceará in four days.

The victim had no criminal record. According to the Military Police, she worked with the sale of raffles. Several pistol capsules were found next to the victim's body.

The Military Police searches to identify and arrest the perpetrators of the crime. The Cascavel Civil Police Station is investigating the case. The report requested information from the Public Security and Social Defense Secretariat (SSPDS) on the case and is awaiting a return.

Transvestite dead in Fortaleza

At dawn last Sunday (1st), Samylla Marry Widson was shot at Avenida Beira Mar, in Praia de Iracema, in Fortaleza. She was seen at a health unit, but died the day after the crime. Two suspects, who had been captured at Fortaleza Airport, were released after giving evidence.
