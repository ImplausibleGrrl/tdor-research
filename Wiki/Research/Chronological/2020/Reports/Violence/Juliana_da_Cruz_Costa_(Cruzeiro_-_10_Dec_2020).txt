Transvestite is murdered in Sudoeste after refusing to give food to the homeless
posted on 11/12/2020 00:06 / updated on 11/12/2020 00:32

The transvestite died stabbed, on Thursday night (10/12), in commercial 101 do Sudoeste, in the parking lot in front of Choparia Sudoeste. The crime is investigated as a homicide by the 3rd Police Station (Cruzeiro). The victim was identified as Juliana da Cruz Costa, 33.

Juliana da Cruz Costa, 33, is murdered in Sudoeste (photo: Personal archive)

According to preliminary information from the Military Police of the Federal District (PMDF), the victim was eating in the middle of the street, when a man, whose identity has not yet been revealed, passed by and asked for some food. Witnesses reported that the transvestite refused to offer the food and, shortly after, the perpetrator struck the victim's left collarbone with a knife.

Until the last update of this report, no one had been arrested. The expertise of the Civil Police of the Federal District has been activated and police are on the streets in search of the suspect's whereabouts.
