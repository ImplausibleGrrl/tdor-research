The victim's body was found on the street with stab wounds. A man who spent the night before with her is being sought by police.

> A 22-year-old trans woman was found dead on rua Washington Luís, in República in downtown São Paulo, on Sunday morning (20). According to a friend of the victim, the suspect was a man who spent the night with her.
> 
> City guards received a report and came to the scene, where they found the victim lying with a knife wound. Samu teams were called, but the woman could not resist her injury and died on the spot.
> 
> Another friend said she went out with the suspect in a car, hours before he returned and picked up the victim. Both were doing programs in the region. According to her friend the man had used a large amount of drugs during the night.
> 
> Objects found near the body were collected and will undergo expert examinations. The case was registered in the 2nd (Bom Retiro), which continues with the investigations.
