Adrianinha Paris was shot by two men who arrived at her home in Recife, Pernambuco (Brazil) on 7 Oct 2020.

Before killing her they make her take her 4-year old niece home.

Adrianinha was 42 and was a hairdresser. #SayHerName #TransLivesMatter #TDoR




A hairdresser was murdered, this Wednesday (7), inside the beauty salon of her property, in the Dois Unidos neighborhood, in the North Zone of Recife. According to the Civil Police, Adrianinha Paris, 42, who was a trans woman, was shot several times. Shortly before the crime, she was with her 4-year-old niece.

The crime occurred on the morning of Wednesday, at Rua Dorândia, in Alto do Maracanã, where Adrianinha had maintained the salon for over ten years, according to family members. Police said two men arrived at the scene when the hairdresser was in front of the establishment with the child.

Also according to the corporation, these men sent Adrianinha to take the child to the family home, in the same area. After the child entered the property, Adriana went to the parlor with the criminals.

Inside the hall, she was killed with several revolver shots. The men ran away. When the hairdresser was hit, the child was already with relatives and suffered nothing, according to police.

The Paris Salon works on the land in front of the house where Adrianinha's family lives. Relatives said they were shocked by the hairdresser's murder.

“She was a very dear person to everyone here. The family has always accepted the fact that she is a trans woman and there was never any prejudice ”, declared the victim's sister-in-law, Cláudia Neto.

She also said she learned of the crime at around 10:30 am. “They called me and told me to go there, at that moment. I arrived and that had happened, ”he said.

Cláudia said that her relatives “have no idea” of what may have happened. Adrianinha was single and considered a “full hand” professional. "She always took care of my hair and made me very beautiful," he said.

A team from the capital's Homicide Task Force, from the Department of Homicide and Personal Protection (DHPP), was on site to make the first investigations. The case will be investigated by the 5th Homicide Police.

Teams from the Institute of Criminalistics (IC) and the Institute of Medicinal Legal (IML) were also at the crime scene.

According to relatives, the funeral of Adrianinha Paris is scheduled for Thursday (8), at the Santo Amaro Cemetery, in the central area of ??Recife.


The Pernambuco Civil Police are investigating the murder of a hairdresser in the Dois Irmãos neighborhood, in the North Zone of Recife. The crime happened on the morning of Wednesday (7). According to the police, the victim, hairdresser Adriana Paris, 43, was at home when an unknown man came in and shot her.

The victim, who was a trans woman, died on the spot. At the time of the crime, Adrianinha, as she was known, was accompanied by a nephew who was only 4 years old. The child suffered no injuries.

A team from the Capital Task Force was at the crime scene to carry out legal proceedings. The police will not release any further information about the case until the end of the investigation. Investigations will be conducted by the 5th Homicide Police Station.
