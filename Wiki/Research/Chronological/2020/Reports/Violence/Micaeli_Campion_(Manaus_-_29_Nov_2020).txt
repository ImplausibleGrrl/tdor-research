The body of Micaeli Campion was found on the street in Manuas, Amazonas (Brazil) early on 29 Nov 2020. She had been stabbed in the chest.

Micaeli was 29.



Manaus / AM - Márcio Belchior Monteverde, 29, better known as “Micaeli”, was murdered in the early hours of Sunday (29), at Av. Autaz Mirim, in the São José neighborhood, East Zone.

The young man who was a transvestite was found stretched in a pool of blood near the Zezão Gymnasium, at about 4 am, by a group of people who passed by the place.

The police report that they were doing routine patrolling on the avenue when they saw several people gathered and came across the crime scene. Forensics were called and concluded that Márcio was murdered with a dagger blow to the chest. The police analyzed the crime scene and collected information that can help identify the culprit. The case is still under investigation.

Manaus - Transvestite Marcio Belchior Monteverde, popularly called “Micaeli”, 30, was killed at dawn on Sunday (29) with a dagger in the chest, on Avenida Autaz Mirim, São José Operário 1 neighborhood, east of the capital.

People found the victim's body at around 4 am, however, they did not provide much information to the military police of the 9th Companhia Interativa Comunitária (Cicom).

The Department of Technical-Scientific Police ( DPTC ) was called in to do the first forensic work on the spot, and found that the transvestite had a piercing in the chest, caused by the dagger.

The police found that the transvestite was a homeless person and, so far, there is no information about the suspect or motivation of the crime.

The body of “Micaeli” was removed to the Instituto Médico Legal (IML) and underwent necropsy exams.

The case will be investigated by the Specialized Homicide and Kidnapping Precinct (DEHS).
