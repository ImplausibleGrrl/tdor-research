The Civil Police confirmed on Thursday (19) that the transvestite found dead in an inn in the central region of João Pessoa was murdered. At first, the possibility of natural death was considered, but after a preliminary examination the hypothesis was discarded.

The body was found on Wednesday night (18), during the cleaning of the environment, located at Rua da República, central region of  João Pessoa . The victim's social name was Sheila. As soon as Marcos Paulo de Oliveira da Silva identified himself.

Witnesses said the victim arrived at the inn during the night, accompanied by a man, appointed as his companion. He left the place 20 minutes after check in.

Civil Police Chief Aldrovilli Grisi said he was the prime suspect, but has not yet been located.

Sheila was 27 years old. The aunt says that she has been beaten several times, victim of prejudice. The family was very concerned about her health and safety.

“Unfortunately, prejudice is widespread every day, growing more every day. Paulo was always chased and sometimes came home with bruises ”, he revealed.

The aunt said that, since she started dating, Sheila has moved away from the family and has been in an abusive relationship.

“It has been more than two months since his companion did not let him out, so it was passed on to us. He would come home, spend three or four days and always return. From then on he didn't come back, it's been two months. He was like being arrested at home because his partner would not let him out and made constant threats to him ”, he concluded.



Transvestite found dead with signs of suffocation in João Pessoa Center
According to the police, the death occurred in the morning and the body was found in an inn

11/18/2020 21:45 - Updated 19/11/2020 at 00:27

After receiving some calls, the Military Police found the body of a transvestite in an inn this Wednesday night (18), in Rua da República, central João Pessoa. According to the police, the death occurred in the morning and the body showed signs of alleged asphyxiation.

The victim was identified as Marcos Paulo de Oliveira da Silva, 27. She was from Rio de Janeiro, but lived in the capital of Paraíba.

According to information from witnesses, the transvestite arrived accompanied by a man, around 3 am. This man was said to have stayed at the site for about an hour and then was seen leaving. Strangely absent, the victim's friends searched, found her unconscious and called the police.

The place where the body was found is close to the Pavilhão do Chá. The victim was found under the bed. The military police cordoned off the area until the expert arrived, which is expected to investigate the cause and circumstances of the death.
