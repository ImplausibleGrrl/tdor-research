A transvestite was killed by firearm shots on Thursday night (26), around 11 pm, in front of the Dr. José Cursino de Azevedo School, on Folha 10, in the Nova Marabá Nucleus. According to information, the victim is Bianca, who was executed by two men who emerged on a motorcycle and fired three shots.

Military Police agents were called in by the popular, via the Integrated Operations Center (NIOP), and when they arrived at the scene, they came across the lifeless victim.

Bianca was formally identified as José Augusto Pereira Veras by the Military Police and worked on programs in the locality.

According to police officers who were at the scene, she and other colleagues were involved in a recent discussion with clients who accused Bianca of theft. There is a possibility that the crime was motivated by revenge.

The garrison carried out the preservation of the site until the teams of the Criminal Forensics and the Legal Medical Institute (IML) arrived to remove the body. The case must be investigated by the Homicide Department of the Civil Police. (Zeus Flag)
