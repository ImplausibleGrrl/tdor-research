Letícia Costa was shot dead in the street in Fortaleza,  Ceará (Brazil)on the night of 10 Aug 2020.

With her death, this particular city has killed at least 3 #trans women already this month.


Transvestite is shot to death in the center of Fortaleza
Letícia Costa was found dead at the intersection of Clarindo de Queiroz and Jaime Benévolo streets, on Monday night (10).
By G1 CE

A transvestite was killed with several firearm shots on Monday night (10), in the center of Fortaleza. According to witnesses, Letícia Costa, had been working in the neighborhood for about four years, but has been out of Fortaleza for the past four months, having returned this Monday to the capital of Ceará.

Still according to witnesses, occupants of a vehicle and men on two motorcycles were seen leaving the area where the body was found moments after shots were heard.
