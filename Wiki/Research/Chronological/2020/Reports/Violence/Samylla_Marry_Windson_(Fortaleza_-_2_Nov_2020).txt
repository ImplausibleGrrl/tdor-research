A transvestite suffered an attempted murder and was shot by two armed men on Saturday night (31) at Avenida Beira Mar, Praia de Iracema, in Fortaleza. The two suspects were arrested.

According to the Military Police, the pair was located moments after the crime at Fortaleza Airport. They were taken to the 13th Police District, where the case was registered. The crime would have been motivated by a disagreement between the victim and the suspects.

The transvestite was rescued to a hospital unit, where she receives care. There is no information about her health status. The case is being investigated by police.

A transvestite suffered an attempted murder and was shot by two armed men on Avenida Beira Mar, Praia de Iracema, on Saturday night (31). The two suspects were arrested.

The pair was located moments after the crime at Fortaleza Airport , according to the Military Police. They were taken to the 13th Police District, where the case was registered. The PM reported that the crime was motivated by a disagreement between the victim and the suspects.

The transvestite was rescued to a hospital unit, where she receives care. There is no information about her health status. The case is being investigated by police.
