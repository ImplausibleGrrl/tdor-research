On the afternoon of November 16, Juana Criollo, a female transgender, was found dead in her room located in the La Roldós sector, in the north of Quito.

According to Iván Naranjo, head of the Crimes Against Life Investigation Unit, the body had a blow to the head and there was evidence in the room that there was violence. The victim tried to defend himself.

In the neighborhood they knew her from the beauty office that she attended a few years ago. “She looked calm, she had no problems with anyone. He only walked with his pet, ”said José, a resident of the neighborhood.
