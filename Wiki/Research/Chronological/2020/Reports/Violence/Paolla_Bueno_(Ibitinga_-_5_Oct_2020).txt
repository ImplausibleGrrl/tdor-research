Paolla Bueno was stabbed by her ex-partner in Ibitinga, São Paulo (Brazil) on 5 Oct 2020.

She was 17. #SayHerName #TransLivesMatter #TDoR



A 17-year-old transsexual was murdered by her ex-partner on Monday (5) in Ibitinga (SP).

According to information from the Civil Police, the young woman was known as Paola. The victim was at her house when the ex-partner broke down the door and struck the knife.

Still according to the police, Paola was rescued, but she could not resist her injuries and died in the hospital.

The man fled the scene, but was found in a vacant lot near the murder site.

He confessed to the crime and was arrested in flagrante delicto for qualified homicide (futile, cruel means, impossibility of defending the victim and feminicide). The suspect was taken to the Provisional Detention Center (CDP) in Taiúva, where he is awaiting a custody hearing.


Late Monday morning (05), a person died after being stabbed in Ibitinga (SP).

The Fire Department was reportedly called after a transsexual was stabbed in the Santo Expedito neighborhood.

Paolla as she was known in the neighborhood, came to the rescue for the UPA. However, after two cardiorespiratory arrests, he did not resist and died.

The victim's companion was appointed as the author of the stabbing. The police are still recording the case.
