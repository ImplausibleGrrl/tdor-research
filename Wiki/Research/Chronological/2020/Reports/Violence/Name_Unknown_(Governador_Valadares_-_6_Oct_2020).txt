A body was found on the banks of the BR-381 on Tuesday morning (6) in Governador Valadares. According to the Federal Highway Police, the victim was located around 5:30 am. She was half naked, with her hands tied to her feet and showed several signs of violence.

Witnesses said she was a 27-year-old transvestite, but there was no identification document with the victim, who has not yet been recognized by relatives.

Still with the PRF, the team passed by the site the night before and the body was not there, so the crime probably occurred during the night.

The authorship and motivation of the homicide will be investigated by the Civil Police.

As it is a federal highway, the Federal Highway Police was called in to register the occurrence. The Civil Police will investigate the case.
