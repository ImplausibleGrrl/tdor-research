====== Neptaly Alejandro TT (10 May 2020) ======
Created Sunday 14 June 2020



[Neptaly Alejandro TT]
35
10 May 2020
Cuenca
Cuenca
Ecuador
stabbed


One person died after receiving several stab wounds. The crime occurred inside a house located between Rumi Urco and Cordillera streets, in Totoracocha in the city of Cuenca. The victim was identified as Neptaly Alejandro TT, 35, a native of the Guayas province. The police presume that it was a transvestite.

The emergency was logged by ECU 911 at 11:58 PM; Rescuers from the Fire Department came to the emergency calls, who confirmed the death.

According to the first investigations, the neighbors realized the fact of violence when they heard noises and screams. When checking, on the floor they found the body that had stab wounds in the abdomen, chest and head.

The case was brought to the attention of the authorities. Investigative units of the National Police in coordination with the Azuay Prosecutor's Office inspected the crime scene and recorded indications that help determine the circumstances of the crime. The aggressor is at large. The Police complied with the removal of the body for its transfer to the Forensic Center.

According to eyewitness accounts, prior to the crime , alcoholic beverages were reportedly on the scene.

The neighbors indicate that previously there were problems in that rental house and several days ago, residents gathered worried about the events. (I)
