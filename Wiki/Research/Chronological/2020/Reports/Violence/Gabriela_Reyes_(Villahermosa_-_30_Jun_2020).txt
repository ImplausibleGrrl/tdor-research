Indignation and mourning in the LGBT community of Tabasco
Gabriela "N", a transgender person, was found lifeless during the first hours of this Tuesday, her body presented blows and bullet impacts

It was located in front of the private Guayacán in the Sabina neighborhood. Photo: Casimiro Sánchez
Casimiro Sánchez | The Herald of Tabasco

Indignation and mourning in the LGBT and trans community of Tabasco after revealing the identity of the body that during the first hours of this Tuesday was found dead on calle 20 de Noviembre, just in front of the private Guayacán in the Sabina neighborhood.

The body was found lying on the pavement and had several bullet wounds, wearing a black blouse, blue skirt and sandals.

The victim suffered injuries caused by projectiles fired by a firearm, in addition to some blows to the back and face.

The FGE began investigation folder 070/2020 for the crime of qualified homicide.

It was Gabriela “N”, a transgender person, popularly known for participating in movements in defense of human rights and sex work at the state and national levels.
