The transvestite Bia, also treated as Natália, very well known in Campo Mourão because of the controversies she was involved in in the recent past, was shot dead earlier this Saturday afternoon (3), in São Pedro do Ivaí.

The crime happened in Jardim Juju. The Civil Police of Jandaia do Sul is on site. The Apucarana IML was also triggered.

According to popular information, at least eight shots were fired. The victim would have been hit in the head and chest.  


Still according to popular people, the transvestite had many enemies in that city, because it revealed the names of people who supposedly would have made a program with her. 

There is still no information about the perpetrator or perpetrators of the crime.


A transvestite was murdered with gunshots to the head this Saturday afternoon, October 3, 2020, in the municipality of São Pedro do Ivaí, Vale do Ivaí region.
According to information provided to Repórter do Vale, the murder took place in Jardim Juju, the victim was identified as Nathalia, known as “Bia”, the Military Police, from São Pedro do Ivaí and also from Jandaia do Sul, were called to answer occurrence. 

The Police carried out investigations and inquiries in order to identify the suspects, as well as to discover the motivation of the fact.
