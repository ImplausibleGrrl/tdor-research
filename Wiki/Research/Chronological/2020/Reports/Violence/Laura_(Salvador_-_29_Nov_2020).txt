Transvestite is executed with more than 30 shots in Pau da Lima
November 29, 2020 - 11:09 am
Death of Pau da Lima
A transvestite was executed this Sunday morning (11/29), in the neighborhood of Pau da Lima, in Salvador. The death was early in the morning, around 5 am, on Rua São Vicente

The victim was found with bound hands, a broken arm and several marks of gunfire. In addition, she was gagged. There were more than 30 shots and several bandits participated in the homicide, according to a source from Informe Baiano.

The IB source did not know the name of the young woman, who appears to be less than 28 years old, but said she lived in the neighborhood of Horizonte, where she would have been kidnapped and taken to Pau da Lima. The killers fled in a black car. The Civil Police reported that the case is being investigated by 2ªDH / Central, a police station that is part of the Department of Homicide and Personal Protection (DHPP).
