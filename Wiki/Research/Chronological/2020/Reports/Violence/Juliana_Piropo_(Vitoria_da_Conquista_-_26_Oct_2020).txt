Location: Avenida Integração (Rio Bahia), in Conquista.

A transvestite with a first name Juliana was murdered with gunshots to the head on the night of this Monday, 26, in Rio Bahia, a stretch of the Sumaré neighborhood in Vitória da Conquista (distant 527 km from Salvador), in the southwest of the state.

According to witnesses, the criminals arrived and fired about 8 shots into the victim's head. The military police are on site awaiting the arrival of the technical police department team.

Juliana lived in the Guarani neighborhood and was a very well-known person in the city.

The body has already been sent to the Legal Medical Institute, where it will undergo necropsy. The police are investigating the crime.

Violence returns to Avenida Presidente Dutra, also known as Avenida Integração, in the center of Vitória da Conquista. It was 8:15 pm and a sex worker was at her point of work when she was caught by a sniper.

She was hit by gunfire and died on the spot. The Mobile Emergency Service found the death. The 77th Independent Company of Military Police together with the Central Duty of the Civil Police and the Technical Police Department recorded the occurrence that will be investigated by the Homicide and Personnel Protection Police.

Juliana's body will undergo necropsy at the Instituto Médico Legal. The Coordination of Public Policies for Sexual Diversity in the Municipality of Vitória da Conquista has not yet commented on the matter.
