Cearense was murdered in São Paulo and family cries out for help



Transvestite from Ceará is beaten and killed in a hotel in São Paulo
December 10, 2020

Travesti from Ceará, Karen Cristina, 18, was beaten and killed last night (9), in a hotel in the city of São Paulo. The young woman's family is campaigning to bring her body from São Paulo to Fortaleza.

Karen even recorded a video moments before the crime happened, stating that she was in a hotel and sent it to her family. In another video recorded after the crime, the young woman appears lying on the floor of the establishment, unconscious, while a friend says that she was beaten by a client who refused to pay for a program.

According to Alessandra, Karen's sister, a report from the Legal Medical Institute (IML) in São Paulo attested that the cause of the young woman's death was head trauma. Karen Cristina went to São Paulo in August this year to work as a call girl and intended to return to Ceará on the 20th of this month.

Alessandra says that Karen was at the hotel accompanied by a client and friend, when her colleague took the client's cell phone and put it in the young woman's purse without her noticing. When the suspect missed the device, he searched Karen's bag and started the assaults after finding his cell phone. The friend and the attacker were arrested, according to the victim's sister.
