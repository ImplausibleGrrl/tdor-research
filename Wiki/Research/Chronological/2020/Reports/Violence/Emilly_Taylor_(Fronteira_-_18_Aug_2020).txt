wo men, one 30 and another 39 years old, were arrested on Tuesday (18) on suspicion of the murder of a transvestite in the city of Fronteira. The 26-year-old victim was found stabbed in the living room in the Vila Reis neighborhood. The crime is investigated by the Civil Police.

According to the police report registered by the Military Police (PM), the victim's brother said that when he arrived at her house around 6 am on Tuesday, he realized that the light was on and the TV was on. As soon as he opened the door, he found his sister injured and, shortly after, called the ambulance and called 911.

The health professionals who helped the transvestite informed the police that she was dead a few hours ago. The body was taken to the Legal Medical Institute (IML). The Civil Police expert was at the scene and collected a knife and the victim's cell phone.

During the surveys, the PM found out that the transvestite's boyfriend, 30, had fought with her and threatened her a few days ago. Witnesses said he was very jealous and that he often argued with his partner, with whom he had been in a relationship for about two years.

In fact, one of the witnesses reported to the police that, last Sunday (16), he went to a bar with the young woman and she said that the man had threatened her, saying that if he met her with another man, he would kill her. over there. The transvestite also told her friend that if she disappeared, it would be his fault.

The PM searched and found the suspect at a construction site in the rural area of ​​Fronteira. Inside his backpack was a bloodstained shirt. Before being taken to the police station, the man was taken to the Joint Health Unit, where it was found that he had a cut on his right thumb.

The man reported to the PM that, last Saturday (15), he left work and met his girlfriend at around 7pm at her house, where they spent the night together. Around 4 pm on Sunday (16), he left. The man justified that the cut on his finger was caused by sharpening a knife for his mother on Sunday night.

However, even during the surveys, it was found that there were several calls on the cell phone of the victim of a 39-year-old man, made on Sunday night. Upon being found, the suspect said that he has known the victim for more than ten years and had sex with her a few times, the last time being in the early hours of Saturday to Sunday.

Due to divergent points in the testimonies of the two suspects, both received a voice of arrest and were taken to the police station, where they were heard and released.

Also according to the delegate, the collection of genetic material was made for confrontation with materials found in the victim's body and on the spot.

“At that first moment, it was not possible to obtain evidence against the accused and they were released. However, this does not exclude the possibility of one of them having committed the crime ", added Bruno Salmen.

No possibility has been ruled out, not even the hypothesis that the perpetrator of the crime is yet to be identified. The Civil Police launched an investigation to investigate the case.
