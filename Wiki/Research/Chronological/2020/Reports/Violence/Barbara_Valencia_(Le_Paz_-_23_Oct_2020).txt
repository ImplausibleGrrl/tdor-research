Bárbara's body was found in her home with a stab wound to the neck in the early hours of Friday 23rd October.

> La Paz, Baja California Sur (BCS ). During the early morning hours of this Friday, October 23, the lifeless body of a person was located, in a home in the Cimarrón neighborhood, within the capital of Baja California Sur , the State Attorney General's Office (PGJE) reported.  
> 
> The events were reported around 1:12 am, in a house located on calle Segismundo Taraval, between Tuna and Urbano Ángulo, being attended by elements of the State Criminal Investigation Agency (AEIC) and experts from the Directorate of Expert Services, who corroborated the finding.  
> 
> Unofficial sources indicated that the victim was a transsexual woman, who was known as "Barbie", who had a wound along the neck.
> 
> The area was secured and the evidence located was safeguarded, which was sent to the laboratories for further evaluation and analysis, in order to help clarify the facts.  
> 
> "The deceased was identified. She was 25 years old and was originally from this capital city," detailed the statement issued by the Attorney General's Office in  Baja California Sur.
