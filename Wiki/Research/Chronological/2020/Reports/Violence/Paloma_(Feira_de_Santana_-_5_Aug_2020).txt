A #trans woman named Paloma found in vegetation in Três Riachos, in greater Florianópolis, Santa Catarina (Brazil) on 5 Aug 2020.

She had been strangled.



Transvestite found dead in thicket in the Três Riachos region

Posted on August 5, 2020by Central Police
Teams from the Homicide Police Station (DHPP) and the Technical Police Department (DPT) were around 4 pm on BR-116 Sul, in Três Riachos, to carry out the cadaverous survey of a man found by people in a thicket.

According to information, the victim was a transvestite and wore purple shorts and a black robe and had signs of strangulation. The man did not carry documents, but it was found that he was known as "Paloma".

Delegate Fernanda Gabriela da Cruz chaired the cadaverous survey and sent the body to the DPT, where it will be necropsied and await recognition.
