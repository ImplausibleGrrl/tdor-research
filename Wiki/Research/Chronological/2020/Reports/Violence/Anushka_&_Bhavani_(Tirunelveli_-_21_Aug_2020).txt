> The bodies of two trans women and their partner were found in two wells in the southern Indian state of Tamil Nadu on Friday morning (August 21), their murders having taken place “a few weeks ago”.
> 
> In the Palayankottai neighbourhood of Tirunelveli, the bodies of Anushka, 35, Bhavani, 34 and their partner Murugan, 38, were found packed into bags and stuffed into two wells near a bypass, police said.
> 
> The three lived in Narasinganallur near Suthamalli, according to friends who knew the trio well. They were part of a thriving trans community in the area and hoped to adopt a child.
> 
> Police have arrested three people, naming one suspect as Rishikesh, a Salem local who lives with a trans woman in Tirunelveli and knew Anushka personally.

Palayamkottai: Recently, a case of crime has come out of Tamil Nadu. There has been triple murder and now a shocking revelation has been made in the case. Two transgenders and a male were killed and the dead bodies were thrown into the well. It is being told that they were killed by strangulation. According to the news, three people have been arrested by the police in this case and now all three are being questioned.

This incident is being reported from Palayamkottai in Tirunelveli, Tamil Nadu. In this case, the police said that 'the bodies were packed in bags and thrown away. The dead have been identified as Anushka (35), Bhavani (34) and Murugan (38). In this case, the police said, 'Murugan married Anushka and Bhavani. He wanted to adopt a child. He gave five lakh rupees to a person named Hrishikesh for this. After receiving the money, Hrishikesh started avoiding him. Allegedly he cheated on them".

Apart from this, the police said that Murugan started messaging on social media to Hrishikesh's sister. After that Hrishikesh got angry when he came to know about this. He then murdered Murugan, Anushka and Bhavani together with his two companions. Later, the trio packed their dead bodies in bags and threw them in a well near the highway. In this case, some transgender complained to the police that they were unable to contact Anushka and Bhavani. After that, the police started the investigation and then the murder case was revealed.
