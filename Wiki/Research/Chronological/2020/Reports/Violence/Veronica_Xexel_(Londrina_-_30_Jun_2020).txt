Londrina - Pr | SHEMALE IS FOUND DEAD INSIDE HOME IN WEST ZONE

Late Tuesday morning (30th), the Military Police were called to attend to a finding of a corpse in a residence located on Rua Neptune, in Jardim do Sol, west of Londrina.

A neighbor to the property where the body was found noticed the absence of the person who lived in the residence since last Sunday (28), and went to the house to investigate the situation when he met the person of Manoel Messias Ferreira de Lima, 50, a transvestite, dead and with the body already in an advanced state of decomposition.

Manoel, who used the social name as Verônica, was half-naked and had some injuries on his body, including in the head region. The house was all removed, but the police did not find out if anything was taken from the place.

The Civil Police, through the Homicide Police Station and a Criminalistics expert were at the scene and gathered some information about the fact. An investigation will be launched to investigate the case.


The body of a transvestite was found inside a house in Jardim do Sol, west of Londrina on Tuesday (30). She was identified only as Veronica and had been missing since Sunday (28). The house is in an alley on rua Netuno and according to neighbors, the stench caught the attention. They also found the door of the house strange, and when they went to the scene they saw the body and called the Military Police. 

Criminalistics went to the scene, along with Civil Police from the Homicide Police. It is not yet known what happened at the scene and it was a murder. The causes of death have not yet been defined.
