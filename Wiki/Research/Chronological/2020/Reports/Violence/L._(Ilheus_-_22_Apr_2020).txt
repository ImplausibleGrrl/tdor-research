Wednesday, April 22, 2020
Homicide in Ilhéus; transvestite was stabbed to death

A homosexual, known as Leto, resident of Condomínio Sol e Mar 2, in block E, in the South Zone of Ilhéus, was stabbed to death inside the apartment where he lived. According to the police, the crime took place on Wednesday afternoon (22). The Technical Police Department was called in and performed the cadaveric examination and removed the body for necropsy. The 7th Coorpin Homicide Sector will investigate the case. (Complain Mouth)
