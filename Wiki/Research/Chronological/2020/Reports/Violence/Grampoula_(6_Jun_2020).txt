====== Grampoula (6 Jun 2020) ======
Created Sunday 14 June 2020


Grampoula
6 Jun 2020
Porto Velho (RO).
beaten
 
The brutal murder of a transvestite identified only as "Grampoula" happened at dawn this Saturday (6th) at a prostitution spot on Avenida Rio Madeira, Lagoa district, in Porto Velho (RO).

The victim had his face disfigured with sticks and the body was found half naked. The Military Police was called to the scene and asked for the Technical Expertise and rabecão. Next to the body was the piece of wood used in the brutal murder of the victim.

The brutal murder of a transvestite identified only as "Grampoula" happened at dawn this Saturday (6th) at a prostitution spot on Avenida Rio Madeira, Lagoa district, in Porto Velho (RO).

The victim had his face disfigured with sticks and the body was found half naked. The Military Police was called to the scene and asked for the Technical Expertise and rabecão. Next to the body was the piece of wood used in the brutal murder of the victim.

Agents from the Specialized Police Station for Crimes Against Life were present and investigations began to try to arrest the perpetrator of the murder and find out what the motivation was. The body was removed from the IML.

Porto Velho


The transvestite identified only as “Grampoula” was beaten to death on Saturday morning (6), at Avenida Rio Madeira, Bairro Lagoa, in Porto Velho.

A witness who was passing by witnessed the victim being beaten to death by another transvestite.

The Military Police (PM) was called, but when it arrived at the place “Grampoula” it was already dead. The victim had a disfigured face and was half naked.

A bloody piece of wood was found next to the victim's body.
