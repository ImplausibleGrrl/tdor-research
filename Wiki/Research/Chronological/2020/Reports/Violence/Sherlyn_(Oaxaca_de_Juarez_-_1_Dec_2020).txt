 person from the LGBT + community was executed tonight on Montes de Oca Street, in the Santa María Exmarquesado neighborhood.

According to reports, the events occurred at 9:00 p.m. when the victim was injured with a firearm by unknown subjects inside the aesthetic where he worked, later those responsible fled aboard a motorcycle.

Upon arrival, the paramedics of the Mexican Red Cross determined that the person no longer had vital signs.

The area was cordoned off by the police and investigators and experts carry out investigations at the site.

The emergency units that came to the scene identified the person as "Sharlín", whose previous name was Fernando, since he was a transvestite person who owned that aesthetic where he was executed.


They murder a stylist

On Tuesday night, Fernando N, a well-known stylist in the Santa María neighborhood, in the Ex Marquesado neighborhood, was shot to death by two unknown individuals who arrived at the scene on a motorcycle.

The police have ruled out robbery or assault in Fernando's case, since the subjects came directly to his premises to stab him.

As they are two people related to the lesbian-gay community, the elements of the State Investigation Agency (AEI) try to establish if they have any relationship that could lead to their clarification.

OAXACA, Oax., December 3 (EL UNIVERSAL) .- The murder of two trans women in the Central Valleys of Oaxaca caused a commotion in the LGBTTIQ + community in the state, as well as in the rest of the country, for which its members demanded justice for both victims: Geovanna and Sherlyn. According to a call that was announced on Wednesday, a march will be held this Friday that will depart at 11:00 am from the Fuente de las Ocho Regiones towards the zócalo of the Oaxacan capital, with the purpose of demanding justice for them , also asking the digital community to join these actions on social networks with the hashtags: #JusticiaPorGeovanna and #JusticiaPorSherlyn.

The body of Geovanna Angulo Ramos, a 27-year-old trans woman, was located last Monday, November 30, in a ravine in the jurisdiction of the municipality of Santiago Suchilquitongo, in the district of Elta. The young woman was seen for the last time since last November 25, and three days later the State Attorney General's Office notified her disappearance through a search file.

One day after Geovanna's body was found on December 1, Sherlyn was attacked with a firearm inside the beauty shop where she worked. Despite efforts to transfer her for medical attention, she lost her life en route. "Justice for all our murdered sisters!" Demanded the collective Marea Verde on its official Facebook page, and assured that the young woman was murdered by her ex-partner.
