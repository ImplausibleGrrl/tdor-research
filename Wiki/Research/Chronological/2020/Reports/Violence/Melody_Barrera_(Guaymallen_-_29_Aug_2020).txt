Melody Barrera was shot by the occupants of a car in Guaymallén, Mendoza (Argentina) on 29 Aug 2020. She was 27. #SayHerName #TransLivesMatter #TDoR



A transvestite was shot this morning from a car in Guaymallén and died on the spot, police sources confirmed.

The brutal attack occurred at 4 in the morning at the corner of the eastern side of Costanera and Correa Saa, in Guaymallén. The fatal victim was identified as Melody Barrera, 27, the sources added.

A call to 911 alerted about the existence of a person lying on the public highway, upon arrival of the acting personnel they found that a trans woman was lying on the sidewalk, who, at first glance, had several gunshot wounds to the chest.

The presence of an SEC ambulance was quickly requested, where upon arrival, the professional in charge found that Barrera had ceased to exist .

According to the first investigations, the authors would have passed aboard a vehicle firing shots, fatally wounding Barrera, and several 9mm caliber pods were also seized.



They murdered a transvestite with six bullets

The incident occurred this morning in the Costanera and Correa Saa area. The victim was missing belongings. They analyze the security cameras in the area.

A 27-year-old transvestite was shot to death at dawn this Saturday in Guaymallén and due to the fact the investigators requested what the security cameras in the area recorded to identify the aggressor.

According to the information accessed by El Sol, the victim was identified as Melody Barrera, who was in the area of Costanera and Correa Sáa practicing prostitution - this is what the first hypothesis supports - when she was approached by, at least, a man who shot him six times with a 9mm caliber pistol.

The detectives, led by the Homicide prosecutor Andrea Lazo,  do not rule out that it was an assault, although it is the weakest version of the instruction. This is based on the fact that the victim had a backpack in the first instance and later they detected that he was missing some belongings.

In any case, they do not rule out that people who passed through the place after the attack stole some valuable items.

From the beginning (the event occurred this Saturday at 4.30), the prosecutor Lazo ordered the activation of the protocol for transvesticides and this allowed the presence of a coroner on the scene. It was quickly detected that the body had six bullet wounds and that the impacts had been at close range.
