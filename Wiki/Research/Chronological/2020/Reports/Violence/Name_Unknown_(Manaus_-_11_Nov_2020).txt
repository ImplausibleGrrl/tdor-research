While doing program, transvestite is brutally shot to death
Posted on 11/11/2020 - 10:18 Last update 11/11/2020 - 10:18

Brazil - Images sent to the CM7 Portal on Wednesday morning (11), show a transvestite stretched out on the floor after being shot while doing a program on the corner of her home.

According to information in the video, the transvestite was killed by at least six shots, three of them in the head.

The case will be investigated by the Civil Police.
