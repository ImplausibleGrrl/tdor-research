Marlei was stabbed to death after a disagreement. Her assailant was arrested.

Márcio Ferreira de Souza, 39, was indicted for homicide.

> Marlei Pinto Costa, 36, who was a [trans woman], was murdered with knife blows this Sunday afternoon, May 10, in Rua Venezuela, Uldurico Pinto neighborhood, in Medeiros Neto.
>
> Shortly after the crime, a garrison of the 44th Independent Company of the Military Police (CIPM), investigated and managed to arrest the suspect, identified as Márcio Ferreira de Souza, 39, who confessed to the murder.
>
> At the headquarters of the 8th Coorpin de Teixeira de Freitas, during testimony to the delegate Bruno Ferrari, responsible for the regional duty, Márcio said that he murdered Marlei with three stabs after a disagreement between them. “I am a manual worker and [she] [Marlei] has always been talking badly about me and my family. I took the knife and went to [her] sister's house, where I killed [her], ”he says.
>
> Asked by the press about the possible feeling of regret, the murderer showed coldness and despite saying that he felt for the victim's family, he reported that he is ready to “pay” for the crime. The killer also said he received a death threat from the victim.
