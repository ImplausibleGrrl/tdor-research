Keila Nunes was shot in Barra de Santa Rosa, Paraíba (Brazil) on 6 Jun 2020.


Keil was shot in front of her home by two men on a motorcycle.

> A [trans woman], identified only as Keila, was shot and killed last Saturday (6) in the city of Barra de Santa Rosa, in Agreste da Paraíba.
>
> According to the police, Keila was in front of the house when two men on a motorcycle stopped and the rump fired several times. Even injured, she still ran into the house, but ended up dying.
>
> The pair fled and, at the time of publication of this article had not yet been found.
