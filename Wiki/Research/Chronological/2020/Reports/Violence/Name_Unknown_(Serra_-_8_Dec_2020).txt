Out of jealousy, companion sets fire to a shemale woman in Espírito Santo

09/12/2020 18h42

A 40-year-old transsexual woman had her body burned by her partner yesterday (8) in the Feu Rosa neighborhood, in Serra (ES), a city in the metropolitan region of Vitória.

According to the police, the assault was motivated by jealousy. The suspect, a 21-year-old transsexual woman, lived with the victim.

The Military Police reported that, after a fight, the suspect would have thrown gasoline and set fire to the victim, who was sleeping in a bed and was his companion. In addition to the victim, the fire also consumed the couple's residence.

Still according to the PM, the flames caused burns on the victim's face and chest, which was rescued and sent to the UPA of Castelândia. The health unit did not inform UOL about the victim's condition, but family members say the woman has 40% of her body burned.

According to the PM, people tried to contain the fire, fearing it would hit neighboring homes. However, it was only with the arrival of the Fire Department that the situation was controlled.

After the occurrence, military police were informed by the 3rd Regional Police Station that the suspect had been admitted to the UAP in Carapina and would be under escort.

The Civil Police reported that, after receiving assistance, she was referred to the Special Duty Station for Women in the Metropolitan Region and fined in flagrante delicto for murder qualified by the use of fire and impossibility of defending the victim. Subsequently, she was taken to the Viana Screening Center.

The proceeding of the act was forwarded and is under investigation by the Homicide and Protection of Women Police, of the Civil Police of Espírito Santo.
