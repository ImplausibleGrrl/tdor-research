Travesti is killed by a knife in the Bonsucesso neighborhood; police investigate the case
Written by Copywriting , 16:10 / 03 August 2020. Updated at 22:20 / 03 August 2020
The crime happened at dawn this Monday
The Department of Homicide and Personal Protection (DHPP) investigates the death of a transvestite  executed with knife blows at dawn on Monday (3). The crime took place on Pedro Cardoso Uchôa street, in the Bonsucesso neighborhood, in Fortaleza. A police inquiry was launched.

The body was found about 700 meters from a unit of the Environmental Police Battalion (BPMA), located near the Maranguapinho River.

According to the Secretariat of Public Security and Social Defense (SSPDS), the victim has not yet been identified. Forensic Forensic Teams from the State of Ceará (Pefoce) were at the scene, where they gathered evidence that they could axillary in police work.

The police search to capture the perpetrators of the crime. At the time of publication of this story, no one had been arrested.
