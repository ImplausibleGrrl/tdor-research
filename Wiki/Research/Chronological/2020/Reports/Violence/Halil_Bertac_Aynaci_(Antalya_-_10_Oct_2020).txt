The trans person was found dead next to the massage table in his home!
The incident took place yesterday at 18.30 in the Çaglayan neighborhood of Muratpasa district. Transgender Halil Bertaç Aynaci, who moved to the ground floor of the 5-storey building 4 years ago, began to live alone. The neighbors who saw Aynaci's house door open yesterday morning did not suspect the situation, thinking they had forgotten. However, when the door was open in the same way in the evening, a neighbor called Aynaci. Neighbors entered the house after no response.

'BRIGHT RISE'

Seeing that Aynaci was lying naked and motionless next to the massage table in the salon, her neighbors reported the situation to the 112 Emergency Call Center. The medical teams who came to the house determined that Aynaci had lost his life in the check they made. While police teams did not find any traces of beatings on Aynaci's body, they determined that there was hair on the inside of her fingernails.


'FRIEND'S TEAR SHIPPED'

Receiving the news of Aynaci's death, his friend came to his house and shed tears. Aynaci's lifeless body was taken to the Antalya Forensic Medicine Institute to determine the exact cause of death after the prosecutor and police investigated.
