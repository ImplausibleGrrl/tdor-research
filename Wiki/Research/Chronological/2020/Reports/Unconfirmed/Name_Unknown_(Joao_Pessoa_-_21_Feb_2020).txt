Transvestite seriously injured after being hit by three shots in João Pessoa
 February 21, 2020 Catolé Now

A transvestite was seriously injured after being hit by three shots, on Thursday night (20), in the Novais neighborhood, in João Pessoa. The victim was found by police officers passing by in a vehicle.

According to the police, the unidentified victim was wearing an anklet.

Samu was called and helped the victim to the Trauma Hospital of João Pessoa. According to rescuers, the transvestite was hit in the face, shoulder and wrist. The state of health is considered serious.

There is still no information on what motivated the crime and no suspects have been identified. The police make patrols in the vicinity.
