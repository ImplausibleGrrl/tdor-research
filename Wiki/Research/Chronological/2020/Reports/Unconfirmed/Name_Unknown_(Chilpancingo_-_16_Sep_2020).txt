Carlos Moreno | The Sun of Acapulco

A man dressed as a woman was attacked with bullets on Vicente Guerrero Boulevard, in Chilpancingo, during the early hours of this Wednesday.

The attack was reported to the authorities around 02:00 in the morning, at the height of the Obrera neighborhood, on the side of the busy boulevard, in the lanes that go from south to north, outside a bar, in the capital .

Three men are linked to the process as alleged perpetrators of homicide in Huitzuco
According to the first versions, the victim was on the aforementioned road when she was attacked with shots by armed subjects.

The authorities reported that the victim was a man dressed as a woman (transvestite) in his 30s, who was shot at least two times, one in the buttock, wearing a red skirt and a black blouse.

After the attack, State and Municipal Police arrived who found the victim sitting on the street with blood stains on his body, for which he was helped by paramedics and transferred to a hospital for medical attention.
