She was shot at dawn on Sunday (25), in Campo Grande, in Vila Progresso, a 26-year-old transvestite. She was accompanied by a friend when a black car appeared and shots were fired.

The attack took place around 1:30 am on Sunday (25), when the victim was making a point next to his colleague on Rua Carlinda Tognini with Rua Oclécio Barbosa Martins and a black Volkswagen Gol appeared and one of the occupants started shooting towards the transvestite .

She ran and fell next to a bus stop with four gunshot wounds, which hit her chest and hand. The Fire Department was called and the victim was rescued to Santa Casa. There is no information about the license plate or possible characteristics of the authors.
