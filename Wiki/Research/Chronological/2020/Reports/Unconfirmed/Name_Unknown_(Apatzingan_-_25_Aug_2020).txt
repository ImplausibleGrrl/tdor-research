They locate human remains of a dismembered woman in Apatzingán, Michoacán
Authorities from Apatzingán, Michoacán , reported the location of the human remains of a woman apparently a victim of gender violence .

by LaVerdad

August 25, 2020 13:44 hs

Another victim of gender violence adds to the statistics of the state of Michoacán , since the human remains of a dismembered woman were found inside plastic bags in the El Tarepe neighborhood, in Apatzingán; Criminal act that adds 160 female persons murdered so far this year in said entity.

Facts like this show that gender violence does not stop in Michoacán . Regarding the foregoing, it was reported that the police authorities were alerted that several plastic bags were lying in a vacant lot in the aforementioned neighborhood, from which blood came out and a strong smell of putrefaction was given off.

As a result of these events, elements of the Michoacán Police and the Municipal Police moved to the site and it was they who confirmed that it was the dismembered body of a person who was a victim of homicide, a situation for which the area was cordoned off and the social representation.

Subsequently, personnel from the Crime Scene and Expert Services Unit (USPEC), as well as elements of the Specialized Unit for the Investigation and Persecution of Doloso Homicide, were established at the scene of the events, who attested to the removal of the human remains in various plastic bags containing the dismembered body of the woman.

It transpired that once the protocols were concluded, the remains of the woman victim of gender violence were transferred to the local Forensic Medical Service, for the purposes of the law, waiting for them to be recognized

It should be mentioned that in 2019, Michoacán ranked seventh in the entire country for the crime of intentional homicide against women, registering a total of 172 murder victims throughout the Michoacan territory, according to a report issued by the Executive Secretariat of the National Public Security System (SESNSP).
