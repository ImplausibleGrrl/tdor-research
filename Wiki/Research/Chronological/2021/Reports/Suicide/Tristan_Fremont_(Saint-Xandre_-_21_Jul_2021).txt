> Charente-Maritime: a body found, which could be that of Tristan Frémont
> 
> At the end of May, the gendarmerie had launched a call for witnesses to find Tristan Frémont.
> Screenshot
> By charentelibre.fr , published onJuly 27, 2021 at 5:13 pm.
> 
> The prosecution believes that the body discovered in Saint-Xandre on July 21 could be that of Tristan Frémont, 19, who has been missing since May 24.
> 
> On Wednesday July 21, a farmer discovered a lifeless body in Saint-Xandre, Charente-Maritime, while he was harvesting a field. It is in this town in the agglomeration of La Rochelle that Tristan Frémont, 19, disappeared on May 24 after leaving the family home.
> 
> Quoted by Sud-Ouest , the prosecution believes that the body is " very probably " that of the young man. " Several bodily identification elements, including his measurements, suggest that it is most likely Tristan Frémont. Next to this body, we notably found an audio headset, " he said.
> 
> DNA analysis
> A DNA analysis has been ordered, which will confirm or not the identity of the corpse. The results should be known at the end of the week.
> 
> In June, searches had mobilized gendarmes, residents and volunteers as part of an investigation for a worrying disappearance. They had given nothing.
