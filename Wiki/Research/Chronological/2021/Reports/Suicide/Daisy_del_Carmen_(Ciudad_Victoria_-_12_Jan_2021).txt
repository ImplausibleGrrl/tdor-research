Daisy died by suicide. Before transition she had been editor of the video game portal Atomix.

> Atomix deeply regrets the death of its former editor, Daisy del Carmen.
> 
> Today, we regret having to share the news about the sensitive passing of Daisy del Carmen , who was part of the Atomix subsidiaries as editor at the beginning of the last decade and until 2014.
> 
> The news was confirmed via Twitter by Ophelia Pastrana, another prominent figure in the video game community, who was close friends with Daisy:
> 
> It is speculated that his possible cause of death was suicide, although for now this has not been confirmed one hundred percent. During her time with us, we knew her as [deadname] del Carmen and she later changed her name to Daisy.
> 
> Rest in peace, Daisy del Carmen.
