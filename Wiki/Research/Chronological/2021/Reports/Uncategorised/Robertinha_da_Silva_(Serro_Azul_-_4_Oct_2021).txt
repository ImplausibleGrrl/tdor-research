TRAVESTI FOUND DEAD IN THE RURAL AREA OF PALMARES
Monday October 4th, 2021

A transvestite was found dead this Monday morning (4), in the district of Serro Azul, a rural area of Palmares, in Mata Sul de Pernambuco.

The body of Roberto José da Silva, 43, known as “Robertinha”, was found by a fisherman on the banks of a river, near the Penderaca plantation. The victim had been missing since last Thursday (30). Since then, family members have lost contact and have started looking for the relative with the help of local residents and through publications on social networks.

The Civil Police was there and called the Criminalistics Institute (IC), which carried out the first surveys and then the body was sent to the Legal Medicine Institute (IML), in Recife. Where after 30 days the report will be issued informing the cause of death.

The case was registered by the police as a death to be investigated.
