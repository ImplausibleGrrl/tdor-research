> Sylvie Markiewicz, age 53 of Westbrook, died suddenly on August 23, 2021. She was born on February 10, 1968, in Nashua, New Hampshire, the child of John and Rose Markiewicz. Besides her parents, she is survived by her two children who she loved deeply, JohnPaul of South Portland and Jay of South Paris. She is also survived by her brother Jeff of Virginia and her loving husband, and best friend Skip Brushaber of Westbrook. She will also be missed by her four-legged buddies Eddie, Beanie, and Grand dog, Cooper.
> 
> Sylvie was a master electrician and for the last twenty-one years did lighting design for Allied Engineering in Portland.
> 
> Sylvie had a great love for all music and especially the Grateful Dead. For years she rode a Harley and along with Skip traveled around the US and Canada.
> 
> Sylvie had a gentle and kind soul and helped many people through difficult times. She was an active member of Recovery and LGBTQ+ Community. She offered love and support to many people struggling with addiction and gender identity. Sylvie had no idea how many people she helped, but the number has become apparent since her passing and continues to grow.
> 
> There will be a Memorial Service and Celebration of Life at St Ansgars Lutheran Church, 515 Woodfords St. Portland September 11, 2021, at 1:00 P.M. Poetry, songs, or just memories of Sylvie encouraged.
