The transvestite found dead on the banks of the BR-116 was identified, a stretch that links Vitória da Conquista to Cândido Sales, last weekend, according to an article published in Rodrigo Ferraz's Blog .

According to information obtained by our report, she was known as Tainá and resided in the city of Cândido Sales.

The body was sent to the Conquest Technical Police Department. It is not yet known if she was run over. The cause of death will be investigated.
