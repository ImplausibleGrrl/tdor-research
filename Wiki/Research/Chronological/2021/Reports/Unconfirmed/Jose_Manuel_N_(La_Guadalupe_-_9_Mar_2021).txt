They murder a young man on the beaches of Tecolutla
The deceased young man was identified as José Manuel "N", 29, a member of the LGBTTTI community

TECOLUTLA, Ver.– A young man was shot to death and his body was abandoned in the beach and hotel area of ??the town of La Guadalupe, where he was located by a group of tourists, who reported it to the authorities.

The scene was attended by members of emergency groups, who confirmed that the body had several gunshot wounds, mainly in the chest.

The deceased young man was identified as José Manuel "N", 29, a member of the Lesbian, Gay, Bisexual, Transsexual, Transgender, Transvestite and Intersex (LGBTTTI) community , who was widely known and appreciated in that population.

The ministerial authorities are investigating the motive for the crime and José Manuel's acquaintances suspect a possible hateful attack.

The State Police cordoned off the place where the body was found and Expert Services personnel dedicated themselves to gathering evidence to transfer them, along with the body, to the amphitheater based in Papantla, to continue with the investigations.

In addition, the Ministerial Police contributes to the proceedings, which is why it has approached the relatives of José Manuel and residents of the area in search of more evidence.
