They demand justice for a trans woman who received at least 4 stab wounds in the San José neighborhood of Villa Mercedes. social media


A trans woman was attacked and received at least 4 stab wounds. She was badly injured, she was surgically treated and although she is recovering, the aggressor is missing.

The incident occurred on Tuesday, in the San José neighborhood of Villa Mercedes , then the woman had to undergo surgery at the "Juan Domingo Perón" Polyclinic. The last medical report received by the Police, the girl is stable and is recovering in the common room.

“Around 3.30 pm, a colleague named Valeria Oviedo came to our house and was approached by a subject, with whom she had previously had encounters. By trusting him, he goes home. I don't know what misunderstanding there was between them, and Valeria was attacked by him, giving her multiple stabs in the body. The most serious was the one that punctured his lung, ”said a friend of Valeria's for Radio La Bomba Violeta.

Valeria was helped by a neighbor and assisted by her friends, who called the ambulance and the police. “The boy, when the police went to his home, he was no longer there. To date, he is a fugitive, "he added.
