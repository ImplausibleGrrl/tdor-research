Suspected of killing restaurant owner - Diario La Tribuna
The Police arrested a suspect of having killed a member of the LGTBI community in Sandy Bay, Bay Islands.

ROATÁN, Bay Islands . The Police arrested a 21-year-old man, suspected of participating in the death of a member of the Gay Transsexual Bisexual and Intersex Lesbian Movement (LGTBI) in this island area of the country.

In the Sandy Bay, Bay Islands community, police officers detained the suspect, originally from Roatan, Bay Islands and a West End resident.

He is accused of being the alleged person responsible for the death of Samuel Ernesto Friddrerks Ebanks (49), owner of a famous restaurant in the Light House, in West End, and who was killed with a knife.

The victim, Samuel Ernesto Friddrerks Ebanks (49), died after receiving several stab wounds to different parts of the body.
The death of the victim occurred in the sector of Sandy Bay, Gibson Bight, Roatán, Bay Islands, today, and according to the preliminary medical report, the deceased had several wounds caused by a knife-type knife.

After reporting his death, the police agents carried out identification and location investigations of the perpetrator, resulting in the capture of the accused.

As an indication of his participation, the arrested man had a knife-type weapon seized that was supposedly used for the commission of the crime. (XM)
