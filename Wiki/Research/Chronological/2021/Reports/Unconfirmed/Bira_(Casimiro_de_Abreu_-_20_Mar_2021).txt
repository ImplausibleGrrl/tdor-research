CRUELDADE: Transvestite is killed and has his head beheaded by a criminal
Essay March 21, 2021

He was identified as Ubiratã, the young man who died in a cruel way at dawn on Saturday (20), in the localization of the São João subdivision in Casimiro de Abreu, the Baixada Litorânea do Rio.

Bira as he was known was killed and had his head cut off with an ax. His body was found inside a stream at the end of 20th Street in the subdivision.

The victim's head was found on the opposite side of where his body was found. Fire department staff in a search found the member who was severed from the victim.

Ubiratã's body was removed at the end of the morning to the Macaé IML. The case was registered at the 121st DP of Casimiro de Abreu.
