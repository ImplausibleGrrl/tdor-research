Police are called to attend alleged home invasion and find transvestite injured with 3 shots in MS
The Military Police, when called to attend an alleged home invasion, on Camboata Street, Vila Moreninha, in Campo Grande, this Saturday (20), found a transvestite, 27 years old, wounded with shots in the thigh, chin and buttock.

According to the police report, the military said the victim was on the floor, bloodied and "said nothing, just moaned". They then called the fire department and the young woman was taken to Santa Casa.

At the scene, a witness said he heard several shots and soon after saw someone entering the yard.

The case was registered at the Community Emergency Department (Depac) of the Specialized Police Center (Cepol), as an attempted murder.
