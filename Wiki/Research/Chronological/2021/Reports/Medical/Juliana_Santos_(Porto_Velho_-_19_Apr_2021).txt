Juliana Santos suffered a cardiac arrest during a cosmetic surgery procedure in a residence in Porto Vehlo, Rondônia (Brazil) on 19 Apr 2021.

She was 22.


The fact happened at the end of the night this Monday (19), in a residence on Rua Borba, Socialist neighborhood, east of the capital. The victim identified as Francisco, 22, died during an aesthetic procedure performed in a clandestine manner.

According to information from the Military Police, the victim began to feel ill during the application of hydrogel on the buttocks, then had a cardiac arrest. An advanced Samu team was quickly deployed but the young man was dying.

The 23-year-old boy who applied the product to Francisco was arrested in the act and placed in the van of the vehicle. The victim's mother reportedly informed the police that her son had been HIV-positive since she was 15 years old, and that she was sick with pneumonia, but she didn't tell anyone.

At the end of the praxis work, the body was removed to the IML where an autopsy should be performed, pointing out the true cause of death. The accused was sent and presented at the Central de Flagrantes where he will be responsible for the crimes of manslaughter and illegal exercise of the profession.
