She passed away on Wednesday night, 21, in Goioerê Luana Bernardino, 33, who was a trans woman. She had a massive heart attack at her residence on Rua Campo Mourão, in the center of Goioerê.
The wake of Luana Bernardino will be held in the mortuary chapel of the Paraná do Oeste district, in Moreira Sales, and the burial will take place at 3 pm at the Paraná do Oeste Cemetery.
To the bereaved family, the condolences of the Goionews team.
                               

Goioerê News
Luana's body (André Ricardo) will be buried in Paraná do Oeste
Thursday, April 22, 2021.

André Ricardo, 33, better known as Luana, will be buried in the late afternoon of Thursday, 22, in the cemetery of Paraná do Oeste

With the burial scheduled for 4:00 pm in the cemetery of Paraná do Oeste, the body of André Ricardo Bernardino, better known as Luana, who died last night, Wednesday, 21, victim of myocardial infarction, will be buried. The burial will take place in Paraná do Oeste, where her relatives live, her mother owns a commercial establishment in that district.

In fact, André Ricardo, was a transvestite, whose name, when he dressed as a woman, was called Luana. The burial is scheduled for 4:00 pm with the funeral taking place in the Mortuary Chapel of Paraná do Oeste.

Luana drew attention for the aesthetic beauty and was admired by everyone who knew her.

To the bereaved family, the condolences of the Tribuna da Região.
