Saulette's body was found partially clothed a local road. She was a stylist who worked in Salvatierra.

> Tarimoro.- The body of a trans woman was found partially clothed and with traces of violence on the road that leads to the La Cuadrilla community.
> 
> From early this Saturday, residents became aware of the existence of the trans woman who was partially identified as Saulette, who was originally from Salvatierra.
> 
> The victim presented various blows to both the face and the thorax, as well as injuries caused by a knife.
> 
> Although her details have not been officially released, it transpires that the victim was a stylist and works in her place of origin, the municipality of Salvatierra.
> 
> The place where the victim was found was processed by experts from the Prosecutor's Office to initiate the first investigations that help determine the mechanics of the homicide.
> 
> While the investigations are being carried out by the Criminal Investigation Agents (AIC), the body was taken to the Forensic Medical Service (Semefo) for a legal necropsy.
