> A [trans woman], initially identified as Raquel, was shot dead this Saturday (11), near a forró house – known as 'Forró do Painho', in the Unamar neighborhood, in the second district of Tamoios in Cabo Frio, in the Lakes Region.
> 
> Preliminary information reveals that Raquel would have been killed with gunshots to the back of the head.
> The military and civil police were at the scene, after which the investigation was carried out and the victim's body was removed to the IML in Cabo Frio.
