Lorrane de Souza was stabbed in Petrolina, Pernambuco (Brazil) on 19 Aug 2021. Her killer was arrested at the scene. #SayHerName #TransLivesMatter #TDoR



> The trans woman, Lorrane de Souza, resident of Juazeiro, in the North of Bahia, was murdered in the early hours of this Thursday (19th) with blows with a machete in the municipality of Petrolina, in the hinterland of Pernambuco.
> 
> According to the information, the crime took place near the Campo das Flores Cemetery, in the central area of ​​​​the city. Lorrane was rescued at the University Hospital in Petrolina, but she did not resist her injuries.
> 
> Also according to the information, the accused to the crime. He reportedly reported that he committed the murder after the victim assaulted him.
> 
> According to Blog Ednevaldo Alves, the author of the crime said that after the robbery he went after the woman to retrieve her belongings, and that the two entered into a physical fight. in the trunk of the car and took the tool in an attempt to defend himself.
> 
> The man was arrested in the act and taken to the police station. The case is being investigated.
> 
> On the Preto no Branco program broadcast on Transrio 99 FM radio, Alzir Brasileiro, president of the NGO Cores, said that he is following the case and trying to contact the victim's relatives.
> 
> “Lorrane was a victim of hate, of transphobia. If she had had the opportunity to have a decent job, access to education, she would not have been stabbed to death. We don't need to survive, we need to live. Stop us killing,” declared the activist.
> 
> In January 2018, Lorrane was a victim of police violence [see https://pretonobranco.org/2018/02/07/lorrane/] during the Juazeiro carnival. At the time, the PNB followed the case and the testimony that the victim gave to Captain José de Alencar, during a complaint to the Military Police's internal affairs department. ( remember here )



> A man was arrested in the act, suspected of stabbing a trans woman to death in the municipality of Petrolina, in the hinterland of Pernambuco. The crime took place at dawn this Thursday (19), near the city's highway. The victim was rescued, but entered the hospital lifeless. 
> 
> According to the Civil Police of Pernambuco, no identification document was found with the woman. After being hit with several knife blows, the victim was rescued by the Mobile Emergency Care Service (SAMU) to the University Hospital of the Federal University of Vale do São Francisco (HU-Univasf), with serious injuries. By means of a note, the unit informed that the victim has already arrived at the scene lifeless and that "the necessary legal protocols were adopted for the referral" of the body to the Institute of Forensic Medicine (IML).
> 
> The author of the crime, identified as Alexandre Gomes da Silva, aged 34, alleged that he was in his vehicle waiting for a friend and that the victim had gotten into his car and stolen some belongings. According to the version he told the police, the criminal action had taken place in self-defense. 
> 
> Chief of Police Magno Neves, responsible for the case, informed that the Civil Police is already analyzing the security cameras in the place to check if the suspect's version matches reality. He was arrested in the act and fined for murder. The delegate also informed that the suspect will be referred to a custody hearing. 


> Petrolina dawned this Thursday (19) with the repercussions of a barbaric crime. A man murdered a transvestite with a machete in the vicinity of the central cemetery. The victim was identified only as 'Lorraine'.
> 
> The killer, who was arrested by military police from the 5th Battalion (BPM), confessed to a crime, which occurred shortly after midnight. At the police station, he reported that he had stopped at the place to urinate and was approached by the victim, who allegedly tried to rob him. It is possible that Lorraine was killed inside the car, as there are bloodstains, but the Civil Police (PC) is still investigating this detail.
> 
> The transvestite was rescued at the University Hospital (HU), but did not resist the injuries. The accused resides in Juazeiro (BA) and asked for his wife to be informed about the fact.
> 
> The information comes from the reporter Ivo da Hora/Programa Edenevaldo Alves.
