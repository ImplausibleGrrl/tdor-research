> Transvestite is found dead in the Sector of Embassies, in Brasília, with a gunshot mark on her head
> Case is investigated as a homicide. Civil Police search for suspects; victim's identity was not disclosed.
> By G1 DF
> 
> 08/26/2021 1:05 PM  Updated 5 hours ago
> 
> The body of a woman was found this Thursday morning (26), at the Southern Embassies Sector, in Brasília. According to the Civil Police, the victim is a transvestite and had a gunshot wound to his head. Nobody was arrested.
> 
> A man passing by the site, near the Canadian embassy, found the victim down and called the police at around 6:00 am. According to police chief Marcelo Portela, who is investigating the case, the forensic experts found condoms and lubricants at the site, which is located on 803 South.
> 
> The woman has already been identified, but her name was not disclosed. The 1st Police Station, in Asa Sul, registered the case as a homicide .
> 
> Also according to the delegate, the woman worked at Conic and "has a history of threats and fights with clients". However, he did not give details about the occurrences.
> 
> Until the publication of this report, the police tried to access images of the place, to help with investigations. According to the corporation, the place is deserted and it gets very dark at night.
