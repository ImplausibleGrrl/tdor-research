Raiane was shot in Caruaru, Pernambuco (Brazil) on 25 Aug 2021.

Hers was the second killing of a #trans person in the municipality in 4 days.



Raiane's body was found in the street on the morning of Wednesday 25th August. Hers was the second killing of a trans woman in the municipality in 4 days.

> A homicide took place this Wednesday (25), in Caruaru, in the Agreste region of Pernambuco. An as-yet [formally] unidentified waste picker was shot dead on the 2nd Travessa Antônio Martins, in the Vassoural neighborhood, close to the wet passage of Saldanha da Gama.
> 
> The body was located early in the morning without any identification and sent to the Institute of Forensic Medicine (IML). According to witnesses, it is that of a trans woman known as Raiane.
> 
> This was the 7th homicide of August in Caruaru. From January until now 73 people have been killed in the municipality.
