Valeria's body was found inside a residence in Villa de Álvarez, Colima (Mexico) on 7 Jul 2021. She had been strangled.

Valeria was 25.


> In Colima , the deaths of two women victims of femicides have been reported. This is the case of Valeria Carrasco, a 25-year-old transsexual woman; in addition to Martha Leticia "Tita" , a minor under 16 years of age. 
> 
> This morning the body of Valeria Carrasco , a 25-year-old transsexual woman, was found inside her home located in the Villas de Oro neighborhood, in the municipality of Villa de Álvarez, Colima. The LGBTTTIQ + community of Colima has been dismayed and demands that the facts be clarified using the hashtag #justiciaparaValeria, in the same way they are preparing a demonstration for this weekend.
> 
> With this case, there are already three trans femicides so far in 2021 in Colima , in addition to being the second registered in the municipality of Villa de Álvarez. 


> After it became known that this Wednesday morning a person with signs of violence was located in the Villas de Oro neighborhood, in Villa de Álvarez, family and friends announced that the person killed (hanging) is Valeria " N ”transsexual girl.
> 
> Faced with this brutal event, the LGBTTTIQ + community of the state of Colima, as well as friends and family of Valeria, demand justice so that her death does not go unpunished, for which they urge the state authorities to investigate this homicide and arrest the alleged perpetrators. .
> 
> It should be remembered that it was unofficially announced that this day around 4 in the morning, the body of a person with signs of violence was found inside a house on Pablo Silva García Street, where he was later found reported that the victim suffered mechanical asphyxia (hanging).
