> The Department of Homicide and Personal Protection (DHPP) is going to investigate the death of a trans woman in the South Zone of São Paulo.
> 
> Her body was found bleeding during the morning of this Saturday (23) at Alameda do Uapês, in Planalto Paulista.
> 
> A friend revealed that her name was Vitória, she came from Ceará and had no relatives in São Paulo. She also said that she was doing programs, had left yesterday in a client's car and that he was the one who threw her out in the street where she was found dead.
> 
> In a statement, the Secretariat of Public Security of São Paulo (SSP-SP) informed that "military police officers on patrol received a call, via Copom, of the occurrence of aggression in Alameda dos Uapês, in Planalto Paulista, south of the capital. the agents found the victim (not identified) on the ground, bleeding. Assistance was requested from the SAMU team, which found the death. The case was registered at the 16th DP, Vila Clementino, and will be investigated by the DHPP. pertinent experts on the spot and on the victim".



> Transsexual is killed on Indianópolis avenue, in São Paulo
> Residents of the region complain about the practice of prostitution in the vicinity
> By Folhapress, by Paulo Eduardo Dias
> 10/23/21 - 22:16
> São Paulo plateau
> 
> An upper-class neighborhood in the south zone of São Paulo, Planalto Paulista reported the murder of a transsexual
> A transsexual was found dead during the early hours of this Saturday (23), in the vicinity of Avenida Indianópolis, in Planalto Paulista, an upper-class neighborhood in the south of São Paulo.
> 
> The region is known as a traditional prostitution spot in São Paulo's nightlife, which gathers female sex workers, transsexuals and transvestites.
> 
> According to the SSP (Secretary of Public Security), military police patrolled the neighborhood when they were called to attend to an incident of aggression in the Alameda dos Uapês.
> 
> At the scene, they found the victim on the ground and bleeding from his body. The PM claimed to have been called at 3:06 am, and at the appointed place they found a person wounded by a knife. The Samu (Mobile Emergency Care Service) was called and found the death on the spot. She had not been identified at the conclusion of this text.
> 
> Call girls who work in the region say the transsexual was stabbed and thrown out of a car. The case was registered with the 16th DP (Vila Clementino), but will be investigated by the DHPP (Department of Homicide and Protection of Persons).
> 
> Unhappy with the movement of prostitution in the region, an association representing residents and condominium traders filed a lawsuit in February this year for the São Paulo City Hall to regulate the work of program girls in the capital of São Paulo.
> 
> A principal intenção é frear uma possível desvalorização imobiliária em regiões como Planalto Paulista e Moema, ambas na zona sul, e Butantã, na zona oeste, além de amenizar incômodos a vizinhos de pontos de prostituição.
> 
> In the action, Acresce (Association of Residential and Commercial Condominiums) asks that, with the regulation of the profession, the city hall start to inspect the work of program girls.
> According to information from the website of the TJ-SP (São Paulo Court of Justice), the process has already had a manifestation by the State Public Ministry and since the 21st of this month it is awaiting judgment.

> A trans woman from Ceará was stabbed to death in São Paulo in the early hours of this Saturday (23). According to witnesses, people stopped in a car, left the body in the Planalto Paulista neighborhood and then left.
> 
> Vitória's friends, as she was identified, found her 30-year-old body in one of the neighborhood's streets. The Military Police Operations Center (COPOM) confirmed that the victim had knife wounds. The case was registered in the 16th Police District, but ended up being referred to the State Department of Homicide and Protection of Persons (DHPP).
> 
> Vitória had left Ceará for the capital of São Paulo in search of a better life.
