The body of an unidentified trans woman was found with signs of violence inside a residence in El Salto, Jalisco (Mexico) on (I think) 14 Jun 2021.

Her partner is the primary suspect.




They locate the body of a trans person, in El Salto
June 13, 2021

Inside a house located in the El Mirador subdivision in the municipality of El Salto, the body of a trans person was found.

The events occurred on calles Ocaso and Asteroide, to the point, elements of the police of said municipality arrived, proceeding to cordon off the area and requesting the presence of the municipal medical services who, when carrying out the procedure under protocol, mentioned that It was a trans person and when evaluating the body, they mentioned that it had several days of cadaveric evolution.

In addition, authorities mention that, traces of violence were found and she was cowering , the data obtained was received by the Public Ministry to open and follow up on the investigation folder of this macabre finding.

Already personnel from the Jalisco Institute of Forensic Sciences transferred the body to the metropolitan morgue to perform the autopsy of the law.
