Transvestite known as Tallyta Soares, 23 years old, was killed in the early hours of Sunday, November 28th. The crime took place shortly after 1:20 am, on Rua Colombo on the corner of Edu Rocha, in Corumb치.

The victim's body was on the sidewalk and had a perforation in the chest region. She didn't carry documents, but there was a cell phone on her hip. The Fire Department was called in, but she was already dead.

The Military Police and the Civil Police Forensic team attended to the incident. The Di치rio Corumbaense found that witnesses who were at the site, known as a prostitution point, revealed that the transvestite had gone out with a client and then returned. The customer came back and wanted the money back, Tallyta refused and the individual left. Then he returned in a red car with three other people.

Also according to witnesses, there was an argument, at which time the author hit the victim with a knife, got into the car and fled with the other three individuals.

The delegate on duty at the 1st Civil Police Precinct, Jhonny Garcia Trindade Monteiro, informed this  Diary that a knife was found at the scene, but it has not yet been confirmed whether it was the same used in the crime. Investigations follow to find the author of the murder. 



This Sunday morning, 23-year-old Elielson Soares da Silva, known as Talita, was stabbed to death in Corumb치, 444 kilometers from Campo Grande. The victim allegedly left with the author of the crime, who was not found.

According to the Military Police, a witness said that Talita went out with one of the possible perpetrators and some time later returned to Rua Colombo, downtown, in a red Fox. Three other people were still inside the car.

There was an argument and one of the men stabbed the victim with a knife, who was hit in the chest and died on the spot. The suspect got into the car and fled and was not located. The case is treated as a simple homicide.
