Devanny Cardiel was shot inside a salon in León, Guanajuato (Mexico) on 5 Sep 2021.




> They kill a trans woman in her aesthetic in Paseos del Molino
> Neighbors were concerned about the death of Devanny, who was attending an aesthetic at her house on Calle Molino de Guanajuato
> 
> Leon, Gto. The death of a transsexual woman in her aesthetics puzzled the residents of calle Molino de Guanajuato in the Paseos del Molino neighborhood.
> 
> This crime was registered at 7:45 p.m. in the business located in a house on the aforementioned street and the corner of calle Molino de Independencia.
> 
> Neighbors claimed to have heard at least a couple of shots from a short-caliber weapon at the home where the victim lived and had his business.
> 
> Municipal police found the bloody body of the woman in the main room of the house.
> 
> Later, paramedics from the rescue corps arrived and confirmed the death of the victim, who was approximately 30 to 40 years old .
> 
> Most of the residents who approached the cordonings guarded by the Municipal Police and the National Guard and identified the deceased person as Devanny Cardiel, a trans ambassador for the state of Guanajuato.
> 
> His neighbors were concerned about his death as there could be a hint of hatred towards him and the shooting crimes registered in the colony in recent days.
> 
> At press time, no detainees were reported who were involved in the murder of the trans woman.
