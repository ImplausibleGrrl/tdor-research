J. S. Cardoso was shot by a client in Parauapebas, Pará (Brazil) on 28 Apr 2021.

She was 21.




> Transvestite is killed with six shots at the point of prostitution
> Man approached on a motorcycle and shot the victim in the back and head. The death will be investigated by the Homicide Department of the Civil Police.
> 
> Wednesday, 04/28/2021, 15:29 - Updated 04/28/2021, 15:29 - Author: With information Correio de Carajás
> 
> A homicide case was registered by the Civil Police at dawn on Wednesday (28). A transvestite was shot to death at a point of prostitution in Parauapebas, in southeastern Pará.
> 
> According to the Civil Police, at about 1 am, the transvestite identified as Joas Sena Cardoso, 21, was at a prostitution spot that is located on Avenida Presidente Kennedy, at the entrance of Bairro Beira Rio, when she was approached by a man wearing a reflective vest and who was on the back of a motorcycle, when approaching the victim, he fired about six shots at the call girl, who died on the spot.
> 
> According to authorities, the transvestite was shot in the back and five in the head. It is not yet known what motivated the execution, which will be investigated by the Homicide Department of the Civil Police.
> 
> Camera images from the City Hall's monitoring service will be analyzed in an attempt to identify the vehicle used in the crime and the killers. The body was removed by the Instituto Médico Legal (IML)
