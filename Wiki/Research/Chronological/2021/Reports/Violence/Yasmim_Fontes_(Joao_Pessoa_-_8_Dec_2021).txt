Yasmim Fontes was stabbed by a client in João Pessoa, Paraíba (Brazil) on 8 Dec 2021.

She was 23.


 

> Trans woman is stabbed to death by alleged client, in João Pessoa
> Per redaction -December 9, 20210707
> 
> The trans woman Yasmim Fontes, 23, was stabbed to death at dawn this Thursday (9), in the neighborhood of Manaira, in João Pessoa.
> 
> Preliminary information from the Military Police, which was passed on by other call girls, Yasmim was on Av. Édson Ramalho when he went out with a client.
> 
> For minutes later, the victim came running half-naked and bloodied. She fell and could not resist her injuries. Yasmin was a native of Campina Grande.




> Transvestite is stabbed to death on Avenida Edson Ramalho, in João Pessoa
> Victim worked as a call girl and was approached on Avenida João Maurício, Orla de Manaíra
> 
> A transvestite was stabbed to death at dawn this Thursday (9) on Avenida Edson Ramalho, in João Pessoa.
> 
> According to an investigation by TV Correio , the victim worked as a program girl in the locality. She was reportedly approached by what looked like a customer on Avenida João Maurício, on the edge of Praia de Manaíra. After some conversation, they walked to Edson Ramalho Avenue and stopped in a land next to a container, a place usually used for sexual activities.
> 
> There, the man started to attack the transvestite. She managed to run for a few meters but could not resist her injuries. The man fled towards the beach. Security camera footage recorded the moment he rushes past, with something in his hand that looks like a purse. The transvestite's purse was found in the vicinity where the crime took place.
> 
> The Civil Police was called and will investigate whether the crime was a robbery or whether the perpetrator was also motivated by homophobia.
