Catalina Santos Arévalo was stabbed in Atizapán de Zaragoza, Estado de México (Mexico) on 21 Aug 2021.

She was 23.



> State of Mexico.- Members of the LGBT population reported the death of a trans woman and activist for the rights of the community, in Atizapán de Zaragoza.
> 
> According to the first reports, the trans woman identified as Catalina Santos Arévalo was the victim of a hate crime at dawn on August 21.
> 
> "We regret the fact that not a single ambulance arrived to take his vital signs."
> 
> 'EMBAJADADOR', LGBT ACTIVIST.
> 
> CATALINA SANTOS ARÉVALO (FACEBOOK)
> The incident occurred on calle Lenin, corner with Descartes, Tepalcapa neighborhood. The trans woman would have been attacked around 02:00 in the morning.
> 
> Catalina Santos Arévalo died at the age of 23 after receiving several stab wounds and a cut in the jugular . His body was abandoned in the Atizapán de Zaragoza colony .
> 
> "More than 3 hours have passed and the experts have not arrived for the removal of the body or any agent of the State of Mexico prosecutor's office"
> 
> 'EMBAJADADOR', LGBT ACTIVIST.
> 
> CATALINA SANTOS ARÉVALO (FACEBOOK)
> According to versions that circulate on social networks and by some witnesses, the activist and trans woman was persecuted for several minutes by her attackers .
> 
> The body of Catalina Santos Arévalo stood in the rain for more than four hours until relatives learned of the fact. The emergency services never arrived .
> 
> 
> They demand justice for a trans woman stabbed in Atizapán de Zaragoza
> Activists from the LGBT population of Atizapán de Zaragoza and the country in general, demand justice for Catalina Santos Arévalo , a trans woman stabbed in the Tepalcapa neighborhood.
> 
> Through Facebook, the activist 'Ambassador' (as he calls himself) called on the government of Atizapán de Zaragoza , as well as the state prosecutor's office, to carry out the pertinent investigation.
> 
> "I strongly condemn the murder of our comrade Catalina Santos Arevalo, homophobia and transphobia must be eradicated from the roots of our country"
> 
> STATEMENT (FACEBOOK)
> For its part, the Lgbtttiq Atizapán Population Committee of Zaragoza , lamented the death of the trans woman and asked the authorities to clarify her death , so that she does not go unpunished.
> 
> In the same way, they urge the authorities to activate the gender alert in Atizapán de Zaragoza , a municipality in the State of Mexico, due to the transfeminicides of the last year.
> 
> At SDPnoticias, we regret the death of Catalina Santos Arévalo and wish her family members a speedy recovery, as well as justice for the victim.
