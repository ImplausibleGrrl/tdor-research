> When I was attending a grocery store, "gay" was shot dead
> The bodies of the bartender José Luis Castellanos Amador and Hugo Renán Mejía (inserted photos), yesterday were removed from the capital's morgue by their relatives.
> 
> Gang members shot and killed a member of the Lesbian, Gay, Bisexual, Transsexual and Intersex (LGBTI) community on Monday night, when the victim was attending a grocery store, in the La Era neighborhood, northeastern sector of Tegucigalpa.
> 
> He is Hugo Renán Mejía, better known among his friends as “Karencita”, who preliminary reports indicate that he was inside the business, when heavily armed unknown men arrived and without a word they attacked him with bullets from the window of the establishment.
> 
> As a result of several shots received, Mejía died immediately at the scene. Agents of the National Police moved to the site to protect the scene, while members of Forensic Medicine carried out the lifting and transfer of the body to the capital's morgue.
