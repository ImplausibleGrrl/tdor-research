Hevelyn Montine was shot dead alongside the GO-174 highway in Rio Verde, Goiás (Brazil) on 4 Sep 2021.

She was 30.


> A transvestite was found dead in Rio Verde, southwest of Goiás, this Saturday (4/9). The body of 30-year-old Hevelyn Montine had three gunshot marks and was lying in a thicket on the edge of the GO-174 highway. According to G1 Goiás, the victim was dressed only in a lingerie upper and a high-heeled sandal. Hevelyn's identification was made by the Criminalistics Institute.
> 
> The Scientific Police carried out an investigation at the place where the body was found. The Civil Police of Goiás is investigating the case and trying to find the person responsible for the murder.



> Goiânia – A transvestite was found dead in Rio Verde , southwest of Goiás, this Saturday (4/9). The body of 30-year-old Hevelyn Montine had three gunshot wounds and was lying in a thicket in her underwear on the side of the GO-174 highway.
> 
> According to G1 Goiás , the victim was dressed in a lingerie upper and a high-heeled sandal. Hevelyn was undocumented and her identification was made by fingerprint, by the Institute of Identification of the Civild Police and Goiás.
> 
> The Scientific Police carried out an investigation at the place where the body was found. The Civil Police of Goiás is investigating the case and trying to find the person responsible for the murder.
> 
> Friends of Hevelyn, who was born in Santa Helena de Goiás, paid tribute on social networks and asked for justice and punishment for those responsible for the crime.
> 
> “God's Justice does not fail. Rest in peace,” wrote a friend.
