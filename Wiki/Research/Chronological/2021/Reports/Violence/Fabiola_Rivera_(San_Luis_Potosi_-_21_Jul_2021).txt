Trans woman murdered in aesthetics in Valle Dorado
Metropolis 07/21/2021 14:27 Writing San Luis Potosí Updated 14:30


Early this Wednesday, July 21, 2021, the murder of a trans woman in the Valle Dorado neighborhood was reported to the emergency lines. Fabiola “N” , 43 years old, was attacked with a knife inside her home, located on calle Perla.

The investigations of the Investigation Police determined the legal identity of Fabiola “N”. Upon discovering that she was a transsexual woman, the case was notified to the Special Prosecutor for Human Rights, because it could be a hate crime towards a person from the LGBT + community in the capital of Potosí.

Expert Services personnel were in charge of processing the scene and transferring the body of Fabiola “N” to Semele where the mechanics of the injuries will be established, as well as the cause of death. In her home, where the trans woman worked as a stylist , several signs of the crime were found.

Once the autopsy is completed, the body of Fabiola “N” will be returned to her relatives. Agents from the Scientific Visitor's Office and the Human Rights Special Prosecutor's Office work in coordination to find those responsible for this possible transfemicide.


SLP. - Following up on the proceedings and investigations carried out by the State Attorney General's Office (FGESLP) for a violent death in a home in the Valle Dorado neighborhood of the Capital city, on the early morning of July 21, 2021, it has been determined that the The victim was a person who identified himself with a female gender identity before the civil registry.

This 43-year-old person was found lifeless in an armchair with injuries caused by a knife in a house set up as a cosmetician on Perla Street; his body was taken to the Legislative Medical Service (Semele) for a legal necropsy and to know exactly what caused his death.

Faced with this criminal death, the Office of the Special Prosecutor for Human Rights works together with the Office of the Vice Prosecutor to clarify the facts and find who or who are responsible for them. 

Meanwhile, the necessary procedures are carried out to return the body to his family.

The Investigative Police carries out the corresponding procedures and conducts field work, as well as interviews with possible witnesses of what happened to obtain information that supports this folder that was opened.

Personnel from the Scientific Vice Prosecutor's Office processed the crime scene and collected various evidence to support the investigations of this case
