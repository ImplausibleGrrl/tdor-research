Transvestite body is found wrapped in a sheet indoors in União dos Palmares
According to the police, there was a lot of blood at the site and a broken bench beside the bed. This is the second crime against homosexuals recorded this year in the city.
Posted on: 07/08/21 at 11:17 AM By Gustavo Lopes | BR104

2nd BPM military at the crime scene |  © Rayanne Rodrigues/BR104
2nd BPM military at the crime scene | © Rayanne Rodrigues/BR104
A man identified as Givanildo Alexandre da Silva, 33 years old, was found dead this Saturday morning (8/7) inside his own residence on Rua Alonso Costa, in the Roberto Correia de Araújo neighborhood, in the municipality of União dos Palmares , in the Zone of the State Forest.

According to first information, the body of the victim, also known locally as Gil, was found in the room above the bed and wrapped in a white sheet. There was still a lot of blood in the place and a broken bench beside the furniture.

Military personnel from the 2nd Military Police Battalion were called to the scene and isolated the area. In a report on the BR104 portal, the commander of the Task Force garrison said that the man lived alone, and that after the perpetrator had committed the crime, he would have closed the property's door with a chain.

Also according to the police, the family informed that Alexandre was a transvestite and that the night before he had drunk alcohol. Teams from the Criminalist (IC) and Legal Medicine (IML) institutes were also called in to carry out the investigation and the removal of the body, respectively.

Givanildo's case will be investigated by the Civil Police of União dos Palmares.
