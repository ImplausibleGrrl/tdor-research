Itu dos Santos was shot in the street in the Massaranduba neighborhood of Salvador, Bahia (Brazil) on the night of 10 Mar 2021. She died at the scene.

She was 30.


A transvestite known as 'Itu do Uruguai', was shot dead on a public road at Travessa São Francisco, in the neighborhood of Massaranduba, in Salvador, on Wednesday night (10). An agglomeration was formed around the body, as she could not resist the injuries and died on the spot. 

The Civil Police (PC) said the victim was identified as Nailton Evan dos Santos and was only 30 years old. 

The authorship and motivation are unknown, the PC will investigate the case.
