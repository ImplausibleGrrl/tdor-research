> Transvestite is brutally murdered in the center of Manaus

> Transvestite is brutally murdered in the center of Manaus
>
> Manaus / AM - A transvestite, identified so far as 'Joelma', was murdered at dawn this Saturday (24th), at around 1 am, on Rua 10 de Julho with Lobo D'Almada, in the Centro district, in the south of Manaus.
> 
> According to information provided by the team from the 24th Companhia Interativa Comunitária (Cicom), which answered the incident, the police officers were doing a routine patrol when they saw a body lying on the floor at the address mentioned.
> 
> When they decided to get out of the car and get closer, they realized it was a possible homicide. The police quickly dispatched the team from the Mobile Emergency Service (Samu), who arrived at the scene and only confirmed the death.
> 
> At the scene, the law of silence followed, but the police had information that possibly the transvestite had been beaten with several blows of blows before she even died.
> 
> The Department of Scientific Technical Police (DPTC) was at the scene doing the forensic work on the body where it found that the victim was indeed murdered with blows, because a stick with blood marks was found at the scene.
> 
> The Specialized Homicide and Kidnapping Police Station (DEHS) was also at the scene to begin the investigations and then the transvestite's body was removed by the staff of the Instituto Médico Legal (IML).
 


> An unidentified transvestite was beaten to death in the early hours of Saturday (24), at Rua Lobo D'Almada, in the center of Manaus.
> 
> According to the Specialized Homicide and Kidnapping Police Station (DEHS), the crime would have occurred around midnight. Police officers said homeless people earlier said the victim had fallen out with another person in the region.
> 
> Soon after, when she was going up the road at the junction with Rua 10 de Julho, there was the aggression. When analyzing the crime scene, the investigators identified that the blows were in the region of the head.
> 
> But despite the information from homeless people about who the victim was, the crime had no witnesses, which makes identification difficult.
> 
> However, the police are already studying requesting images from the security cameras of businesses in the region to try to find out who committed the crime.
> 
> The transvestite's body was taken to the Instituto Médico Legal (IML). But, because it is unidentified, it will not be released yet. An investigation will be carried out by DEHS to try to solve the crime.
