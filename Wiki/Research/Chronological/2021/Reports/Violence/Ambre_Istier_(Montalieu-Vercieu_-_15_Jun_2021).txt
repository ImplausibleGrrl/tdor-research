> A 50-year-old man was indicted and remanded in custody Thursday in Grenoble for the murder of a transgender woman killed by gunshot at her home Tuesday in Isère.
> 
> The 49-year-old victim was born male before changing marital status. The suspect, a friend, was arrested on Wednesday.
> 
> The Bourgoin-Jallieu prosecutor's office had then indicated that this woman "wished to end her life and had requested the help of the person currently in police custody", according to a note left at her home in Montalieu-Vercieu, in the north of the department.
> 
> 
> A gun shot
> Blows to the head were initially mentioned to explain the death but an examination of the corpse finally revealed a shooting by gun, reports Le Dauphiné Libéré.
> 
> Before the investigating judge, "the person confirmed his first statements made in police custody, during which he indicated that he had acquired a firearm at the request of the deceased, who wished to end her life and had requested the help of his friend to do this, "said the floor on Thursday evening.
> 
> "He had to kill himself"
> "He had accepted and had to commit suicide himself at the end with the same weapon, which he finally gave up doing", according to the same source which adds that the defendant, unemployed and under guardianship, has already been convicted twice for domestic violence.

> By Redaction Grenoble
> Published on 16 June 21 at 16:07 
> 
> He is suspected of being the author of the beatings observed on the lifeless body of a transgender woman who wanted to kill herself. A man was taken into custody on Wednesday June 16, 2021 in Isère for assassination, said the Bourgoin-Jailleu prosecutor's office .
> 
> The corpse of this 49-year-old woman, born male before changing civil status, was found on Tuesday, June 15, 2021 at her home in Montalieu-Vercieu, a town located north of Isère on the banks of the Rhône, has announced in a press release Dietlind Baudouin , prosecutor of Bourgoin-Jailleu, confirming information from the regional daily Le Dauphiné Libéré .
> 
> Blows to the face
> Investigators quickly concluded that his death was caused by blows to the face and they arrested, Wednesday morning, a 50-year-old man, friend of the victim but "without any love or conjugal relationship," said the prosecutor.
> 
> His statements and a note left by the victim "make it possible to understand that (the victim) wanted to end her life and had requested the help of the person currently in custody", writes the magistrate.
> 
> "The gesture which led to the death being obviously premeditated", the 50-year-old man was placed in police custody for murder on Wednesday morning, while waiting to be presented to an examining magistrate in Grenoble.
> 
> An autopsy is due on Thursday.
