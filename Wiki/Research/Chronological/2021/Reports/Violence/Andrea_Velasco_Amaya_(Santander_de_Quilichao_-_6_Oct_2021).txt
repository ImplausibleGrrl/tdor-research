A trans woman identified as Andrea Velasco Amaya was attacked with a firearm and at the moment the whereabouts of the person responsible for the homicide in the municipality of Santander de Quilichao, in northern Cauca, is unknown .

Inhabitants of this population reported that the victim, a member of the LGTBI community, was found seriously injured in the Transquilichao sector.

After the attack, she was taken to a clinic in Cali, but died due to the severity of her injuries.

According to the first versions, the woman was attacked with several shots in the middle of a street fight.

"We have information that she is a street dweller, a consumer, and that she had problems on the street with someone, " said the Municipal Government Secretary, Iván Carvajal.

The Technical Investigation Body, CTI, of the Office of the Attorney General of the Nation assumed the urgent actions to clarify the case.




As Andrea Velasco Amaya (Wilmer) was identified the member of the LGBTIQ community, victim of a knife attack in the early morning of Wednesday, October 6, 2021, at the height of 6th Street between 13 and 14 races, the sector where the Buses of the Transquilichao company of that North Caucana town.

The facts were confirmed when they requested the presence of the Police in the sector, where Andrea Velasco Amaya lay seriously injured.

"People realized that the citizen was practically agonizing, hence they asked for support from the quadrant to facilitate the transfer to the local hospital and then referred to the Valle del Lili clinic due to the severity of the injuries recorded," explained bystanders and journalists who were on the site.

Despite this, in the afternoon hours of the same Wednesday the death of the young woman was confirmed.

Apparently, the events would have unleashed in the middle of a street brawl, which took place in that sector. RIP
