According to information received, Siate, PMPR and GMSJP teams were triggered on the night of Monday (06), to address a situation of multiple Firearms (FAF) victim, in the Swiss Garden, Quissana neighborhood in São Paulo Joseph of the Pinhais.

Arriving at the scene in front of a residence on Rosa Maranho Bobato Street almost corner of Ernesto Montanarin Street, rescue teams couldn't do anything, as the girl couldn't resist the multiple firearm shooting and came to death on the scene.

The girl has been identified as 21-year-old Andrielly Priscila de Melo.

According to information raised by De Olho São José, two bicycle gunmen drove through the scene and made at least 10 firearm shots at the victim and then fled taking overlooked sense.

Motivation and authorship are unknown, no one has seen anything and no one knows anything.

GMSJP teams isolate the site until the arrival of the Criminalistica team, which was on the scene and made expertise in the execution scene.

The investigation of this murder here in the municipality is borne by investigators of the Civil Police Regional Police Station.
The Legal Medical Institute (IML) of Curitiba has been triggered and collected Andrielly's body.
BY MARCIO / EYE SAINT JOS É



Andrielly Priscila de Melo, 21, was hit with several shots from a firearm this Monday night (6), on Rua Rosa Marinho Bobato, in the Quissana neighborhood, in São José dos Pinhais. 

Local residents heard the shots and found the victim lying in the middle of the street. 

According to the Municipal Guard, the system did not show any passing of Andrielly by the police, however, according to the Military Police, on November 18, she was taken to the police station in São José dos Pinhais, suspected of drug trafficking. 

Police officers found portions of marijuana, cocaine and crack at her home in the Jardim Itallia neighborhood. Andrielly informed the chief that he was just saving the narcotics for a dealer.
