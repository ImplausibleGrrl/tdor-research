Brianna Kamila Espinosa Sanchez was a transgender woman, stylist of the town of Yumbo, last February 15 she was attacked at her hairdresser with a firearm by a subject who shot her in the head from the back leaving her seriously injured, Kamila was urgently moved to a Cali City Clinic fighting for her life, unfortunately she couldn't hold it any longer and died in a surgery looking to extract the projectile embedded in her head...
