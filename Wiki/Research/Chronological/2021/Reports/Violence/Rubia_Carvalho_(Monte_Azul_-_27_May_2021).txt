The body of Rubia Carvalho was found in Monte Azul, Minas Gerais (Brazil) on 27 May 2021.

She was 23 and had been missing for a week. A suspect has been arrested. #SayHerName #TransLivesMatter #TDoR



> YOUNG TRANS WHO DISAPPEARED IN AMOUNT AZUL HAS BEEN MURDERED TRAVESTI WOULD BE AUTHOR (check pictures of the prison)
> 
> Civil police arrested on Thursday's night (27), a man appointed as author of homicide against a young trans who had been missing from the city of Monte Azul since last weekend. The 23-year-old was spotted at a gas station and was since missing. According to the Civil Police this man who would be a transvestite from a neighbouring city, would have lured the young trans to her residence and then killed her with knife stroke at the time of her neck soon afterwards would have spawned the body in a rural area of the city of Monte Azul, being body found this Thursday by police. 
> 
> Police arrived at the transvestite after checking images at the gas station where the young lady was looking for customers. In these images, the victim appears with the transvestite appointed as the author of the crime. The information is that the transvestite was arrested by civilian police officers and confessed to the whole story. 
> 
> Motivation of crime would be a dispute over customer dispute for sexual programs. The discovery of the case caused revolt in the population of Monte Azul. During the transvestite's arrest by the Civil Police, he had to be taken due to the demonstration of dozens of people at the civil police station door. The information is that the transvestite should be brought to Janaúba City. Check out the exact moment when a Civil Police car leaves the police station under protest from most residents.
