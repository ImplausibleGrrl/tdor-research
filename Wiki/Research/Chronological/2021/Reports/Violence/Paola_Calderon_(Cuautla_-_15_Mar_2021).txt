Paola Calderon was beaten and shot in her home in Cuautla, Morelos (Mexico) on 15 Mar 2021.

She was 24. #SayHerName #TransLivesMatter #TDoR



Paola,  a  transsexual girl,  was shot and  beaten to death  in her home by a subject who allegedly hired her to have sex in the Año de Juárez neighborhood,  Cuautla, Morelos .

On Monday around 9 a.m., a woman arrived at her home and upon entering one of the rooms she found her daughter unconscious and with blood stains, for which she requested an ambulance, but after the arrival of paramedics they confirmed that  already had passed away.

Authorities arrived at the site to cordon off the area. Experts found a .25 mm caliber percussion shell   and pipes with drugs.


The mother stated that her daughter was called  José Juan  ,  24 years  old, but he was known as  La Paola.

He said that his daughter was transsexual and was dedicated to offering her sexual services through Facebook, serving her clients within the same home. From what he deduced that one of them was the murderer.
