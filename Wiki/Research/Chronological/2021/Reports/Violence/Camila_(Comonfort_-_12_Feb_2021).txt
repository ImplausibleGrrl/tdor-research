Camila was shot in Comonfort, Guanajuato (Mexico) on 12 Feb 2021.

She was 29.



Trans woman executed in Comonfort
COMONFORT, GTO. FEBRUARY 13, 2021.- Camila, a well-known transsexual woman from Comonfort, was shot to death on Friday night.

It was last night when Comonfort Public Security learned of an armed attack on calle 20 de Noviembre in the Guadalupe neighborhood.

The body of 29-year-old Camila was found on the sidewalk, who, according to witnesses, was well known in the area.

According to the investigations, minutes before the victim was standing on the sidewalk, when a vehicle arrived from where they shot him.

Camila fell and died almost instantly from the injuries she presented.

His body was picked up by Semefo and taken to San Miguel de Allende for a legal necropsy. 

The experts raised several percussion shells at the scene of the crime.
