A trans man identified only as Bielzinho Costa, had his house invaded and was shot dead around 20:00 this Wednesday (29), at the Miguel Arraes residential complex, in Timon (MA).

 According to information from the 11th Battalion of the Military Police, witnesses reported that two armed men arrived and entered the victim's residence. They fired firearms and then fled.

A team from the Mobile Emergency Care Service (SAMU) was called to the scene and found that the victim was already lifeless. The suspects have not been identified and Timon's Civil Police will investigate the crime.



Transsexual is shot and executed inside residence in Timon
The case will be investigated by the Homicide Police, but so far, there is no information on who would have committed the crime.

A transsexual identified as Beatriz da Silva Costa, better known as 'Bielzinho', was shot several times on Wednesday night, December 29, in the Miguel Arraes complex, in the municipality of Timon-MA.

According to information from the people passed on to the Military Police, the crime took place around 8:30 pm. The victim was at his home when three men arrived on foot, invaded the place and fired shots at him, who still tried to flee, but was hit.

A team from the Mobile Emergency Care Service (SAMU) was even called to the scene, but 'bielzinho' had already died. The Forensic Medical Institute (IML) performed the removal of the body. The case will be investigated by the Homicide Police, but so far, there is no information on who would have committed the crime.
