Transvestite is stabbed to death by stepfather while trying to defend mother from aggression in Piauí

A young woman known by the social name “Lupita” (Lucas Moraes da Silva), was killed with knife blows while trying to defend her mother from aggression on Thursday night (07/01) in Bairro Liberdade, in the municipality of São João do Arraial, in the North of Piauí. The main suspect in the crime is his stepfather known as Luciano. The information is from  Revista AZ.

According to preliminary information, the suspect was arguing with Lupita's mother, who has not yet been identified, when he drew a knife and began to attack her. Lupita tried to defend her mother from aggression and was also stabbed, but she couldn't resist her injuries and died on the spot. The suspect's companion was brought to Dr. Júlio Hartman State Hospital in Esperantina, with serious wounds from the bladed weapon. She was attended by a medical team and is at risk.

The Military Police continues to investigate the suspect.
