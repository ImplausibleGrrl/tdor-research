Gala Ocampo was shot in Amacuzac, Morelos (Mexico) on 18 Jan 2021.

She was 28. #SayHerName #TransLivesMatter #TDoR


Amacuzac, MORELOS.-  A transsexual known as “Gala” was found dead yesterday morning at the entrance to the El Sureño neighborhood. Her body had at least two bullet wounds. 

According to police sources, the deceased was officially identified as Josué David Ocampo Álvarez, 28. 

Shortly before 08:30 hours they reported to the authorities that a person was lying on the side of the local Coahuixtla-Cajones highway. 

Paramedics who answered the call could only confirm that "Gala" no longer had vital signs, so police cordoned off the crime scene and reported it to the South
West Regional Prosecutor's Office . 

The experts packed a couple of casings and the removal of the corpse was in charge of Semefo to start the investigations, since the motive for the homicide is unknown at the moment.


Transgender woman shot to death in Amacuzac
Written by Erika López Islas
A transgender woman was shot to death last Sunday morning, on the local highway Coahuixtla-Cajones, in Amacuzac.

According to sources consulted by La Unión de Morelos, the police received the report, around 08:30 hours, about the location of a body at the entrance to the El Sureño neighborhood, in the town of Coahuixtla, in the aforementioned municipality. .

In that place, the officers found dead who was identified as David "N"; He had gunshot wounds to the head and chest.

Subsequently, the personnel of the Forensic Medical Service (Semefo) carried out the lifting of the body of the transgender woman, who was known as Gala; He was wearing blue jeans, a green shirt and sandals.


SADNESS AND INDIGNATION IN THE LGBTTTI COMMUNITY BECAUSE OF THE VIOLENT DEATH OF GALA OCAMPO

Colectivos de la Diversidad Sexual of different municipalities of #Morelos, have spoken out for justice and clarification of the murder of Gala Ocampo, member of the Sexual Diversity Committee in Morelos and inhabitant of the municipality of #Amacuzac, who was killed on this day; her colleagues and friends believe it was a hate crime on the basis of sexual preference as Gala Ocampo fought for the rights of transgender people, an issue that has been 'frozen' on the legislative agenda for years.
