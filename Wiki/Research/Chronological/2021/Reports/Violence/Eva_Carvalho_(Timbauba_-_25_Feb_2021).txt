On the night of Thursday (25) a young man was murdered at Rua Maria Iraci Arruda de Albuquerque, Vila dos Quinhentos, in Timbaúba. Victim Everton Alves de Carvalho, 17 years old, known as “Eva”, suffered several firearm shots, mainly in the head, legs and chest. According to the victim's mother, Lídice Alves de Carvalho, the victim was at her home, around 10 pm, when she received a phone call, answering the call, going to the place and informing that she would be back soon. The mother even heard the gunfire. The victim was a drug user, according to the mother. According to information, the author of the firearm shots would be “Victor do Cruzeiro”. The victim's body went to the IML in Recife.
