====== Badega (Salvador - 31 Jul 2021) ======

A young woman known as “Badega” was murdered on Saturday night (31) in the Alto do Cabrito neighborhood, a railroad suburb of Salvador. According to information from residents, the victim was a transvestite and was hit by several shots, on Rua F, next to the workshop in “Dolinha”.

According to information from the Police, agents were called upon by people, but when they arrived at the scene of the crime, they found the victim dead. The PMs isolated the area and called the Department of Homicide and Protection of Persons (DHPP), which arranged for the investigation and the removal of the body. 

The Civil Police will investigate the authorship and motivation of the murder.
