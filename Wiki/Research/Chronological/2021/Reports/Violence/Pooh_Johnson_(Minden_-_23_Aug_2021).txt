Pooh Johnson was shot dead inside a vehicle in Shreveport, Louisiana (USA) on 23 Aug 2021.
USA

She was 25 and a makeup artist who also went by the name of Titanizer Mua.


> Minden, Louisiana - Brennan “Pooh” Johnson was shot to death inside a vehicle behind a home in the 300 block of 74th St. She was found in the early morning of Monday, August 23rd, 2021. She was also known as Titanizer Mua which was her business brand for her glamour-centered make-up artist business.
