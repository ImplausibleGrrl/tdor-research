Transvestite is executed by criminals and friend taken in serious condition to hospital in Mesquita Metropolitan Region of Rio.
January 15, 2021


One transvestite died and another was injured after an execution on Friday afternoon (15), in Mesquita Metropolitan Region of Rio.

According to police they were identified as Yasmin and Leticia Lessa, the crime took place in the late afternoon on a dead-end street in the neighborhood.

Also according to the PM, Yasmim was hit in the head by a criminal, she was riding a motorcycle and her friend was on the rump when she was hit in the chest.

Leticia was taken to the nearest hospital in serious condition, while Yasmin was removed to the IML of São Gonçalo.

It is not yet known what motivated the murder.

The investigations will be in charge of the Homicide Police of Baixada Fluminense.
