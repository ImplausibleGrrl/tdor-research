Wanda Soraya/Soraya De La Fuente was shot in La Plata, Buenos Aires (Argentina) on 19 Jun 2021.



> A trans woman was shot dead in El Mondongo
> It happened at dawn on diagonal 73 and calle 3. The perpetrators are at large.
> June 19
> 
> A trans woman was shot and killed in El Mondongo . The crime occurred in the early hours of the morning, at diagonal 73 and calle 3 and at the moment there are no detainees.
> 
> According to police sources, the victim was a sex worker and was in the red zone of La Plata when she was approached by three individuals who were driving a gray Ford Ka car. One of them pulled out a gun, began to make several shots and ended up hitting a bullet in the body of the trans woman.
> 
> A taxi driver rushed the victim to Gutiérrez Hospital, with a gunshot wound to the chest , but doctors were unable to save his life and she died minutes later.
> 
> At the moment the causes of the brutal attack are unknown, so DDI La Plata personnel are carrying out the investigation. Likewise, agents at the Novena police station carried out a lockdown operation in the area, but could not find those involved in the incident . UFI Nº 7 intervenes in the case in turn.



> A trans woman was killed today by two shots to the chest in the so-called "Red Zone" of the Buenos Aires city of La Plata, and for the crime they sought to identify the driver of a vehicle that escaped, informed judicial and police sources.
> 
> The incident occurred after 3 in the morning in the area of ​​Diagonal 73 and 64, when, for reasons that are the subject of investigation, a man aboard a Ford K vehicle started a discussion with the victim and shot him in at least Two occasions.
> 
> Police and judicial sources informed Télam that the attacker fled and the trans woman, whose name was Soraya De La Fuente , was seriously injured.
> 
> A taxi driver who was circulating in the area took her urgently to the Ricardo Gutiérrez Hospital in La Plata. According to reports, despite the efforts of the doctors, the victim died after a few minutes due to the severity of the injuries.
> 
> The crime is being investigated by the prosecutor Virginia Bravo, head of the Functional Instruction Unit (UFI) 7 of the La Plata Judicial Department.
