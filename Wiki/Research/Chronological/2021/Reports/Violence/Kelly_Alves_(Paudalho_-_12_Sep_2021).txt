Kelly Alves (named as "Dandara" in some reports) was shot in Paudalho, Pernambuco (Brazil) on 12 Sep 2021.



> A trans woman was shot dead in the Entra Apulso community, in the Bobocão allotment, in the municipality of Paudalho , in the Zona da Mata Norte of Pernambuco. The victim's body was found inside a house, on Monday (13), and the crime was confirmed by the Civil Police this Tuesday (14).
> 
> The victim was identified as Kelly Alves. Witnesses reported that they heard gunshots in the house where she was on Sunday night (12). The next day, her body was found and the Military Police was called for the occurrence.
> 
> The PM reported that a team from the 2nd Battalion was called to isolate the crime scene. The Criminalistics Institute went to the residence and Kelly Alves' body was taken to the Institute of Forensic Medicine (IML), in the center of Recife , to be examined.
> 
> Kelly's age and profession were not disclosed. The Civil Police reported that the trans woman was hit by more than one shot from a firearm.
> 
> The suspect in the crime was identified only as Bruno, whose relationship with the victim was not disclosed by the police. Until the last update of this report, no one was arrested.
> 
> The murder is investigated by the Department of Homicide and Protection of Persons (DHPP) of the Civil Police. "The investigations were initiated and continue until the complete elucidation of the crime," the corporation said in a statement.




> Trans woman is gunned down in Paudalho, in the Zona da Mata of Pernambuco
According to the police, the crime took place in the community of Entra Apulso, in the neighborhood of Bobocão
>
> A trans woman, identified as Dandara, was shot dead last Sunday (12) in Paudalho, in the Zona da Mata of Pernambuco. According to the police, the crime took place in the community of Entra Apulso, in the neighborhood of Bobocão. 
>
> The victim was shot several times in the head with a firearm and witnesses point out that she was already found dead. Authorship and motivation of the crime are investigated by the Civil Police. 



> With a deep perforation, at the level of the left shoulder, the body of the trans woman, Kelly Alves, was found lifeless this morning (13), inside a residence, located in the community of Entra Apulso, in Bobocão, in Paudalho.
> 
> According to neighbors at the crime scene, last night, the noise of gunfire and shouts were heard ordering some people to run. This morning the victim's body was located, where the Military Police was called.
> 
> In the community, no one knew the victim. The body had been collected for the IML, in Recife, without identification, being later recognized by family members.
> 
> The owner of the property informed the Civil Police that she had rented the house to a man, identified only by Bruno, who was not located.
> 
> The motivation and authorship will be investigated by the 47th DP.
