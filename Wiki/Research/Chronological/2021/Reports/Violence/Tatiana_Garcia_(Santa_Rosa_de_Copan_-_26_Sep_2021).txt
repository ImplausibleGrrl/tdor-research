COPÁN, HONDURAS . Yesterday, Sunday, September 26, the lifeless body of "Tatiana" was found, a transvestite woman who lived in Santa Rosa de Copán, whom unknown subjects stabbed inside her home.

This person also went by the name of Erick Humberto García, 32, who was well known for being an active member of the LGBT community. He lived in the Díaz Valenzuela neighborhood of Santa Rosa de Copán. And according to statements from his relatives, he did not answer phone calls, this being the first sign of concern.

Preliminary versions established that "Tatiana" died on Saturday night, but it was until Sunday that her family found her body.

Tatiana's friends say goodbye to her through social media posts.

Currently, the police authorities began to investigate anyone linked to the tragic event, as well as those who used to hang out with the victim. On the other hand, the body remains in the Santa Rosa de Copán morgue.

In social networks, many people expressed their regret and disgust at the atrocious death. “In this country they kill people like nothing else and everything goes unpunished, they never solve it”, “Life is worth less and less in Honduras”, “May she rest in peace, I knew her, she was very helpful. God strengthen the soul of his mother ”, are some comments.

“There are more and more deaths in Santa Rosa … how sad”, “Honduras, something has to be done. There is a great wave of discrimination. I complain about my country for this, it is not possible for it to remain unpunished ”, were others.
