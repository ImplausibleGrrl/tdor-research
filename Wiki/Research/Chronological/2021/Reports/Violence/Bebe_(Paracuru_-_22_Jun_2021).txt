Transvestite is stabbed to death in Paracuru, west coast of Ceará
Victim's body was found in the rural area of the city; according to one resident, there was a knife stuck in her face.
By G1 CE


A trans woman named Bebê was stabbed in Paracuru, Ceará (Brazil) on 22 Jun 2021.

She was 29.
 




> The transvestite known as Bebê, murdered with a knife in the city of Paracuru , on the west coast of Ceará, was killed by transphobia, according to police chief Sâmella Saraiva, head of the Metropolitan Police Station of Paracuru, who is investigating the case. On the afternoon of Wednesday (23), a man was arrested and a teenager was apprehended by the Civil Police, on suspicion of participating in the crime.
> 
> “We collected traces and testimonies, which confirmed the presence of suspects at the crime scene. The two showed coolness when talking about the crime, especially the teenager, who told details about the action. In addition, the body was found with injuries resulting from blunt and sharp objects, after a discussion between those involved and the victim”, reports the delegate.
> 
> According to Sâmella, during the investigations, the agents received anonymous reports about the whereabouts of Manoel Moreira Freire Neto, 22, with a history of theft, and a 16-year-old teenager, with criminal offenses for vehicle theft, reception and illegal possession of a weapon of fire.
> 
> In the search for the suspects, the transvestite's bicycle was found in the teenager's residence. A third suspect, aged 19, who has already been identified, is wanted by the police.
> 
> The shoes used by the detained pair were retained by the Civil Police and will be analyzed by the Forensic Investigation of the State of Ceará (Pefoce).
> 
> Manoel was booked in flagrante delicto for aggravated homicide for the shameful motive, due to the evidence of transphobic conduct and the impossibility of defending the victim. In the same terms, an infraction similar to the homicide against the adolescent was registered.
> 
> The Civil Police will deepen the investigations to locate the whereabouts of the third suspect and later the conclusion of the police investigation.
> 
> The body of the transvestite, known in the region as Baby, was found this Tuesday morning (22), in the locality of Riacho Doce. According to information from a resident who prefers not to be identified, she was found with marks of various aggressions and with a knife stuck in her face.
> 
> The Secretariat of Public Security (SSPDS), the victim had injuries resulting from blunt and sharp objects.
> 
> Teams from PCCE, PMCE and Forensic Investigation of the State of Ceará (Pefoce) were called in and carried out the first surveys on the spot.
> 
> The resident stated that the local population is perplexed by the murder of the transvestite. She claims that the victim had a good relationship with everyone and did not appear to have enemies.
> 
> “She was a good person and everyone knew him. Always well connected with people. I talked to everyone. We are not believing anyone can harm the Baby. I cannot understand how anyone could have the courage to do this to him. Very sad and revolting”, he said.



> A 22-year-old young man was arrested on suspicion of participating in the murder of the transvestite known as Bebê, in Paracuru, on the east coast of the state, on the afternoon of Wednesday, 23, the day after the crime. A 16-year-old teenager was apprehended for an offense analogous to murder. Through anonymous reports, the civil and military police found Manoel Moreira Freire Neto, 22, with a history of theft, and the teenager, with offenses such as vehicle theft, reception and illegal possession of a firearm. They were taken to the Paracuru Municipal Police Station.
> 
> According to the Police, there are signs that the behavior of those captured is related to transphobia. The searches were initiated after the victim's death was registered, on Tuesday morning, 22. According to police chief Sâmella Saraiva, head of the Metropolitan Police Station of Paracuru, traces and statements were collected that confirmed the presence of the suspects at the scene of the crime. “The two showed coolness when talking about the crime, especially the teenager, who told details about the action. In addition, the body was found with injuries resulting from blunt and sharp objects, after a discussion between those involved and the victim”, he says.



06/22/2021 13:32 Updated 2 hours ago

A transvestite was murdered with a knife in Paracuru, 87 kilometers from Fortaleza. The body of the transvestite, known in the region as Bebê, was found this Tuesday morning (22), in the locality of Riacho Doce. According to information from a resident who prefers not to be identified, she was found with marks of various aggressions and with a knife stuck in her face.

The Public Security Secretariat (SSPDS) informed, through a note, that the Civil and Military Police are carrying out investigations in order to identify the author of the murder. According to preliminary information, the body of the victim, who was 29 years old, was located this Tuesday morning in the Riacho Doce neighborhood.

According to the folder, the victim had injuries resulting from blunt and sharp objects. Teams from PCCE, PMCE and Forensic Investigation of the State of Ceará (Pefoce) were called in and carried out the first surveys on the spot. The case is being investigated by the Municipal Police of Paracuru.
