> They find the stabbed body of a man inside his home in Naucalpan - La Prensa - Noticias Mexico
> 05/29/2021
> 
> This morning, with brutal blows to the body and a deep stab in the back , the corpse of an alleged transvestite was found inside a room that rented in a neighborhood.
> 
> Police authorities reported that so far the motive for the crime and the identity of the alleged murderers are unknown , as the residents of the neighborhood claimed not to have seen or heard anything.
> 
> The body of this man, who is identified but whose name was not revealed, was found this morning, inside a room that he rented in a neighborhood, located on the corner of Río Hondo and Centenario avenues, in the neighborhood San Antonio Zomeyucan of the municipality of Naucalpan.
> 
> After the neighbors realized that the alleged transvestite had not left for several days and that a strong fetid smell came from inside his home, considering that he was possibly dead, they reported it to the police.
> 
> Minutes after elements of the SSEM arrived at the property and carried out a review, they confirmed that this subject was already dead, so they cordoned off the entire area and requested the intervention of the MP to attest to the facts.
> 
> During the first eye inspections that he carried out inside the house, the MP reported that the deceased was lying face down inside his room and that he had suffered brutal blows to the body and a deep stab in the back that took his life.
> 
> Investigative police officers reported that they have already begun their police work to establish what was the motive for this crime and who or who were responsible for searching and detaining them.
> 
> Meanwhile, for these events, the MP began the corresponding investigation folder for the crime of homicide .
