Allessandra Ferreira was shot by a client in Rio Verde (Brazil) on 16 Sep 2021.

She was 29.


Alessandra was the second trans woman killed in Rio Verde in less than two weeks, following [Hevelyn Montine](https://tdor.translivesmatter.info/reports/2021/09/04/hevelyn-montine_rio-verde-goias-brazil_d2087251) on 4th September.


> A transsexual of 29 years was killed with a shot in the face after an alleged fight with a customer in the early hours of Friday (16), Rio Verde in southwest Goiás. A video shows when the shooter in a car , the victim approaches the vehicle and is shot. Then other people at the scene rush to try to help her (see above) .
> 
> According to the Civil Police, the transsexual worked as a call girl and the suspicion is that she was killed after a fight with the shooter, who would be her client. The case is being investigated, but as of 3:00 pm, the police still had no clues about the criminal.
> 
> According to police chief Adelson Candeo, a witness reported that she would have kept some of the client's objects, but this has not yet been presented at the police station. It's just a suspicion.
> 
> After being shot in the face, the victim was rescued and taken to the city's Emergency Care Unit. As the delegate, she arrived alive, but died on the spot.
> 
> The case continues to be investigated and new witnesses are expected to be heard in the coming days.
