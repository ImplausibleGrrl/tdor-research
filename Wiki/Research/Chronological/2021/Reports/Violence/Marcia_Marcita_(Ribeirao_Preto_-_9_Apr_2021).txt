A 48-year-old transvestite, identified as Márcia Marcita, was found dead by a friend, this Friday morning (9), in her own residence, on Egydio Pedreschi street, located in the Avelino Alves Palma Industrial Park, in the North of Ribeirão Preto. 

The friend, who found the victim's body, said that since Monday (5) he had not received any information about Márcia, which took him to her home to check the situation. At the scene, the man found the victim's body lifeless and with marks of aggression. 

The cause of death, not yet identified by the police, remains a mystery. According to Marcia's colleague, who did not have his identity released, the victim received no threats, but the body was found with blood and explicit marks of violence. "We have no information on this, but there is a lot of malice, the Expert will know what it is, there are marks," he said. 

The case will be investigated by the Civil Police, and so far no suspects have been arrested. The text will be updated as new information about the case is released.  


Transvestite is found dead at home in the North of Ribeirão

The body would have been found with signs of violence this Friday (9), in Parque Avelino; victim did not keep in touch and friends decided to go to the residence where she lived

PM was called to the victim's home on Friday morning (9), in Ribeirão Preto (Photo: Michelle Souza / CBN Ribeirão)
A transvestite was found dead on Friday morning (9), in the residence where she lived, in the Avelino Alves Palma Industrial Park, north of Ribeirão Preto. 
According to a friend of the victim reported to CBN Ribeirão, the body would be with marks of violence in one of the rooms of the property. The victim was approximately 45 years old.

"She was dragged from the living room to the bedroom and we believe she was killed, because there are marks," said her friend.

The PM (Military Police) was called and had not yet spoken. 

Still according to the information, the victim would have been missing since last Monday (5), and her friends decided to visit the property to check what had happened, when they found her dead .

So far, there is no information about possible suspects.
