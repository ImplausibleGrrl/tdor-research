Article published on November 3, 2021
by Elizabeth Vargas

A person was found dead at noon this Wednesday in Colonia Munguía.
The first data indicate that around 12:10 hours, on November 3, C-4 reported that, on calle Sokolow, in the Munguía neighborhood, a dead person was found inside a house.

Upon arrival, the officers confirmed that the body of a person was in a state of decomposition and had what is presumed to be a neck wound.

After the discovery, the area was cordoned off pending elements of the State Attorney General's Office, who are responsible for carrying out the corresponding investigation.

The first unofficial data indicates that the victim was a transsexual.



Cadena Noticias,  Mexico, Baja California, Tijuana,  03 November 2021 15:42
During this noon, the discovery of a lifeless body inside a home located on calle Sokolow in the Munguía neighborhood in the Ensenada municipality was reported.

According to the authorities that were notified by the C4, the victim was a transsexual woman who had a severe neck injury.

The house was located next to a car junkyard.
