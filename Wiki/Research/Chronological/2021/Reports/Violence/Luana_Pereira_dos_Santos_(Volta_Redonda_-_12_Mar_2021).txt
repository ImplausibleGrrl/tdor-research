Homicide is registered in the neighborhood of Jardim Paraíba, in Volta Redonda

03/13/2021 08:10:45

A young man, 26, identified as Luan Cristian Pereira dos Santos, was shot dead on Friday night (12), in Volta Redonda. He was cross-dressing and was known as "Luana".

According to the Military Police, the crime took place on Rua 544 in the Jardim Paraíba neighborhood. The agents said he was taken by Samu (Mobile Emergency Service) to Hospital São João Batista, but he did not resist.

The policemen isolated the crime scene until the forensics arrived. According to information given to PM, Luan was being threatened.

The case was registered with the city's police station. So far, no one has been arrested. The hypothesis of passionate crime is not ruled out. Also on Friday, a man was shot in the San Remo neighborhood of Volta Redonda and taken to a private hospital.
