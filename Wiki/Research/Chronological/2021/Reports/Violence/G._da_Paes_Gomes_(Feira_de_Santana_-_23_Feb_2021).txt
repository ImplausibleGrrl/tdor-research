Transvestite is shot dead in Mangabeira neighborhood
Laiane Cruz

A transvestite was murdered, around 12:30 pm on Tuesday (23), at Rua Barbosa, at the back of Residencial Laranjeiras, in the Mangabeira neighborhood in Feira de Santana.

According to police officers from the 66th Independent Military Police Company (66th CIPM), local residents reported that it was Gilvan da Paes Gomes, who lived in Agrovila, in the Mangabeira neighborhood.


Photo: Aldo Matos / Acorda Cidade

He was shot in the right face, right ear, neck and right ankle. The Civil Police were also at the scene and reported that so far the authorship and motivation are unknown.

Delegate Felipe Ghiraldelli carried out the cadaverous survey together with experts from the Technical Police Department (DPT) and civil police.

The body was sent to the organ where it will be necropsied.



G. was shot in the street early in the afternoon by an unidentified assailant. She died at the scene.
