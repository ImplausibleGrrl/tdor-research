A transgender woman was murdered and her body was abandoned in a vacant lot in the municipality of Chalco. According to security reports, the body had several blows to the body that allegedly caused death.

At the time of her discovery, the authorities verified that the victim was naked from the waist down. Because she was found in an unpopulated area next to the Río de la Compañía, in the Emiliano Zapata neighborhood, there is no report on detainees.

Police officers reported that the victim had alleged bruises on the abdomen and forearms, as well as that she had skin wounds.

"At first glance, you can see (the body) lying down that presents blows to different parts of the body," confirmed security agents. After an inspection, the officers indicated  that the body corresponds to a person of approximately 30 years who was wearing a white blouse and blue jeans.

Experts from the Attorney General of the State of Mexico (FGJEM) arrived at the scene to initiate investigations, clarify the identity of the body and those responsible.
