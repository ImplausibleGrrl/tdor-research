====== Raqueli (Rio Novo do Sul - 15 Aug 2021) ======

shot



A 28 year old trans woman named Raqueli was found shot dead in Rio Novo do Sul, Espírito Santo (Brazil) on 15 Aug 2021.



> A transsexual identified as Raqueli, 28, was found dead this Sunday morning (15), in Rio Novo do Sul, in the interior of Espírito Santo.
> 
> The victim's body was found by a resident who was walking on the road that connects Rio Novo do Sul to Santa Cândida. The woman called the Military Police.
> 
> According to the PM, Raqueli was hit by gunshots in the face and chest.
> 
> Witnesses said Raqueli was last seen getting into a red car.
> 
> So far, no one suspected of committing the crime has been arrested.




> Transsexual woman's body is found on the road inside ES
> According to information from TV Gazeta Sul, the young woman was 28 years old and used the social name of Raquele. The case was registered as a homicide and will be investigated
> 
> The body of a transsexual woman was found at dawn this Sunday (15) in Estrada Velha, in  Rio Novo do Sul , in the  south of Espírito Santo . According to information from TV Gazeta Sul , the young woman was 28 years old and used the social name of Raqueli. The  Civil Police  stated that, after the investigation was carried out, the case was registered as a homicide and will continue under investigation.
> 
> Popular told the TV Gazeta Sul report that a resident was walking in the region, around 7 am, when she found the body and called the police.
> 
> The body had several perforations from firearms and was located in a desert place, with little movement of people.
> 
> By means of a note, the Civil Police stated that the victim's body was sent to the Legal Medical Service (SML) of Cachoeiro de Itapemirim, to be released to family members and to undergo a cadaveric examination, which should indicate the cause of death.
