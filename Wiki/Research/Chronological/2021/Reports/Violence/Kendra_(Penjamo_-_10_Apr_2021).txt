A trans woman identified only as "La Kendra" or "La Barbie" was shot near her home in Pénjamo, Guanajuato (Mexico) on 10 Apr 2021.

She had survived a previous assassination attempt in the same area a year earlier.



After surviving an armed attack a year ago, she is finally executed


Pénjamo.- A transsexual who survived an armed attack in April 2020, died tonight while receiving medical attention at the General Hospital, after being attacked with bullets while traveling in his vehicle through Colonia Los Presidentes , in the municipal seat.

The event occurred on Saturday night alle Elías Calles, between Álvaro Obregón and Gustavo Díaz Ordaz. Neighbors in the area notified the 911 emergency system about a shooting at the scene.

Several patrols of the Municipal Police were mobilized to the site and located a person with gunshot wounds, aboard a sports car.

They immediately requested the support of paramedics from the local Red Cross , who treated the injured and rushed him to receive medical attention.

The victim, identified only by the aliases of 'La Kendra' and / or 'La Barbie', died a few minutes later as a result of the serious injuries suffered in different parts of the body.

Already on April 11, 2020, he had survived an attack when he was shot in his own home, on Elías Calles street, near the site where he was attacked tonight.

Experts from the State Attorney General's Office secured several percussion caps that were integrated into the investigation folder.


Penjamense shot dead in Colonia Los Presidentes 

Pénjamo.- A penjamense who was attacked with bullets in the Colonia Los Presidentes, in Penjamo, during the night of this Saturday, died while receiving medical attention at the General Hospital, was shot near his home, where in April 2020 they also tried murder him.
They happened to us around 9:30 p.m. this Saturday, on Calle Elías Calles, between Álvaro Obregón and Díaz Ordaz, in Colonia Los Presidentes.
There, a penjamense was attacked aboard a sports car of his property, seriously injured, he was approached by a Red Cross ambulance who rushed him to the General Hospital, where he died of serious injuries. It turned out that the attack also left an injured woman who was taken to the hospital in a private vehicle.
Who lost his life was identified with the nickname "La Kendra" and / or "La Barbie", a transsexual who survived on April 11, 2020, an armed attack on his own home.
The ministerial authorities are already carrying out the corresponding investigations.
