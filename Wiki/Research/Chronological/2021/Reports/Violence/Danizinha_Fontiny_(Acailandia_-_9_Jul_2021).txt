Transvestite is gunned down in Açailândia
Civil Police investigates the death of the transvestite. (Photo: Disclosure)

AÇAILÂNDIA - A transvestite, registered as Danillo Domingos Cardoso de Sousa, but known as 'Dani', 27 years old, was shot dead in the early hours of this Friday (9), in Açailândia, in a municipality 567 km from São Luís. victim's body was found in the morning by the Leolar allotment guard. In addition to the body, three rounds of firearm ammunition were found at the scene.

The Civil Police of Maranhão (PC-MA) awaits the expert report to find out if Dani was also beaten and investigates the authorship and motivation of the crime, with the help of images from security cameras that show the transvestite being thrown to the ground after a car stops at the place where the body was found.

According to the police, the transvestitis reported to the family that she was being threatened and, according to the police chief Saniel Thunder, who is investigating the case, she also had a history of the attempted murder.
