Aguilares: brutal murder of a trans woman at noon on Sunday
Monday, April 19, 2021 00:45

A trans woman was savagely slit her throat by her ex-partner during noon today. The police work at the scene.
A savage transfemicide occurred this noon in the city of Aguilares, Tucumán, where a trans woman was beheaded by her partner in B ° Tagusa Norte.

According to the first information, the deceased woman is known as "Vicky", 37 years old. The victim was found in his room in the middle of a huge pool of blood. His partner, a certain "Rubio" was apprehended, and is the main suspect in the crime.

Relatives of the victim accused his partner of being the author of the brutal attack. Personnel of the Motorized Patrol apprehended him in a neighborhood passage located to the east of Aguilares, being identified as "Rubio" who was with a deep wound as a result of having received a strong blow with a machete by a family member of the victim fatal »Detailed sources linked to the case to the BLL site.

According to the first investigations, the couple had separated a long time ago and there were already complaints of violence against the 24-year-old man arrested. In addition, the knife with which she was attacked was seized, ”explained the Chief Commissioner, Raúl Cornejo, Head of the Southern Regional Unit.

Around noon this Sunday, April 18, the Public Prosecutor's Office intervened in a transvesticide that took place in the Tagusa Norte neighborhood in the city of Aguilares. In the event, the Fiscal Unit for Serious Crimes against Physical Integrity of the Judicial Center of Concepción, whose head is Miguel Varela, intervened.

According to the first investigations, a male nicknamed "Rubio" stabbed Viki Nieva with a knife at the home where she lived. The 24-year-old subject is apprehended and the hearing to formalize the charges and the coercion measures will take place in the next few hours.

Investigators indicated that the attacker had used a knife that was recovered at the scene. The victim had multiple injuries in different parts of his body (arm, chest and neck).
