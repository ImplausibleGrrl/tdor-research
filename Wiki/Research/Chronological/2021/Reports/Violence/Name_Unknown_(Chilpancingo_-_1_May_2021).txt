An unidentified #trans woman was shot dead in Chilpancingo, Guerrero (Mexico) on 1 May 2021.

She was 25-30 years old. #SayHerName #TransLivesMatter #TDoR



They murder a transsexual in the vicinity of the Chilpancingo City Hall

The crime was reported to the authorities around 10:30 at night at the corner of calle Cristóbal Colón and Juan Ruiz de Alarcón, near the Zócalo in the capital.


> On Saturday night a transsexual woman was shot to death, two blocks from the City Hall in downtown Chilpancingo.
> 
> The crime was reported to the authorities around 10:30 pm on calle Cristóbal Colón corner Juan Ruiz de Alarcón, near the Zócalo in the capital.
> 
> According to the first versions, the victim, between the ages of 25 and 30, was attacked by armed individuals when he was walking in the area.
> 
> After the report of an injured person, State, Ministerial Police and members of the National Guard arrived who confirmed the fact and located a transsexual woman lying on the side of the street and sidewalk with shots in the body; She was wearing a green and black striped dress, tennis shoes of the same color, and they found a yellow bag.
> 
> The area was cordoned off by police officers, while the staff of the State Prosecutor's Office carried out the legal proceedings where they found percussion caps.
> 
> After the proceedings, the body was transferred to the facilities of the Forensic Medical Service (Semefo) for the corresponding legal procedures.
