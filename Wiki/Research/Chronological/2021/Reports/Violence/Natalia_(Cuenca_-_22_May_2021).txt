> OAXACA.-The Operational Unit for Crimes against Women for Gender Reasons of the Cuenca Regional Vice Prosecutor's Office reported that after being found responsible for the murder of a trans woman, in San Miguel Soyaltepec, in Cuenca, Oaxaca, a man is sentenced to 12 years in prison.
> 
> The case was judged as simple homicide , after the inquiries that were settled in the investigation file 16291/FCUE/SOYALTEPEC/2021, giving rise to criminal case 155/2021, according to the Oaxaca State Attorney General's Office (FGEO).
> 
> It indicates that on May 16, 2021, around 7:30 p.m., upon arriving at her home, Natalia was intercepted by Apolonio Sarmiento Simón who , with a machete in hand, began to insult her and say transphobic phrases.
> 
> Later, he hit him with a machete on his right leg; Due to the injury, the victim fell on his back to the ground , then the subject tried to hit him in the face with a machete , but the victim covered himself with one hand and lost two fingers after the machete hit.
> 
> Although Natalia was taken to the hospital for medical attention, after having lost a lot of blood from her injuries, on the night of May 22, she lost her life.
> 
> The organization Prodiana AC documents that during 2020, 15 trans women were murdered in the state of Oaxaca.
> 
> On its side, between 2008 and 2020, the Observatory of Murdered Trans People documented 528 cases of transfeminicide in Mexico , a figure that reveals the critical state that exists in the country, where there is a fragmented panorama in human rights and discrimination towards LGBTTT+ people . , in the fields of health, labor, social and institutional.
