They kill a transvestite in the Sexto Sentido motel in Cancun
It was initially said to be a suicide, but later it was reported to be a homicide
Cancun (Marcrix Noticias) .- A homicide was registered this Tuesday morning in a motel of the Supermanazana 63, Quintana Roo Police agents and elements of the Ministerial came to know.

The incident was reported at 11:30 in the morning when through 911 it was indicated that the lifeless body of a person was in one of the motel rooms and that he was hanged.

The first report indicated that it was a suicide since the body was suspended from the neck in the shower in the bathroom, but later information was circulated that revealed that he was in bed completely inert.

Agents of the Ministerial Police took up the case and there they learned that the body could be a transvestite who entered at night with his partner.

Elements of the State Attorney General's Office came to take charge of the crime which has not yet been fully clarified.
