They execute a trans woman near a gas station in Colonia El Llano, VdA

Tonight, a trans woman was shot to death on Pino Suárez Street, almost at the intersection of María Ahumada de Gómez, in the El Llano neighborhood, in VdeA. 

According to unofficial information, the trans woman, between 30 and 35 years old, had gunshot wounds to her neck and face. The female wore denim shorts and a shirt as well as two tattoos on both legs

The alleged perpetrators fled aboard a motorcycle, so far no people have been detained. The authorities arrived at the scene to cordon off the area, initiate investigations and proceed to remove the body.

Villa de Álvarez, Col.- This night a transgender woman was murdered in the El Llano de Villa de Álvarez neighborhood.

It was reported that around 8:15 pm, hitmen opened fire on the person who died almost instantly.

Those responsible fled to an unknown destination and were not arrested, the SEMEFO arrived at the place to remove the victim's body.
