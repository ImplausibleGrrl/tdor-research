> This Tuesday night, the fight resulted in the 26th homicide of the year in the Amesc region. The crime took place around 9:30 pm, at Cohab, in Praia Grande.
> 
> According to information, the 22-year-old transvestite was stabbed with three stab wounds—which hit the abdomen and chest—did not resist and died in front of a residence.
> 
> When the Military Police arrived at the scene, the victim was already dead, the area being isolated until the arrival of the Civil Police, the General Institute of Expertise (IGP) and the Legal Medical Institute.
> 
> As found at the crime scene, next to the body was a sum of money and some cocaine pins.
> 
> The weapon used was not found and the author of the 2nd homicide of the year in Praia Grande fled the scene. He has already been identified and at that time vehicles are carrying out searches to locate him.
> 
> The Civil Police began investigative work to identify the motive for the crime.
