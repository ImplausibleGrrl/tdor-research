Travesti is shot to death on a corner in the South Zone of Macapá; criminal ran away
Murder took place around 9 pm on Avenida Diógenes Silva with Rua Barão de Mauá, in a well-known prostitution spot in the Buritizal neighborhood.

A transvestite was shot dead on Thursday night (8) at a prostitution spot on the corner of Avenida Diógenes Silva and Rua Barão de Mauá, in the Buritizal neighborhood, in the South Zone of Macapá . According to the Military Police (PM), she was approached by a man and shot after an argument.

Her cell phone was taken, but money she had in her wallet was left. The PM said there were at least two shots in the back.

The Fire Department was still called for emergency care, but she died on the spot.

Friends of Marcinha Vaz, known as "Maquita", were at the place, but did not want to speak to the report. They just said that she was recently in Macapá from the community of Paredão, in Ferreira Gomes, in the interior of the state.
