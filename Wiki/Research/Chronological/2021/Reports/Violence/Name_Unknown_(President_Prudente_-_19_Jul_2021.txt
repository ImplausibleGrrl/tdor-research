> Body found on a rural road in Prudente is a transvestite, says PM
> Victim had no identity revealed, but was murdered after being shot in the rib region
> 
> Body found on a rural road in Prudente is a transvestite, says PM - Reproduction
> The body found on Monday (19) in the rural area of President Prudente is a transvestite, according to police. The victim was on a rural road that gives access to Bairro do Gramado with a shot in the rib region. According to the police, it was taken to the Regional Hospital, where the death was confirmed.
> 
> Near the crime scene, the PM found a crashed car with four people, three men and a call girl. All were arrested and taken to the Participatory Civil Police Precinct, where the case was registered.
> 
> MISUNDERSTANDING
> 
> Apparently, the transvestite was murdered after falling out with the suspects. There is a suspicion that he was on a sex show before he was murdered. "There was a commercial disagreement between the victim and the perpetrator and then there was the shooting," summed up the aspiring officer Thalles Macena, who responded to the incident.
> 
> The man named by the PM as the author of the murder already has police tickets for robbery and drug trafficking. The weapon used in the crime, a .38 caliber revolver, was discarded but found by a sniffer dog hours later. The case remains under investigation.
