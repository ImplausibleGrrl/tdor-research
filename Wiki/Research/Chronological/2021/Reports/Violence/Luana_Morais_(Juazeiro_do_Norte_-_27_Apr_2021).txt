Apr 27, 2020 at 10:00 am
Transvestite from Crato shot to death in Juazeiro do Norte

Exactly three weeks later and another transvestite was murdered in Juazeiro do Norte. At around 10:30 pm this Monday Pedro Lucas Morais, 20, the transvestite Luana, who lived on Rua Bom Jesus (Bairro Zacarias Gonçalves) in Crato, was injured with several shots. The crime took place on Rua João Pereira da Silva (Campo Alegre) in Juazeiro and it was rescued at the Regional Hospital of Cariri (HRC), but died early this Tuesday.

In 2018 Luana became a witness to a crime of drug trafficking that had as an indictment, at the Police Station of Crato, Rafaela Ferreira Batista, 25 years old. In the following year, she was the victim of a criminal misdemeanor whose author was Jonnas Oliveira da Silva, whose procedure is still pending in the Special Civil and Criminal Court of Crato.

The other transvestite killed this year in Juazeiro was 16-year-old Pietra Valentina, who lived on Rua Ernestina Sobreira near the Nossa Senhora das Dores dispensary in the Limoeiro neighborhood. She was murdered with several knife blows on April 5 at Rua São Sebastião near Escola Tabelião Expedito Pereira in the Pio XII neighborhood.
