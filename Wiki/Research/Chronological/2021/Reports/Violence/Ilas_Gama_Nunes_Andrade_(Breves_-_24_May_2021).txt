Ilás Gama Nunes Andrade was stabbed in Breves, Pará (Brazil) on 24 May 2021. A suspect has been arrested.

I've not found anything to confirm how they identified.



> PM arrests accused of killing transvestite in Breves
> The accused is Fabrício Nunes who was found by the police in a boat.

> After the death of the homosexual Ilás Gama Nunes Andrade, images from security cameras helped PM de Breves to arrive at the whereabouts of the criminal. The crime took place during the early hours of Tuesday (24), where  the victim was stabbed to death on the shore of Breves .
> 
> In the images, it is possible to see the accused and the victim arriving at the scene, Ilas is in front who turns off the light. Then, the two together smoke and talk, a body fight arises and the victim is stabbed. The accused flees, leaving the crime scene behind.
> 
> The accused is Fabrício Nunes who was found by the police on a boat, he was arrested along with the knife probably used in the crime.
> 
> According to the police, Ilás, was killed by reckoning due to the sale of a cell phone. The victim's body will be buried now in the morning and the accused remains under arrest at the disposal of justice.




> The edge of the city of Breves (Beira rio) during the early morning hours is seen as a place of prostitution, sale and consumption of drugs. It was in this scenario that the transvestite, popularly known as Ilás, was stabbed to death. The crime took place during the dawn of this May 24th, our team arrived at the scene together with the military police, who soon obtained details about the murder of the transvestite.
> 
> “We arrived at the place as soon as we received the information and found that the body belonged to Ilás, known here on the edge, but what we have is still little. We will try to speak with more witnesses to get the details we need about the case, but so far, we have not identified anyone ”said 9th BPM sergeant Costa I.
> 
> The victim had already passed through the police for theft and drug trafficking, according to information from witnesses, Ilás, may have been killed due to reckoning or a fight over drugs. Security cameras in the area can help police find who killed the victim.


> Transvestite is killed in hit or fight for drugs in Pará
> According to witnesses, the victim had already been through the police for theft and drug trafficking.
> 
> The shoreline of the city of Breves, one of the main islands of the Marajó archipelago, in Pará, is considered a tourist spot during the day due to the beauty it provides to those who visit or live there. But, unfortunately, in the early hours the pleasant place becomes a place for prostitution, sale and consumption of drugs.
> 
> It was in this scenario that the transvestite, known as Ilás, was killed with several stab wounds. The crime happened during the dawn of this Monday (24).
> 
> According to witnesses, the victim had already been through the police for theft and drug trafficking. Incidentally, she may have been killed due to reckoning or a fight over drugs.
