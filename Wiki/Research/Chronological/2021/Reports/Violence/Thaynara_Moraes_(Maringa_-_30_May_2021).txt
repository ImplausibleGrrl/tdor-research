Thaynara Moraes and Janine Naomi Ferreira Dos Santos were shot dead by two armed assailants in Maringá, Paraná (Brazil) on 30 May 2021.

Thynara was 18 and trans; Janine was 20 and cisgender. #SayTheirNames #TransLivesMatter #TDoR

> Double homicide in Maringá PR.
> A crime that scared the residents of the site took place early Sunday morning on José Toral Cherubim Street in the Conjunto Requião, the northern part of the city.
> According to two witnesses who were inside the house where the crime happened, the criminals using two motorcycles stopped at the front of the residence and entered the house.
>

They were gunned down at a residence located on José Toral Cherubim Street.
> 
> According to information, Janine was outside the property, when two armed men arrived, they dragged the young girl inside the residence where she killed Janine and Thaynara who was laying in bed.
>
