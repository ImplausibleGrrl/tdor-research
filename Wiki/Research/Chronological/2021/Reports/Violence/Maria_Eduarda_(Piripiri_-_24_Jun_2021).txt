Maria Eduarda was shot by two assailants on a motorcycle in Piripiri, Piauí (Brazil) on 24 Jun 2021.

She was 22.


> A young transsexual girl identified as Maria Eduarda, 22 years old, was murdered with gunshots, in the Parque Recreio Complex, in Piripiri. The crime occurred around 20:30 on Thursday (24).
> 
> According to popular information, two men arrived on a motorcycle and the pillion made the shots. The victim died on the spot.
> 
> Populars claim the victim may have been murdered in error.
