Travesti is assaulted and dies in the North of Ribeirão Preto, SP; suspect is arrested

According to the Military Police, the man named as the perpetrator of the crime was subsequently attacked by residents. He was detained by the PM at the UPA while receiving care.
By g1 Ribeirão Preto e Franca

The travesti died after being assaulted last Wednesday (13th) in the Jardim Salgado Filho neighborhood, in the North of Ribeirão Preto (SP). According to the first information from the Military Police, the suspect was arrested in the act while being treated in the emergency room.

According to PM soldier Fernando Souza, the police were called to respond to an incident of aggression at the intersection of Americana and Jacareí streets.

Witnesses informed the police that the transvestite and the man fell out for unclear reasons and began to fight.

The transvestite was seriously injured. According to Souza, residents angry at the situation started to attack the man, who is known as ‘galinha pintadinha’ ['the painted chicken'].

The police officer informed that the two were taken to the North Emergency Care Unit (UPA), but that the transvestite did not resist the injuries. The suspect was located while being treated and was eventually arrested.

The police report is being prepared at the Judiciary Police Center (CPJ) in Ribeirão Preto.

The identities of the victim and the perpetrator have not yet been released by the police.
