The victim's body was found floating in the Rio Preto river on the morning of Sunday 11th April. She was gagged and her hands were tied behind her back.

> On Sunday morning (11) the Muriaé Military Fire Brigade found the body of a 17-year-old [trans woman] floating in the waters of the Rio Preto. The case was reported by people calling 193, and after the military removed the victim's body from the water they handed it over to investigators. The suspicion of the firefighters, corroborated by the Civil Police of Minas Gerais (PC / MG), is that an execution took place, since the victim's hands were tied back and there was a cloth in her mouth.
