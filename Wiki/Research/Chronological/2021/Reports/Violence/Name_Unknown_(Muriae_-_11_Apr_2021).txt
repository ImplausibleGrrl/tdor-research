Military suspects that victim was executed; Civil Police investigate the case
04/12/2021 at 15:48

On Sunday morning (11) the Muriaé Military Fire Brigade found the body of a 17-year-old transvestite, floating in the waters of the Rio Preto. The case was denounced by people on the phone 193, with the military removing the victim from the waters and handing it over to the expert services. The suspicion of the firefighters, corroborated by the Civil Police of Minas Gerais (PC / MG), is that an execution took place, since the victim's hands were tied back and there was a cloth in his mouth.

Content continues after the announcement
The case is monitored by the State Department of Homicide Investigation and Protection of the Person (DHPP) of the Civil Police, which, in contact with the report, confirmed that it is compiling the information and making preliminary surveys about what happened.

The PC stated that the investigations are still in an embryonic stage, but that it has already been possible to identify the victim, who would be a drug user. Still according to the corporation, the crime may be related to the “drug theme”, but the agents are still investigating the details of the case.
