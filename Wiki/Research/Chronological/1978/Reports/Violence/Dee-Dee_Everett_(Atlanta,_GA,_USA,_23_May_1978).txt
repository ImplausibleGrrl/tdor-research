Dee-Dee Everett, 22, was shot and killed in the doorway of a vacant apartment at 754 Myrtle St N.E., Apt 3., Atlanta, Georgia. She was one of five women attacked (three killed, including Dee-Dee) between March and July 1978. Paul Jones,  31, of Thomson, GA was arrested and charged after a gun in their possession was connected to the shootings. No motive for the killings was given as of the publication date.
