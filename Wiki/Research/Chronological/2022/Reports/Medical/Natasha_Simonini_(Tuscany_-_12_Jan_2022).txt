In mourning, Bambola Star pays tribute to Natasha Simonini
In a video posted on Instagram, Bambola Star wears a black dress and says goodbye to her friend

January 14, 2022
GAY BLOG BR by SCRUFF
The Bambola Star , from the viral “Bom dia, Brasil! Good afternoon, Italy”, he  decided to pay a last tribute to his friend Natasha Simonini, who died this Wednesday, January 12, due to complications due to Covid-19. Simonini became famous for another meme: “Buonasera, Natasha. Buonasera, Katuxa”.

In a video posted on Instagram, Bambola Star wears a black dress and says goodbye to her friend. "I couldn't help but record this video in honor of Natasha Simonini".

The information before the death of Natasha Simonini was given by Katuxa Close on her Instagram. The meme “Buonasera Natasha” (“Good night, Natasha”, in Italian) gained backlash on Twitter in early 2021.

The video had been recorded on July 31, 2011, when Natasha Simonini got out of a luxury car when she arrived for Bambola Star's birthday party, which has also become a meme with the catchphrase "Good morning Brazil, good afternoon Italy".
