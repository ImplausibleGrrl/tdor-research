> The death of a transsexual activist moves in Tehuacán
> After the death of Sandra Martínez, a transsexual woman and activist from Tehuacán and the Sierra Negra , emerged on Friday afternoon, members of the LGBTTTIQ collective in Tehuacán as well as society have expressed their shock at the news.
> 
> In social networks, members of the LGBTTI community were surprised by the news, as were other activists and society in general.
> 
> For years, Sandra supported members of the community with different actions , among which support in changing the name and sex on birth certificates stands out, as well as participating in awareness campaigns and raising her voice to denounce problems that afflict the community .
> 
> As if that were not enough, Sandy is recognized for her fight against homophobia and transphobia as well as generating support for communities in the Sierra Negra where she was proudly originally from.
> 
> The activist and representative of Respecttrans , she died of a stroke due to a fall, which is why she was hospitalized for several days fighting for her life.
> 
> In this regard, Kevin Williams, representative of the LGBTTTI community, announced that in the next few days a tribute is expected to be made in honor of Sandra, hoping that her legacy will remain.
