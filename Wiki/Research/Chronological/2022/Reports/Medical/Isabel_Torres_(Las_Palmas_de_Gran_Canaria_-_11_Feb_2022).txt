> Died this Friday 11, at the age of 52, the transsexual actress Isabel Torres , known for the series Veneno .
> 
> The artist has been treating lung cancer since 2018. In November, she announced that she was publishing her latest video after being diagnosed with bone metastasis and knowing that she would have two months to live.
> 
> "Today, February 11, 2022, we say goodbye to Isabel. Although her family and friends are deeply sorry for her loss, we know that wherever she goes, she will have fun as only she knows," it was posted on her Instagram profile .
> 
> "Thank you for all the expressions of affection and concern. She left feeling very loved and held."
> 
> Isabel was a model, presenter and businesswoman and the first transsexual from the Spanish region of the Canary Islands to change her gender on her identity document, in 1996.
> 
> In 2020, she gained worldwide fame by playing the pioneering transgender actress Cristina Ortiz (1964-2016), in one of the phases of her life, in the Spanish series Veneno , on HBO Max.
