> Women are arrested on suspicion of applying industrial silicone in buttock augmentation in BH; transvestite clients died
> According to the Civil Police, the duo had no qualifications in biomedicine. The value of the application varied between R$ 2 thousand and R$ 3 thousand.
> By Camila Falabela and Vagner Tolendato, g1 Minas and TV Globo — Belo Horizonte
> 
> 06/21/2022 12:41 pm  Updated3 hours ago
> 
> The Civil Police of Minas Gerais (PCMG) arrested two women, aged 22 and 38, suspected of applying industrial silicone to customers, in the Coqueiros neighborhood, in the Northwest Region of Belo Horizonte .
> 
> According to investigations, the duo had no qualifications in biomedicine or authorization to perform the procedure , which was performed in a home. The information was released this Tuesday morning (21), during a press conference.
> 
> According to investigations, on April 7 this year, a 37-year-old transvestite died at the scene, shortly after taking the injection . A month earlier, she had already applied two liters of the substance to each buttock.
> 
> On the day of the death, the suspects informed the police that the victim was at the scene recovering from treatment in Goiás. For delegates Hugo Arrudas and Frederico Abelha, responsible for the case, the version presented by them was an attempt to prevent the investigation of the case.
> 
> During the investigation, a second victim was discovered. This is another transvestite, 33 years old, who died on June 1 from complications after receiving industrial silicone in her body.
> 
> Information was passed on by the Civil Police, during a press conference — Photo: Vagner Tolendato/TV Globo
> Information was passed on by the Civil Police, during a press conference — Photo: Vagner Tolendato/TV Globo
> 
> According to PCMG, the place where the applications took place was totally unhealthy: an environment with mold, without ventilation. Customers took the product. The value of the application varied between R$ 2 thousand and R$ 3 thousand .
> 
> With the women, cell phones and about 60 syringes with anesthetic were seized .
> 
> They can answer for aggravated murder, illegal practice of medicine and forgery .
> 
> The 38-year-old suspect had already been arrested for the crimes of homicide, sexual exploitation of minors, bodily harm, threat and on suspicion of running a prostitution point in Contagem, in the Metropolitan Region of Belo Horizonte .
> 
> PCMG reinforces that industrial silicone is totally contraindicated by the Health Surveillance Agency (Anvisa) for this type of procedures in the human body . It is used for cleaning airplane and car parts and in glass waterproofing.
