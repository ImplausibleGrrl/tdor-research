Victoria de Lima was shot in Maringá, Paraná (Brazil) on 25 Jun 2022.

She was 25.


Allegedly died in front of the hospital after being told no doctors were available - see https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=BAjM68MuANM.Died%20in%20front%20of%20the%20hospital%20after%20being%20told%20no%20doctors%20were%20available%20

> Transvestite is murdered during June party in Maringá
> By Editorial GMC Online
> 
> 06/26/2022
>  
> A 25-year-old transvestite was executed with at least four shots in Maringá. The crime took place on the night of this Saturday, 25, in the Santa Felicidade complex.
> 
> Click here and receive our news by WhatsApp 
> Follow GMC Online on Instagram
> The victim was identified as Victoria de Lima. She was participating in a June party that took place in Praça Zumbi dos Palmares, when she was surprised by the shooter. After shooting, the man fled.
> 
> People called for help, but the victim's family preferred to refer her to the UPA. When they arrived, they found a vehicle blocking the entrance, so they took Victória to the Municipal Hospital, next to the UPA.
> 
> She had injuries to her abdomen, chest and head. According to reports from a cousin of hers, when they arrived at the hospital they were told that there was no doctor to attend to the young woman. She would have died in front of the hospital unit.
> 
> The report contacted the Maringá City Hall, which will report back on what happened.
> 
> The crime will be investigated and the police believe it is linked to drug trafficking.
