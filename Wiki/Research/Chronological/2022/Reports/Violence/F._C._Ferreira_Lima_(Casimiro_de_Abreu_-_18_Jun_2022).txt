> Transvestite shot dead in Casimiro de Abreu identified.
> 
> A transvestite was shot dead this Friday morning (17) in Rio Dourado, in Casimiro de Abreu. The victim was identified as Fabrício Costa Ferreira Lima, 26 years old.
> 
> According to the Military Police, the agents were called on Estrada Velha de Berra de São João, where the body of the transvestite, who has a criminal record for drug trafficking, was located.
> 
> The corpse was removed to the Legal Medical Institute (IML) of Macaé and the occurrence registered at the 121st Police Station (121st DP). There is still no information about the authorship and motivation of the crime. Nobody was arrested.




> Transvestite body is found with gunshot marks in Casimiro de Abreu
> Crime occurred on the night of this Friday (18) in Rio Dourado. The victim has not yet been identified.
> A transvestite was shot dead on Friday night (17) in Rio Dourado, in Casimiro de Abreu (RJ). The victim has not yet been identified. She was found undocumented, according to police.
> 
> According to the Military Police, the agents were called and went to Estrada Velha de Barra de São João, where the victim's body was found and removed to the Legal Medical Institute (IML) of Macaé.
> 
> The case was registered at the 121st Police Station.
> 
> The report, the Civil Police reported that witnesses are being heard and steps are carried out to identify the author and clarify the motivation of the crime. The report also asked about the identity of the victim and is still awaiting a return from the Civil Police.
