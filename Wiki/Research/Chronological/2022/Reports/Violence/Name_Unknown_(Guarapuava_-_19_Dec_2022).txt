> Feud between transvestites ends with one dead in Guarapuava
> Murder suspect arrested
> 12/20/2022 08:50 am
> 
> 
> An old feud between two transvestites ended up with one dead on the night of that Monday (December 19) in Guarapuava.
> 
> The occurrence was recorded by the Military Police at 9 pm, on Avenida Moacir Julio Silvestri. The police were summoned to the Hospital São Vicente de Paulo, where a patient who was the victim of physical aggression was admitted. At the scene, the doctor told the PMs that Samu's ambulance sent the 27-year-old transvestite for medical attention, and she couldn't resist her injuries and died. The doctor also said that, later, a friend of the victim arrived at the hospital, informing that they worked together and that the victim would have been attacked by another transvestite.
> 
> The witness was located by the police and confirmed who was the author of the homicide, passing on her possible whereabouts.
> 
> During patrolling, the PMs found the 25-year-old suspect, who confessed that she had assaulted the victim, saying that they already had an old feud and that, according to her, the victim would have said that he was going to kill her and that yesterday he went to “collect the tape".
> 
> The suspect received a voice in prison and was sent to the 14th SDP.
> 
> The body of the fatal victim was collected from the Legal Medical Institute (IML) of Guarapuava.
