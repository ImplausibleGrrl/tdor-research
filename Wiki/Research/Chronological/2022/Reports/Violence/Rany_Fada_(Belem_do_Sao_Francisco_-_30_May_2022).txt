Rany Fada's body was found in Belém do São Francisco, Pernambuco (Brazil) on 30 May 2022.


Rany's body was found on Monday 30th May. A suspect was arrested less than a day later.

> A trans woman known by the name Rany Fada was stoned to death in Belém do São Francisco. The victim's body was found on Rua José Alves Maia, in the Ipsep neighborhood, on Monday (30).
> 
> Residents held a protest on Monday night to demand the elucidation of the case. Less than 24 hours after the crime, police officers from the 1st CIPM arrested a man suspected of killing the young woman. The Civil Police continues with the investigations.
> 
> This is the second crime committed against a trans woman in the Sertão de Pernambuco, in the month of May. On the 13th, a young woman known as Bruna was shot dead in the municipality of Lagoa Grande (PE).
