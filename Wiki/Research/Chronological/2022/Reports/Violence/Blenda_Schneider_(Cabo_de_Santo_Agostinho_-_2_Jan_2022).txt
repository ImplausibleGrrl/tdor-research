> A 34-year-old trans woman was shot dead on Sunday (2), in the Bela Vista neighborhood, in Cabo de Santo Agostinho. According to the Civil Police, the victim, identified as Blenda Schneider, was murdered on 19th Street around 21 pm.
> She was coming back from a friend's house when she was shot. Blenda was known in the community where she lived as Guegê and worked as a kitchen assistant, but was unemployed.
> Neighbors of the victim, who did not want to record an interview or identify themselves, claimed to the TV Globo report team that she had many disaffects and used to engage in confusion frequently. However, there is no information about the motivation and authorship of the crime.
> The case was recorded by the South Metropolitan Region Homicide Task Force. Blenda wasn't helped, because she couldn't resist the injuries and died at the scene. Her body was taken to the Institute of Legal Medicine (IML), located in the neighborhood of Santo Amaro, in the Centro do Recife.
> ′′ A police inquiry has been instituted and further information may be provided after full enlightenment ", the Civil Police said, by way of note.
