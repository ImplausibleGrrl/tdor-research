> A transgender woman was found dead this Friday night (12), with a chest wound in João Pessoa, the state capital. According to the Military Police (PM), the crime took place at a prostitution point, located in the Ernesto Geisel neighborhood.
> 
> Bia, who had been a call girl for more than five years, used drugs and was involved in robberies in the region, according to a friend's account, who believes a mistaken robbery attempt may have taken the woman's life.
> 
> The victim, whose full name and age are still unknown, must undergo an expert examination to identify the type of weapon used in the crime. Likewise, the motive and the suspect also remain undefined.


> A trans woman was found dead with marks of violence on a public road, in the early hours of Saturday (13), in the Geisel neighborhood, in João Pessoa.
> 
> As delegate Diego Garcia informed Notícia Paraíba, the victim, identified as Bia, 28 years old, was murdered with a perforation in the neck.
> 
> The transvestite's body was found sent for expertise at the Scientific Police Institute (IPC). 
> 
> According to the delegate, during investigations at the scene, no one was able to provide more information about the case.
> 
> The authorship and motivation of the crime are still being investigated by the Civil Police.
