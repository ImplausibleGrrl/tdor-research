> A 16-year-old trans teenager was stabbed to death in the early hours of this Thursday (3), in Ibicaraí, in the south of Bahia. There is no information on the motive and authorship of the crime.
> 
> According to the Civil Police, the victim was identified as Kauana Vasconcelos. Residents told police that the teenager was taken by a man on a motorcycle to a house on Rua Nova, where two other suspects were. The victim was allegedly stabbed and then dragged to a vacant suit, where they tried to drown her.
> 
> Kauana was rescued by neighbors and taken to the Arlete Maron Municipal Hospital, but she couldn't resist.
