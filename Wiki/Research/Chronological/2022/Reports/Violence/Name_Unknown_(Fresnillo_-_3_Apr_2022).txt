> Two people were killed this Sunday afternoon after a shootout was recorded in the vicinity of the Hemiciclo Hidalgo, in the center of Fresnillo, Zacatecas. A transsexual woman died immediately and a 17-year-old minor was taken seriously injured to a hospital where he lost his life, police sources reported; So far his identities are unknown officially. The shooting left the center of Fresnillo empty, whose population has had to get used to not going out on the streets , said Yesenia Robles, who witnessed the shooting: “Well, it is better to be at home, because there is insecurity,” she said. The number of violent deaths over the weekend began with a double murder of two young people in Guadalupe, in Fresnillo three bags with human remains were found and the number of victims is unknown.
