> Trans is stabbed to death after hanging out with customer in the upper part of Maceió
> 10/01/2022 15:23  Leave a comment
> 
> A transsexual, social name Jasmyne da Silva, 20 years old, was stabbed to death, on Sunday, 09, in Tabuleiro do Martins, in the upper part of Maceió.
> 
> Through social networks, friends of the victim report that Jasmyne, who was a call girl, would have gone out with a client and he would have killed her with several blows of melee weapon.
> 
> "I saw her around 10:00 last night. She went to see a client. He, in wickedly, stabbed her after the show. He had no money to pay or has left in the intention of doing evil. So trans girls be very careful because Jasmyne never hurt anyone. She's a person i don't have to talk about. My feelings for the family," a friend of the victim, Lanna Hellen, said on Instagram Stories.
> 
> After the crime, the police authorities were activated. The experts of the Institute of Criminalistics carried out the surveys in order to find traces that help in elucidating the case. Then the body was released for burial.
> 
> According to the Reception Center Ezequias Rocha Rego, this is the first murder of trans people in the year 2022.
