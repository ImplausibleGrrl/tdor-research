Günay Özyıldız was found with stab wounds at the entrance to an apartment building in İzmir (Turkey) on the evening of 16 Jan 2022.

She was 48.




Transgender person found dead at entrance of building

The incident occurred in an apartment on İnönü Street in Üçkuyular Mahallesi yesterday evening. Allegedly, those who saw a person lying on the floor at the entrance of the apartment reported the situation to the police.

In the investigations carried out by the teams who came to the address after the notice, it was determined that Günay Özyıldız (48), living in the apartment and claimed to be a trans person, was stabbed in the neck. Thereupon, 112 Emergency Medical teams were called to the address. The medical teams who came to the address determined that Özyıldız died.

On the other hand, after the crime scene investigation teams and the prosecutor's examination, Özyıldız's lifeless body was taken to the morgue of the Izmir Forensic Medicine Institute, and a large-scale investigation was started by the police teams as to who or by whom the murder was committed.
