> Colonel Jorge Mauro Córdoba, commander of the Department Police, reported: "Unfortunately we had a homicide with a sharp weapon inside a residence, there was a transgender person known by the name of Paula and all the work for the collection was carried out. of all the evidence.
> 
> He added: “ the victim was dedicated to being a web cam model, that is what we have been able to advance initially in the investigation, but we establish all the activities to clarify the fact.”
> 
> In this regard, the leader of the community table in the city, Yamile Zabala, rejected this type of event and maintained that it is not the first time that it has happened, which is why it called the attention of the authorities to reinforce protection measures. She pointed out that the LGBTI population continues to be highly stigmatized due to sexual preference, so she hopes the case will be clarified and those responsible will be caught so that they can answer for the criminal act.
> 
> For his part, the mayor of the Quindian capital, José Manuel Ríos, rejected this type of event that mourns the community and stressed that they work to protect anyone regardless of their sexual condition. He stressed that the investigations are progressing, but the main hypothesis suggests that it would be about passionate issues, however, work is progressing to clarify the case.
