> ACAPULCO, Gro., September 25, 2022.- A trans woman was stabbed to death in the bar area of the Iguala Center.
> 
> The crime occurred minutes before 2 o'clock on José María Morelos y calle Pavón in that city in the northern region of the state.
> 
> According to a report and testimonies from neighbors, the victim argued with a young man, who would be his partner, and took out a knife from his clothes with which he injured her and then fled.
> 
> The seriously injured person requested help from paramedics, but upon arrival they confirmed that he no longer had vital signs.
> 
> The 30-year-old victim was wearing a navy blue blouse, blue jeans, a white and black fanny pack, and blue tennis shoes.
> 
> At the end of the expert work, the body was transferred to the facilities of the Forensic Medical Service (Semefo) for the necropsy of law.


> Guerrero .- A transsexual woman was murdered with a knife in the heart of the city of Iguala de la Independencia, located in the northern part of the state.
> 
> The events occurred around 3:00 a.m. this Sunday on José María Morelos y calle Pavón, outside the bar area. 
> 
> According to police reports, neighbors warned about a fight involving several people at that point in the city.
> 
> Upon arrival, the police corporations found a lifeless person, who had blows to the face and various stab wounds to the body produced by a knife. 
> 
> The fatal victim, whose name and exact age are unknown, was wearing blue jeans and a navy blue blouse. So far no one has been arrested for these events.
> 
> Later, experts from the State Attorney General's Office arrived and carried out the first investigations, and at the end of these investigations, the body was transferred to the city's Forensic Medical Service.
