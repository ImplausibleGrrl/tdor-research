An unidentified trans woman was shot in the street in Resende, Rio de Janeiro (Brazil) on 13 Apr 2022.

She was 29.


> A 29-year-old transvestite was shot dead in the early hours of Wednesday, the 13th, in the city center. The crime took place at the door of a commercial establishment, near the city's bus terminal. The victim was identified as Rodrigo da Silva Franco. According to the Military Police, two men who would be on a motorcycle are suspects in the crime. As of this writing, no suspects have been arrested.
> 
> Thank you for watching
> 
> Police officers from the 37th Military Police Battalion (BPM) were called at around 3:40 am to check for gunshots, in the vicinity of the bus terminal. At the scene, the military received information that the transvestite had been shot. She was rescued by a team from the Fire Department to the Henrique Sérgio Gregori Municipal Emergency Hospital, in the Jardim Jalisco neighborhood. However, the victim did not survive his injuries.


> A 29-year-old transvestite was shot dead at close range in the early hours of Wednesday (13) in Resende. The crime took place on Avenida Gustavo Jardim, downtown, near Rodoviária Velha.
>
> According to the Military Police (PM), security cameras caught when two men on a motorcycle arrived at the scene. One of them got down and walked towards the trans woman. A man who was sitting on the sidewalk runs away before the shooting.
