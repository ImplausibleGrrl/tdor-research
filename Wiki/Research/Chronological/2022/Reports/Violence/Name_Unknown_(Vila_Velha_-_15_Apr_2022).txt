====== Name Unknown (Vila Velha - 15 Apr 2022) ======
Created Sunday 17 April 2022

Name Unknown
Avenida José Júlio de Souza
15 Apr 2022
Praia de Itaparica
Vila Velha
Espírito Santo
Brazil
-20.3703447,-40.3045652
shot 




> A transvestite was shot in the back, on Avenida José Júlio de Souza, one of the busiest on Itaparica beach, in Vila Velha. The crime happened on the night of this Friday (15). 
> 
> The place where everything happened is deserted, but a witness saw the moment when a man, dressed in a white shirt, argued with the transvestite. Then he shot the victim. 
> 
> Despite having a purse when she was killed, the victim had no identification documents. A camera from the city hall that is a few meters from the scene of the murder can assist in the investigations. 
> 
> The Military Police reported that police officers went to the scene, but when they arrived to attend to the occurrence, a team of the Municipal Guard was already in the region. The Civil Police was called. 
> 
> The Civil Police said the case was registered as a homicide and will continue under investigation. So far, no suspect has been arrested and details of the investigation will not be released, for now.
> 
> The victim's body was sent to the Legal Medical Department (DML) of Vitória, to be necropsied and later released to the family.



> The Praia de Itaparica neighborhood, in Vila Velha, was the scene of the death of a transvestite last Friday night (15).
> 
> According to the Military Police, agents were called to go to the neighborhood to investigate the complaint that a person had been shot in the region. Arriving there, they found that the victim was a transvestite who did not resist the wound. 
> 
>  A team from the Municipal Guard was already at the scene and the Civil Police was called. Also according to the PM, according to reports she would have argued with a man previously. 
> 
> The Civil Police said in a note that the case was registered as a homicide and will continue under investigation. So far, no suspect has been arrested and details of the investigation will not be released, for now.        
> 
> The victim's body was sent to the Legal Medical Department (DML) of Vitória, to be necropsied and await release to the family.
