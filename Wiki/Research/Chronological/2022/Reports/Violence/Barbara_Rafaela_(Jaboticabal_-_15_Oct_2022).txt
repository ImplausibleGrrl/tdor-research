> Mother of the trans woman shot dead along with her boyfriend during an argument in Jaboticabal (SP) over the weekend, Ana Paula de Sousa Sampaio asked for justice during her daughter's funeral, on Sunday (16).
> 
> The shootings, according to the Military Police, were carried out by a man who refused to pay for a sexual program with trans people, known as Bárbara Rafaela, 23, on Saturday (15).
> 
> "It's difficult, it's a pain... the only thing we want is justice, to look for this murderer, because he destroyed an entire family."
> 
> In addition to hitting Bárbara and her boyfriend, José William, one of the shots hit Ana Paula's leg, who received medical attention and has since been discharged. Her husband and a dog were also grazed by injuries.
> 
> "I heard the noise of the shots. By the time I ran there, he had already shot my son-in-law, my son-in-law was already down. My son, I went to hug him, and he also shot my son in the chest [the trans woman], and my son died too. I was hit, my husband was grazed. It was a lot of tragedy."
> After shooting, the man fled by car and until the publication of this report had not been located. The case was registered as a double homicide and bodily injury by the 1st Police District of Jaboticabal.
> 
> Bárbara Rafaela's funeral took place in Taquaritinga (SP) on Sunday afternoon. José's body was buried in Promissão (SP), in the region of Bauru (SP).

> A 23-year-old transvestite and her 29-year-old partner were shot dead during an argument inside a tenement in Jaboticabal (SP) on Saturday night (15).
> 
> According to the Military Police, the shootings against a person identified as Bárbara and José William da Silva Jesuino were carried out by a man who allegedly refused to pay for a sexual program with the transvestite.
> 
> In addition to the couple, the shots hit Barbara's mother. She was referred to an Emergency Care Unit (UPA) in the city, but there is no information about her health status.
> 
> As of the publication of this report, no suspects for the crime had been arrested.
> 
> Military police were called around 8:10 pm and, when they arrived at the scene, which has several houses on the same land, they found the couple lifeless.
> 
> To the corporation, a witness informed that the reason for the fight was a disagreement in the payment of a sexual program, in which the suspect of the shootings would have hired Barbara, but not made the payment.
> 
> During the argument, Barbara's companion tried to intervene, but the two were shot. At least one of the shots also hit the transvestite's mother.
> 
> The suspect then fled in a car.
> 
> The expertise and Civil Police investigators were called to the scene of the crime and found a 9mm magazine with 12 intact ammunition and 11 spent shells, in addition to seven cocaine eppendorfs, of which five were empty and two were full, and two portions of marihuana.
> 
> Three cell phones and R$185 in cash were also seized.
