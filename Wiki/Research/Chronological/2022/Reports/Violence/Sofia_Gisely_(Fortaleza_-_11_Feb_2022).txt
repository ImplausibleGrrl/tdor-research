> Transvestite is found dead on land in the Bom Jardim neighborhood, in Fortaleza
> Sofia Gisely's body, found in a field, was injured by a blunt object
> Posted on Feb 11, 2022
> 
> This Friday morning (11), a 22-year-old transvestite was found dead in the Bom Jardim neighborhood, in Fortaleza. Sofia Gisely's body, found in a field, was injured by a blunt object.
> 
> According to the Secretary of Public Security and Social Defense (SSPDS), the Department of Homicide and Protection of Persons (DHPP) is investigating the case. So far, no one has been arrested.
> 
> The state folder emphasizes information that the population can contribute to investigations by passing on that helps police work. Complaints can be made to the number 181, the SSPDS Complaint Dial, or to (85) 3101-0181, the WhatsApp number, where complaints can be made via message, audio, video and photography.
> 
> Reports can also be forwarded to the Homicide Division's telephone number (85) 3257-4807, which is also the Department's WhatsApp.

> 
> The body of transvestite Sofia Gisely, 22, was buried this Saturday morning (12), in the public cemetery of Bairro Bom Jardim, in Fortaleza. The young woman was murdered in the early hours of Friday (11) and the body was found on a plot of land on Avenida Osório de Paiva . As of Saturday afternoon, no one has been arrested. The Department of Homicide and Personal Protection investigates the case.
> 
> In the farewell, friends and family paid tribute to Sofia. Sister Kaylliane Façanha stated that Sofia Gisely was always cheerful and that the day before her murder dela she spent the day with her dela.
> 
> "Very happy person, but very happy. She never cried. There wasn't a day she cried. She just made us laugh when we were sad. Last time I saw her was the night before. She spent the day and night there. at home. She left and still looked at me and it was her last goodbye. I want justice. She didn't deserve to die like that. She didn't deserve this", he said.
> 
> Transvestite is chased, beaten and dies by hitting her head on the floor; video
> Transvestite is arrested after setting fire to her mother's house in Ceará
> The president of the Association of Transvestites and Transsexual Women of Ceará, Andrea Rossati, regretted what happened and said that her death is yet another case of violence and impunity that happens against transvestites in the state.
> 
> "It's another case of transphobia, another case of cruelty against a transvestite, a trans person and they are acts always with refinements of cruelty. Unacceptable acts, because nothing justifies taking a person's life with so much cruelty and so much refinement of hate was the marry Sofia", he lamented.
> 
> According to the Secretariat of Public Security, the transvestite's corpse was found with injuries resulting from a blunt object. Teams from the Military Police and State Forensics were also called to the scene. An investigation was launched by the Homicide Division, a unit that investigates the circumstances of crime.


> Family and friends were present at Bom Jardim Cemetery, in Fortaleza, where they accompanied the procession of the body of transvestite Sofia Giselly, this Saturday, 12. She was stoned to death during the early hours of Friday, 11, in Osório de Paiva, in Fortaleza.                                
>                             
> A family member, whom O POVO does not identify, points out that many friends were at the wake and burial, which took place in Grande Bom Jardim. Sofia was dear and loved in Parque Santo Amaro, where she had many friends. Her dream was to become a hairdresser and set up her own salon. "I always wanted to have a profession, to have a salon," she reported.
>
> After the farewell moment, the family members' mission is to ask for justice. According to information from the family, there are security cameras in the vicinity of the crime scene that can assist investigations. Relatives also question the initial version that she was killed by a client.                            
>                             
> Even with procedures to be resolved about her daughter's death, Sofia's mother, according to the family member, cannot find the motivation to leave the house. "She's in no condition. She's just lying down. Only God can comfort people's hearts", she explains.
>
> Sofia's favorite song was De Mal a Pior, by singer Larissa Emily. She always asked her sister to listen. The song was placed at the wake, before the coffin was closed. "But it's always like this, when everything is going well, someone insists on changing. Heart gets used to it, but it still gets hurt", says the lyrics.
>
> For mother, sister and friends it is impossible to get used to the absence that the music speaks of. "It's been more for me. I've lived together a lot. I'm the one who was most present", says a sister whose name was preserved.
