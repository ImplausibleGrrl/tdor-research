> By MANUEL MARÇAL
> 04/21/22 - 11:58 am
> 
> A 33-year-old woman was murdered in the early hours of this Thursday (21) by her partner with knife blows and a blow to the head. The crime took place in the Mantiqueira neighborhood, in the Venda Nova region, in Belo Horizonte. The 28-year-old man confessed to killing his girlfriend after being found with blood-stained clothes. His mother was the one who called the police.
> 
>  The couple had been in a relationship for two and a half years and lived together. The motivation of the crime according to the Military Police of Minas Gerais (PMMG) was jealousy. She would be allegedly cheating on her partner with another man.
> 
> Sergeant Fúlvio Mussolini, from the Group Specialized in Motorized Prevention Ostensive Rapid Patrol (GEPMOR), said that there is no evidence that a third person was at the scene. "I think he lied, he got here, had a falling out with the victim and committed the murder."  
> 
> The soldier said the man showed no regrets. The perpetrator was found at his mother's house, who lives in the same neighborhood. 
> 
> She told the report that she called the PMMG after her son arrived at her house, around 6 am, with blood-stained clothes, upset and telling her that he killed the victim. 
> 
> "I said to him: 'What did you do that for? She loved you so much, she's such a good girl. Why did you kill her? You couldn't have killed her. Leave her there and go away'", narrates owner Geralda Francisca dos Reis Barbosa. , 65 years old. 
> 
> The victim's mother-in-law said that she waited for her son to get in the bath to call the PM, because she was also afraid of him. She said the man tried to trick the police by changing his clothes, but it was she who gave the bloody clothes to the military. 
> 
> According to neighbors heard by the report and by the PMMG, the victim worked as a call girl. Her mother-in-law said she didn't know that and that she still adored her. 
> 
> The couple had several fights and, in none of them, a police report was registered. "My son was very jealous of her. He even said to her, in front of me: 'If you betray me, I'll kill you,'" the lady narrated in tears. 
> 
> "I'm sadder for her death than for my son's arrest, because he shouldn't have killed her. I'm sad, boy. Life, who gives, it's only God. And only He can take it away. having called the police for him".
> 
> The author of the murder has several passages by the police, among them for trafficking and robbery. The mother said he has mental retardation, asthma and a hearing problem.
