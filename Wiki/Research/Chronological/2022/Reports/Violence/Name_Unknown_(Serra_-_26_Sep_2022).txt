> Transvestite is assaulted and stabbed to death in Serra, ES
> 
> A transvestite was attacked and stabbed to death at the end of the night of this Monday (26) in the Jardim Limoeiro neighborhood, in Serra, Grande Vitória.
> 
> According to eyewitness reports to the police, the crime would have happened after an argument between her and people who were inside a car.
> 
> The victim has not been identified. Police officers must return to the scene to seek images from video surveillance cameras that can help find out how the suspects fled the scene and other information that leads to the clarification of the crime.
> 
> The police report says that after being stabbed by a knife, the victim was hit by a vehicle.
> 
> After the crime in Jardim Limoeiro, a vehicle was seized by the PM on suspicion that it had been used in a hit-and-run in the region.
> 
> Two people inside the car ran away. The vehicle was subject to theft and robbery.
> 
> The Military and Civil Police were contacted by the report for more information about the seizure of this vehicle and also to clarify the death of the transvestite. This article will be updated as soon as there is feedback.
