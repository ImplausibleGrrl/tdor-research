Danielly do Carmo Santos was stabbed by her ex-partner in Tucuruí, Pará (Brazil) on 16 Jun 2022.

She was 24.




> The young Danielly do Carmo Santos, 24, was signed on Thursday night (16), in Tucuruí, after a disagreement with her ex-partner. Dany, who was transsexual, said no to her ex, Erick Matheus, 26, who did not accept the end of the relationship and stabbed her seriously. The crime happened around 10 pm, on Cametá street, Vila Peniel neighborhood, close to the Navegantão stadium.
> 
> Dany, who lived with her mother, was rescued by her sister, Daiane do Carmo, but arrived lifeless at the UPA in Tucuruí. The Civil Police was called and searches for Erick, who is on the run.
> 
> According to information provided by the Civil Police, it was around 10 pm when Erik would have looked for the victim to try to resume the relationship. In the face of Dany's refusal, in the heat of an argument, he stabbed the victim and then fled the scene.
> 
> The suspect lived with the victim for about a year and did not accept the end of the relationship. Danielly told family members that the young man was unusually jealous. The two had an argument last night. Erick hit the woman with a blow to the neck.
