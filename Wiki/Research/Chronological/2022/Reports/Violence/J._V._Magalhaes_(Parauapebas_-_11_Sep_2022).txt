====== J. V. Magalhaes (Parauapebas - 11 Sep 2022) ======

@violence

Rua Rio Branco & Avenida Presidente Kenedy
Beira Rio I
Parauapebas
Pará

-6.076811788393937, -49.885715746214444
shot
[see also https://parawebnews.com/em-parauapebas-cliente-nao-paga-a-conta-e-travestis-o-espancam/]


J. and another trans woman named Vitória were both shot in the street in the early hours of Sunday 11th September. Although Vitória survived, J. died from her injuries.

J's social name is not known.
 
> In the early morning hours of this Sunday (11), a transgender woman was shot dead on Avenida Presidente Kennedy, on the corner with Rua Barão do Rio Branco, in the Beira Rio I neighborhood, in Parauapebas, southeast of Pará.
> 
> According to the police, it was around 1:45 am when the trans woman identified as João Vitor was shot in the back and died on the spot.
> 
> Another trans woman, who was only identified as Vitória, was shot in the leg and taken by an ambulance from Samu to the municipal hospital of Parauapebas.
> 
> Police are investigating information passed on by witnesses that the shooter was in a black pickup truck. Several 9mm ammunition cartridges and a veiled holster were found at the scene.
