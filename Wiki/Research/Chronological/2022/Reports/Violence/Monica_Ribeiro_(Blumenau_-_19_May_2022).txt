Mônica Ribeiro was shot dead by an assailant on a motorcycle in Blumenau, Santa Catarina (Brazil) on 19 May 2022.

She was 36.



> Transvestite is shot dead at a bus stop in Blumenau
> Published by Redaction
> 20/05/2022
> 
> A transvestite was shot dead on Thursday night (19) in Blumenau . According to preliminary information, the victim is approximately 35 years old and was at a bus stop when she was shot. There is currently no information about the killer.
> 
> The crime was recorded around 10:45 pm on Rua Engenheiro Udo Deeke , in the Salto do Norte neighborhood. neighborhood . According to witnesses, the criminal arrived at the scene on a motorcycle and shot several times at the victim, who died on the spot. According to witnesses shortly after, the suspect fled the scene after the crime.
> 
> Portal Alexandre José was at the scene moments after the crime and followed part of the work of the Military Police. Check-out:
> 
> Military Police garrisons are in the region searching for the killer. Meanwhile, teams from the General Institute of Forensics (IGP) gather information at the scene of the occurrence. The victim's body will be sent to the Legal Medical Institute (IML) , and the case will be investigated by the Civil Police.
