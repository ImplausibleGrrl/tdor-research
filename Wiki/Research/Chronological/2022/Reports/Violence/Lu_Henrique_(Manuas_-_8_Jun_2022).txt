> Manaus/AM – Luís Henrique, 22, a transvestite better known as “Lu”, was shot dead in a square at dawn on Wednesday (8), on rua Japurá, in the Jorge Teixeira neighborhood, in the East.
> 
> Lu lived with two other friends in the same neighborhood, and according to them, the transvestite had left the house around 2 am.
> 
> Hours later, residents heard gunshots and when they went out to see what had happened, they found the victim lying on the ground.
> 
> According to the forensics, Lu was killed with two shots to the back of the head. Her body was sent to the IML and the murder will be investigated by the Civil Police.
