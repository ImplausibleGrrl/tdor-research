Transvestite, identified as Soraia, was stabbed to death. (Photo: Publicity)

By: From the Editor March 17, 2022
On Tuesday night (15), the transvestite, identified as Soraia , 59 years old, was killed with knives inside her own home in Vila Maresia, in the municipality of Raposa , Metropolitan Region of São Luís .

According to the police, Soraia worked as a hairdresser in the region. The victim had her residence invaded by suspects, who fled with several objects from the scene.

Also according to the police, Soraia's neighbors found the victim lifeless and called in the Military Police .

Soraia's body was removed to the Legal Medical Institute ( IML ), in the Bacanga neighborhood. The case is investigated being by the Superintendence of Homicide and Protection of Persons ( SHPP ) as robbery, which means robbery followed by death.

Until the beginning of this Thursday (17), no one involved in the crime had been arrested.
