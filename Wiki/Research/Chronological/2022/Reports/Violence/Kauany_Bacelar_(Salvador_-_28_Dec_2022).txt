Kauany Bacelar was shot in the street in Salvador, Bahia (Brazil) on 28 Dec 2022.

She was 17.




> Bahia: Young transsexual is approached by criminals and killed with several shots, in the middle of the street
> BAHIABIZARREGRIEF
> Updated Dec 29, 2022
> On social networks, several family members and friends mourned the death of young Kauany Bacelar. Police investigate crime of homophobia or passion.
> 
> A young woman identified as Kauany Bacelar was found dead in the neighborhood of Pau da Lima, in Salvador, early this Wednesday (28/12). Residents of the region found the body of the young woman left in one of the streets of the neighborhood.
> 
> According to the military police, the body of the victim, who was trans, was found on Rua da Esperança. In her head, agents noticed several gunshot marks. There is still no official confirmation that it is transphobia, that is, a crime because of the gender adopted by Kauany. On social media, several family members and friends mourned the young woman's death. One of them commented on the victim's profile that she would have gone out to enjoy the night with some friends. According to the Civil Police, the case is being investigated by the 2nd Homicide Police Station (DH).




> A transvestite was murdered in the neighborhood of São Marcos, on the border with Pau da Lima, early this Wednesday (28/12). The crime took place around midnight, on Rua da Rendenção.
> 
> According to the source of Informe Baiano, the victim was found on a public road with several marks of gunshots.
> 
> A resident told IB that drug dealers from the “A Tropa” faction killed the young woman in another location and abandoned the body on Rua Rendenção, which is dominated by the Bonde do Maluco (BDM). The crime must be investigated by the Department of Homicide and Personal Protection (DHPP).
> 
> Around midnight on Wednesday (28), police from the 47th CIPM were called via Cicom with information that the body of a person had been found on Rua da Redenção, in the neighborhood of São Marcos. At the scene, the PMs found the victim without vital signs and called the Crime Scene Investigation Service (SILC) to remove the body and carry out the expertise. Authorship and motivation will be investigated by the Civil Police .
> 
> The Department of Homicide and Personal Protection (DHPP) is investigating the death of a trans woman, still without formal identification, whose body was located on Rua da Redenção, in Pau da Lima, early this Wednesday (28). She was the victim of gunshots. The case will be investigated by the 2nd Homicide Police Station (DH). The Technical Police Department (DPT) was on site to carry out the crime scene expertise.
