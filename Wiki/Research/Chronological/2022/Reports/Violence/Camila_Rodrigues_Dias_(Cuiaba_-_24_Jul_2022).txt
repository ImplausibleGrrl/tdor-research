Camila Rodrigues Dias was beaten to death during a robbery at the club where she worked in Cuiabá, Mato Grosso (Brazil) on 24 Jul 2022.

She was 41.



> Camila Rodrigues Dias, 41, was murdered in the early hours of Sunday (24) at the Playcy Bar, in the São José neighborhood, in Cuiabá, during a robbery, according to the Military Police. A man was also attacked and his arm was broken. Suspect invaded several rooms of the nightclub, which works as a brothel, and stole several cell phones and personal objects, the PM found.
> 
> People said the suspect was drunk. That at dawn he left towards the rooms installed in the unit. In the first, there was a man sleeping. The door was broken down and with a piece of wood, he proceeded to attack it. Then he stole his cell phone.
> 
>  Then he went to the room where Camila was. The door was forced open and the victim was beaten with several blows to the head. Her cell phone was also taken. She then broke into two other rooms and took the victims' phones and valuables. 
> 
> Civil Police and Politec were called, as well as the Mobile Emergency Care Service (Samu). 


 
> A transgender woman was brutally beaten to death in the early hours of Sunday (24), in Cuiabá.
> 
> The crime took place in a bar/club located in the region of Avenida Fernando Corrêa da Costa, where the victim acted as a call girl on a daily basis.
> 
> The suspect is a regular at the establishment itself, according to witnesses. He would have gone to the scene, struck the victim and other customers, fleeing the scene later.
> 
> According to reports, the man would have stolen other people's belongings. The transsexual received medical attention, but she could not resist and died on the spot. The suspect has not yet been located.
> 
> The motivation is determined by the Specialized Police Station for Homicide and Protection of the Person (DHPP).
