> MEXICO CITY.- Camila Ignacia, a member of the LGBT community, was located with signs of decomposition in an apartment building in the Juárez neighborhood, in the Cuauhtémoc mayor 's office .
> The discovery of the 42-year-old victim occurred on Friday night when one of his roommates noticed the fetid smell and worms at the entrance to his room.
> 
> The tenants alerted the landlady, who entered the room and found the body covered with a black cloth and with signs of decomposition .
> 
> Although the causes of death were not specified due to the state in which the body was found, her acquaintances fear that she has been murdered since May 27, when her last connection was on WhatsApp.
> 
> According to preliminary reports, the person responsible would have been his sentimental partner, a man of Chinese nationality, nicknamed "El Chino".
> 
> The acquaintances of the victim mentioned that Camila and El Chino had recently had differences, since he infected her with HIV.
> 
> It will be the investigations of the Capital Justice Prosecutor's Office that determine the causes of death and the motive for death.





> Trans activist Camila Boloco is murdered and her body is left in an apartment in Cdmx
> By Zocalo Group
> 
> The Chilean activist was found in an apartment seven days after she was murdered; activists ask for justice
> 
> Mexico City.- The young transsexual of Chilean origin, Camila Boloco , was found dead in an apartment in Mexico City, according to various communications and videos broadcast on social networks , the main suspect could be her sentimental partner, who so far it has not been located.
> 
> The activist had lived in Mexico for some time and according to the information that has been released, the young woman was a strong activist of the Union of Trans and Transvestite Sex Workers , a human rights organization that looked after the interests and rights of sex workers . .
> 
> “With much pain and indignation we inform the community of the trans-homicide of Camila Boloco. Activist of our union, who was currently living in Mexico.
> She was found dead several days after her death in horrible condition. The case is being investigated so we cannot provide more information.
> Send us a big hug to her family and close to her.
> Enough of transhomicides @redlactrans
> 
> Various voices have raised their hands to demand justice for the probable femicide of the activist , who, according to her acquaintances, was a great woman, a good person, with an incredible vibe and unfortunately both her friends and her family are ruined by the way when they took his life
> 
> In the profile of Amanda Jofre , activist of the Chilean Corporation of Trans People , a message was spread echoing the information about the murder of the young woman , in said message it is explained that for the moment no further details have been given about what happened and meanwhile the investigations on that unfortunate fact are advancing.
