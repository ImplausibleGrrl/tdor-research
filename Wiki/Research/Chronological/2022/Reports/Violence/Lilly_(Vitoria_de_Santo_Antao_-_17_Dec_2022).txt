> Brazil – A transvestite known as “Lilly” was executed last Saturday night (17), in Vitória de Santo Antão (PE). The shots hit the chest, back and head region. A video that shows the exact moment of death of “Lilly” circulates through social networks, showing the victim's last breath, while the crowd around triggers the authorities.
> 
> According to preliminary information, the crime occurred on Avenida Mariana Amália, when “Lilly” was brutally executed while passing through the street. “Lukinha? Ah, that's it!”, comments the witness who filmed everything, revealing the possible name of the victim, Lucas, while a woman is ready to warn the mother of “Lilly”.
> 
> One of those present in the group of people that formed around the body notices that he was still breathing, however, soon one of them claims that there is no chance of survival, “nothing escapes”. Information about the motives for the incident and suspects have not yet been released, but the crime has features of execution.
> 
> The body of “Lilly” was removed by the Legal Medical Institute, after being examined by the Technical-Scientific Police Department (DPTC). The case should continue to be investigated by the Civil Police of Pernambuco.
