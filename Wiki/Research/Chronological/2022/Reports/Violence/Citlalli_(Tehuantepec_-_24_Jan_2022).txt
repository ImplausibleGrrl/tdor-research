> A woman and a transgender woman are shot to death on the Isthmus of Tehuantepec
> 24 January, 2022
> 
> A woman and a transgender woman are shot to death on the Isthmus of Tehuantepec
> 
> This Sunday night, a 47-year-old woman identified as Sinai Sánchez and her companion, a 42-year-old trans woman known as "La Citlali", were shot to death.
> 
> The armed attack was recorded in a bar called Flor de Canela located in the town of Unión Hidalgo on the Isthmus of Tehuantepec, where unknown individuals arrived opening fire.
> 
> Due to these facts, elements of the Oaxaca Attorney General's Office initiated the corresponding proceedings. No arrests have been reported for these events.
> 
> Meanwhile, in social networks, members of the Transgender Movement in Oaxaca, condemned the events and demanded justice for the person of the trans and muxe community.
