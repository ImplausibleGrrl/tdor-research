> Transvestite is found dead and tied to the bed inside the house in DF
> Better known as Lu, she was tied with her hands behind her back with marks of violence. The case is being investigated by the Civil Police of the DF.
> posted on 6/22/2022 11:41 pm / updated on 6/22/2022 11:41 pm
>    
> The body of a woman was found , on the night of this Wednesday (22/6), inside a house, on Rua 49 de São Sebastião. According to the Civil Police of the Federal District (PCDF), the victim is a transvestite. Better known as Lu, she was tied up and with marks of violence. 
> 
> Civilian police were called around 9:15 pm. The Correio found that Lu's friends had not heard from the woman and, worried, they went to the residence. Arriving at the place, the door of the house was open and, when they entered, they found the woman on her stomach on the bed  with her hands tied behind her. 
> 
> The victim's car from the garage, a Honda HRV, was taken away. The case will be investigated by the 30th Police Station (São Sebastião). Until the last update of this report, no one had been arrested.




> Investigators from the 30th Police Station (São Sebastião) are investigating the case of the death of a transsexual woman as robbery. Identified as "Lu", the victim's body was located on Rua 49 da Vila Nova, in São Sebastião , on Wednesday night (22/6). The door to the house was unlocked and the police found her tied up on top of the bed.
> 
> The column found that the victim was face down on the bed, with his hands tied. She would have been strangled to death in her own clothes. Some pieces were found wrapped around her neck. The perpetrator of the crime would still have taken her car, a Honda HR-V.
> 
> Aside from the door being found open, there were no signs of a struggle inside the property. Lu's cell phone was found by a recyclable material collector. The device was taken by the man to the police station after answering calls from friends of the victim who were worried.
