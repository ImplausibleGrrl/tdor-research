Bianca Machado Rodrigues disappeared in Aparecida de Goiânia, Goiânia (Brazil) on 22 May 2022.

Her dismembered body was found several days later.

Bianca was 33.




Bianca disappeared on Sunday 22nd May and her dismembered body three days later. Shortly afterwards her ex-partner was arrested and confessed to killing her.

> Man is arrested suspected of killing, dismembering and burying transvestite ex-girlfriend, in Aparecida de Goiânia
> Civil Police found part of the body hidden under a sofa in his house and the rest was in a closed forest. Suspect confessed to the crime.
> By Rafael Oliveira, g1 Goiás
> 
> 05/26/2022 10:26 am  Updated7 hours ago
> 
> 
> A man was arrested on suspicion of killing, dismembering and burying the body of transvestite Bianca Machado Rodrigues, in Aparecida de Goiânia , in the Metropolitan Region of the capital. The Civil Police found part of the body hidden under a sofa in his house, and the rest in a closed forest.
> 
> The man's name was not revealed by the Civil Police. Therefore, g1 did not locate the defense to comment on the case until the last update of this report.
> 
> The police discovered that the suspect was the transvestite's ex-boyfriend. He was arrested on Wednesday (25) and confessed to the crime, in addition to showing where the body parts were hidden. The motive has not yet been disclosed.
> 
> The police investigation was able to establish that the victim was killed by asphyxiation and dismembered by her ex-boyfriend. She was at the suspect's house, in the Jardim das Esmeraldas Sector, in Aparecida de Goiânia .
> 
> After leaving his residence, the victim would go to the beauty salon where she worked as a hairdresser, but was not seen again and did not make contact with friends and family.
> 
> After the investigation is over, the suspect can be held criminally liable for femicide and concealment of a corpse, according to the Civil Police.




> Transvestite who was missing is found dead; ex-partner is suspect
> Bianca Machado Rodrigues was last seen on Sunday (22)
> 05/26/2022 - 07:49
> Bianca Rodrigues
> 
> The body of a 33-year-old transvestite who had disappeared in Aparecida de Goiânia, last Sunday (22), was found dismembered in Jardim das Esmeraldas, near Vila Brasília. The case is under investigation, but there is a suspicion that the victim's ex-partner is involved.
> 
> Bianca's family and friends even asked for help to find her after her disappearance, until the discovery of the body.
> 
> According to the integrated care record (RAI), Bianca left home on Sunday morning and never came back. She reportedly went to the suspect's house, from where she left the next day in an app car bound for work. However, she did not arrive at the scene.
> 
> Family members started looking for her and also informed the Civil Police's Missing Persons Investigation Group. Her body was located in Aparecida de Goiânia and suspect, arrested in the act for murder and concealment of corpse. He confessed to the crime.
> 
> As determined by the Civil Police, the suspect in the crime asphyxiated to death and then quartered Bianca. Part of her body was found hidden under a sofa in the man's house. Other parts were taken in a suitcase and thrown into a closed forest in the Villa Sul sector, in Aparecida de Goiânia. 
> 
> The police investigation will be concluded in the coming days and the suspect will be available to the Judiciary.
