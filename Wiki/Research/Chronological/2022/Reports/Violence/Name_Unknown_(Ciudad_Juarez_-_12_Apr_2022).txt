The body of an unidentified trans woman with signs of suffocation was found in Ciudad Juarez, Chihuahua (Mexico) on or shortly before 12 Apr 2022.


> Juarez.- A man was murdered and his body thrown on the public highway dressed as a woman, the discovery occurred at the intersection of calles Navojoa and Ajusco in the neighborhood Ampliación Fronteriza.
> 
> According to the first data, the victim was a male person who was apparently killed by suffocation, since his face was covered by a black plastic bag; likewise, he was dressed in women's clothing.
> 
> People who crossed the site observed the body and proceeded to make the report to the 911 Emergency service.
> 
> Agents from the Municipal Public Security Secretariat arrived at the scene to cordon off the area while experts from the State Attorney General's Office proceeded to carry out the investigations.
