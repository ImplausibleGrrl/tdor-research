Anne Souza was shot in the early hours of 2 May 2022 on a dirt road in Piúma, Espírito Santo (Brazil).



> Trans woman shot and killed in southern state
> The accused would be a man, who was in a white truck
> Clovis Rangel | 05/02/2022, 11:35 11:35 h | Updated on 05/02/2022, 11:35
> 
> A young transsexual, identified as Anne Souza, was shot dead in the early hours of Monday (2) on a dirt road in Maranguá, in the interior of Itapemirim, on the southern coast of the state. 
> 
> According to the Civil Police, a man, in a white pickup truck, would have shot the victim - who was wearing an orange blouse and skirt and had tattoos all over her body - hitting her with a shot in the neck and three in the chest, around 2:30 am. She died instantly.
> 
> Also according to the investigations, Anne was the woman's social name and her family is from Piúma. 
> 
> “It has not yet been fully identified. Anne was the social name, but it was registered as Gabriel and her family is from Piúma. That's what we were able to find”, said delegate Djalma Pereira Lemos, who will lead the case at the Itapemirim Police Station. 
> 
> The Military Police were at the scene of the crime, isolated the area and called the Civil Police Expert, which carried out the expertise and sent the corpse to the Legal Medical Service (SML) of Cachoeiro de Itapemirim.
