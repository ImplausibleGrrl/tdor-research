> Ximena García, a trans activist, was the victim of a transfeminicide : she suffered an attack last Saturday, January 22, in the Azcapotzalco mayor's office and died in a hospital in the Cuauhtémoc mayor's office that same day.
> 
> The 24-year-old was stabbed last Friday and was transferred to the General Hospital of the Doctores neighborhood, where she died hours later.
> 
> In the face of the murder, the #JusticiaParaXimena campaign was launched on social networks as it was considered a hate crime, to which members and groups of the LGBT community have joined, as well as militants from Morena, a party of which the activist was a member.
>

> The spokesman for the Mexico City Attorney General's Office (FGJ-CDMX), Ulises Lara, indicated this Tuesday, January 25, that the investigation into transfeminicide was opened.
> 
> "Investigative Police agents have also been given the task of carrying out field and cabinet work, such as analysis of public and private video surveillance cameras, and location of possible witnesses that allow clarifying the facts," he said in a statement. message through social networks
> 
> The capital official added that the Prosecutor's Office for the Investigation of Crimes Committed in Offense of Priority Attention Groups, of the General Coordination for the Investigation of Gender Crimes and Attention to Victims, will attract the case.
> 
> "In all cases, we reiterate the firm and determined position of the Mexico City Attorney General's Office to always stand by the victims," ​​Ulises Lara asserted.
> 
> The murder of Ximena García occurs only a few days after the activist Natalia Lane suffered an attempted transfeminicide in a hotel in the Portales neighborhood, Benito Juárez mayor's office. She was attacked with a knife, but managed to survive, on January 16.

> In social networks, the LGBT+ community and activists demanded justice for the victim through the hashtag #JusticiaParaXimena.
> 
> The trans woman had just begun her transition when the hate crime took her life.
