> Two arrested for killing transgender
> By APPJuly 03, 2022
> Rawalpindi: Police have arrested two accused for stabbing and killing transgender, informed police spokesman.
> 
> The accused along with his accomplice killed a transgender Aamir.
> 
> The case of the incident was registered in the Racecourse Police Station three days ago on the complaint of the nephew of the deceased. Racecourse Police arrested the accused with the help of modern technology and human intelligence.
> 
> SP (Potohar) appreciated Racecourse Police for arresting the accused adding that all legal requirements will be met to convict the accused.
> 
> Every possible steps are being taken to ensure protection of life and property of the deprived sections of the society, he added.



> RAWALPINDI: A transgender person died after being stabbed in the jurisdiction of Race Course police.
> 
> Rashid Haleem, nephew of Amir Masih (the victim), resident of Misryal Road lodged a first information report (FIR) with police saying that he received a phone call from someone who informed him that his uncle (Amir) had been murdered.
> 
> He said he along with his father and brother went to the apartment where Ishfaq Masih informed them that Amir was stabbed and then pushed by two people during an argument about a cooler. The argument happened because two people accused Amir of damaging their cooler.
> 
> The body was shifted to the District Headquarters (DHQ) Hospital for postmortem and later handed over to his family.The suspects are yet to be arrested.
