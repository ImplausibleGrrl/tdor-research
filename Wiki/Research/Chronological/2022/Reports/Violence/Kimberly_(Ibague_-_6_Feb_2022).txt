Diskimberly was stabbed in Ibagué, Tolima (Colombia) on 6 Feb 2022.

She was 30.


> The identity of the foreign woman from the LGBTIQ+ community murdered in Ibagué
> Tue, 08/02/2022 - 06:00
> The trans woman of foreign nationality was stabbed to death.
> 
> 
> The Judge of Valle de San Juan with Function of Control of Guarantees on duty in Ibagué, sent Robinson López Montoya to the Picaleña Prison accused of murdering a person from the LGBTI community near the Andrés López de Galarza Park.
> 
> killer
> The deceased trans woman was identified as Antonio José Villegas, of Venezuelan nationality, who was known in his personal circle as 'Kimberly'.
> 
> In the early hours of Sunday morning in the Carrera Segunda with calle 18 corner near Parque Andrés López de Galarza, allegedly "Kimberly" stole some money from Robinson López Montoya. 
> 
> The 49-year-old man, from San Antonio, south of Tolima, reacted and stabbed the female member of the LGBTI community with a knife at the abdomen.
> 
> La joven ‘trans’ fue trasladada por unas amigas en un taxi hasta el Hospital Federico Lleras Acosta, sede La Francia, donde los médicos no pudieron salvarle la vida. 
> 
> Los uniformados de la Policía Metib que fueron avisados del hecho de sangre, reaccionaron y capturaron a López Montoya a quien le hallaron en su poder un arma blanca, con la cual habría asesinado a la venezolana. 
> 
> La Fiscal 16 seccional del Grupo de Flagrancias presentó en audiencia virtual a Robinson López Montoya a quien le imputó cargos por el delito de homicidio agravado por colocar a la víctima en situación de indefensión o inferioridad o aprovechándose de esta situación.
> 
> The detainee did not accept the charges and due to the seriousness of the facts he was sent behind bars while the process against him progresses.
> 
> 30 years old had 'Kimberly'.
