====== Bianca (Primavera do Leste - 6 Oct 2022) ======
Created Saturday 08 October 2022
@violence



Bianca [Wallison Denis Carvalho Gonçalves]
22
6 Oct 2022
Primavera do Leste
Mato Grosso
Brazil
shot


> Military Police confirmed that Wallison Denis Carvalho Gonçalves, 22, was killed with at least two shots to the head near a fair in Primavera do Leste (244 kilometers from Cuiabá), last night. According to a PM soldier, the victim introduced herself as 'Bianca' and was known in the city for working as a transvestite.
> 
> The Civil Police is investigating the crime as robbery (theft followed by death), as the victim's purse and other belongings were taken.
> 
> The body was sent to the Legal Medical Institute (IML), will undergo necropsy exams. According to the Primavera funeral home, Wallison Denis' body will be transferred to São José do Rio Preto (SP), where the family members reside.
