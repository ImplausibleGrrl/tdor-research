F. E. Caetano was shot in Santana do Acaraú, Ceará (Brazil) on 6 Jan 2022.

She was 44.



F. was found shot dead on the morning of Thursday 6th January. We don't know her social name.

> Trans woman is gunned down in the city of Santana do Acaraú
> 
> The body was found this Thursday morning (6th) behind the School of Vocational Education, the victim lived in the same neighborhood where she was killed
> People called the PM after finding the body of Francisco Edgar Caetano, 44, this Thursday morning (6) on a plot of land behind the Francisco das Chagas Vasconcelos State Professional Education School in the João Alfredo neighborhood in the city of Santana do Acaraú.
> 
> According to the PM, the Trans woman was shot dead, she lived in the same neighborhood where she was murdered, the body was recognized by family members and collected by the Forensic Expertise at Sobral's IML, there is no information on the authorship of the crime, which will be investigated by the Police Civil of Santana do Acaraú.
