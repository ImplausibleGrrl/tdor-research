> The Justice converted into preventive the arrest of Juarez de Oliveira Souza, suspected of murdering the transsexual Rihana, 37 years old, with sticks in Jardim Noroeste, in Campo Grande , last Sunday (20). Juarez was Rihana's boyfriend and had been living with the victim for 8 days.
> 
> In a statement to the police, the suspect said that on Saturday night (19) the couple used crack and ended up arguing because the victim allegedly sold the suspect's belongings, including a stove, to buy drugs and did not pay him.

> Sale of stove led to fight that ended in the death of a trans woman

> He left the place and upon returning on Sunday morning (20), Rihana allegedly attacked him with a knife. The suspect would have said that he no longer wanted to live with the transsexual. Amidst the confusion, Juarez admitted that he took the piece of wood from the victim's hand and, hit Rihana in the neck, which fell to the ground. On the ground, the suspect struck three more blows to the victim's head.

> Juarez told the police that he left the place without knowing that the transsexual had died. Neighbors who witnessed the fight, called the Samu (Mobile Emergency Service), which confirmed the death of the woman. Juarez was found by the police in front of a bus stop in the neighborhood, where he was arrested and taken to the Deam (Women's Police Station). The crime was registered as a femicide. > Sejusp-MS/Disclosure
> 
> A 37-year-old trans woman was beaten to death by her partner in front of a residential occupation in Jardim Noroeste, in Campo Grande, this Sunday (20 ) . The author of the crime, identified as Juarez de Oliveira Souza, was arrested trying to escape in front of a bus stop.
> To the police, the suspect reported that he had a romantic relationship with the victim and had gone to her house to collect her debt. Upon arriving at the scene, the couple got involved in a fight, when Juarez hit the woman in the head.
> 
> In testimony, neighbors said they heard several screams. Residents saw the couple's fight and reported that Juarez wielded a knife and made threats against the victim. To defend herself, the woman grabbed a piece of wood.
> It was then that Juarez took the piece of wood from the victim's hand and struck the first blow to her neck. The woman fell to the ground and the suspect struck another three violent blows to the victim's head, reports the BO.
> 
> Very injured, the victim lay motionless on the ground and the neighbors called the Emergency Medical Service (Samu) and the Military Police. Death was confirmed by rescuers.
> 
> After fleeing the crime scene, Juarez was found by the police in front of a bus stop. The man was taken to the Women's Service Police Station (Deam), where the crime was registered as a femicide.
