An unidentified trans woman was stabbed by another trans sex worker in the Pigalle area of Paris (France) during the early hours of 11 Aug 2022.

She was 39, and reportedly from Congo.




> Deadly brawl between two transgender people in Pigalle. The victim, aged around 30, was stabbed to death on the night of Wednesday to Thursday in Paris (18th century). Around 2:10 a.m. at the corner of boulevard de Clichy and rue Germain Pilon, an altercation broke out between these two trans women. One of them stabs the abdomen while the other collapses, her head hitting the pavement.
> 
> A witness warns the CRS who are securing the area. Help arrives quickly and takes care of the victim who, eviscerated and unconscious, is in cardiopulmonary arrest. The Samu team gave her cardiac massage for an hour before taking her to the hospital with a vital prognosis. Despite the care provided, she died at 5 a.m.
> 
> According to the neighborhood, the alleged perpetrator fled on foot towards the Moulin Rouge before getting into a taxi. The murder weapon was found on the street by officials. The judicial police have been tasked with carrying out the investigations. Suspect number one is actively sought. For now, the motive for this crime remains unknown.
> 
> In Paris, transgender people are regularly assaulted in the Bois de Boulogne where they prostitute themselves. The most emblematic case is that of Vanessa Campos . Two Egyptians aged 24 and 29 were sentenced last January to fifteen and twenty years' imprisonment for this crime committed on the night of August 16 to 17, 2018 on this undocumented transgender sex worker. Four other men received three-year prison sentences, two of which were suspended for six years for their participation in the deadly expedition.




> A transgender prostitute died overnight from Wednesday to Thursday in Paris after being stabbed by another trans sex worker, AFP learned on Friday from a source familiar with the matter.
> 
> A murder investigation has been opened and entrusted to the 2nd district of the judicial police, according to the Paris prosecutor's office requested by AFP, confirming information from Le Parisien .
> 
> The case took place overnight from Wednesday to Thursday around 3 a.m. in the Pigalle district of Paris. For reasons still unknown, the two women began to fight, before one of them stabbed the second and fled.
> 
> The victim, aged 39 and from Congo , died two hours later despite the care of the Samu, according to the source familiar with the matter. An autopsy of the victim's body was to take place on Friday.
