> Manaus (AM) – A transvestite identified as Paloma, 34, was killed with at least two shots to the head and chest. The case took place on Monday night (5), on Campa Street, Colonia Terra Nova neighborhood, north of Manaus.
> 
> According to Captain Rômulo Botelho, from CPO Norte, the victim's family said that the murder took place during a fight with her boyfriend, who is suspected of carrying out the shooting. He is being wanted by the police.
> 
> “We are investigating what could be a crime of passion. His boyfriend would have come here, there was an argument and he ended up shooting two at the victim, who died here at the scene. It's not a common occurrence, we don't see this kind of thing here in the North Zone, where the boyfriend kills the other boyfriend”, said the captain.
> 
> The Legal Medical Institute (IML) was called to remove the body from the scene and carry out tests.
