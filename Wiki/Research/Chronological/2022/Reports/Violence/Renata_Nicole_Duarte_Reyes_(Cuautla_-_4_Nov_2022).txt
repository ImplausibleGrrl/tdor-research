> She was 29 years old, a trans woman found lifeless, handcuffed and shot in the head, in Cuautla.
> 
> It was during the morning of this Friday when the authorities received the report of the discovery of two dead women, on Pradera street in the Cerritos neighborhood in Cuautla.
> 
> Hours later, the leather was identified as who in life responded to the name Renata Nicole Duarte Reyes, a 29-year-old trans woman.
> 
> Members of the LGBTTTIQ+ community lamented the crime, so the authorities are carrying out the corresponding investigations.
> 
> It is worth mentioning that this event was recorded, after a video went viral on social networks showing a group of armed people, with vests, and who assure that they are Rex's people, and that they will carry out the cleanup in Cuautla .
> 
> It should be noted that Renata was found lifeless, handcuffed and with gunshot wounds to the head, along with another woman, and a threatening message signed by Lieutenant Rex.
