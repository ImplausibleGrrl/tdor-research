> This Tuesday night, the body of a 26-year-old trans woman, identified as Ignacia Palma, appeared completely burned inside a house armed with cardboard, installed near the Francia de Valparaíso metro station .
> 
> The victim, who died from the seriousness of his injuries, was also stabbed, and Movilh Chile said so , after hearing the story of people close to the deceased.
> 
> «Brutal crime: they murder and burn a trans woman in Valparaíso. Enough! Justice for Ignacia Palma. Our solidarity for her family and her colleagues from the Corporación Trans Féminas Diversas en Acción », the organization published on its Instagram account.
> 
> As explained by Police Lieutenant Colonel Juan Escobar, the deceased was found next to the railway line, on the corner of Avenida Errázuriz and Simón Bolívar.
> 
> The woman lived with her mother, but at the time of the discovery she was not in the place, and only in the hours of this afternoon was her whereabouts found.



> Trans woman victim of an attack
> Firefighters and SAMU personnel arrived at the site where Ignacia Palma was found, who transferred the trans woman to the Carlos Van Buren Hospital, with severe burns.
> 
> "A cruel attack yesterday took the life of Ignacia Palma, a 26-year-old trans woman who was stabbed and burned by a group of subjects," denounced Movilh and the Corporación Trans Féminas Diversas en Acción today.
> 
> For his part, the person in charge of Human Rights of the institution, Ramón Gómez, pointed out that "the information is preliminary, but the truth is that third parties intervened in the death."
> 
> Ignacia's death coincides with the birth of Daniel Zamudio, who would be 35 years old today, August 3, 2022. Young man who died after a homophobic attack.



> Santiago de Chile, Aug 3 (EFE) .- The Movement for Homosexual Integration and Liberation (Movilh), the main organization of the LGBTI community in Chile, denounced this Wednesday the murder of Nacha Palma, a young transgender woman who was allegedly stabbed and burned. Live in Valparaiso.
> 
> According to the Movilh background, the 26-year-old woman was attacked in the streets of this coastal city, 100 kilometers from Santiago, and was found alive by firefighters, who took her to a hospital where she died minutes later due to the burns that reached his entire body.
> 
> “Together with the family, we are collecting all the information necessary to deal with this brutal attack that shakes us as people and as activists. So far the information is preliminary, but the truth is that third parties intervened in the death," said Ramón Gómez, the person in charge of human rights at Movilh.
> 
> Palma was stabbed and burned by a group of subjects, detailed the organization, which is defining what legal actions to file for this hate crime.
> 
> The president of the Corporación Corporación Trans Féminas Diversas en Acción, María López, also condemned the death of the young woman: "We demand preventive measures from the Valparaíso authorities against abuse and attacks. For now, we will concentrate on guiding the family" .
