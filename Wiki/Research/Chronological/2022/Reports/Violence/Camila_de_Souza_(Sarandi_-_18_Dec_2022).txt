Camila de Souza died in hospital in Sarandi, Paraná, (Brazil) over the weekend of 17-18 Dec 2022.

She was 29, and had been admitted after being beaten by her brother on 2 Dec 2022.


> THE CIVIL POLICE IS INVESTIGATING THE DEATH OF A TRANSVESTITE WHO DIED THIS WEEKEND IN SARANDI. THE VICTIM WAS LEFT AT THE UPA – EMERGENCY CARE UNIT, ON DECEMBER 2ND, WITH A SERIOUS HEAD INJURY.
> Due to the severity, Camila de Souza, 29, was transferred to Hospital Santa Rita, where she died days later.
> 
> According to information from the incident report, she and her brother got involved in an argument. During the fight, the boy hit Camila in the head with a stick.
> 
> The aggression would have taken place at the family's residence in the Jardim Verão neighborhood. The body was sent to the Legal Medical Institute of Maringá.
> 
> The young Camila de Souza, who was a trans woman, ended up dying at Hospital Santa Rita de Maringá.
> 
> The Civil Police opened an inquiry to investigate the case, as there is a suspicion that she was attacked on December 2 during a family dispute. She was only 29 years old.
> 
> Information about the wake and burial of Camilla's body was not disclosed.
>
