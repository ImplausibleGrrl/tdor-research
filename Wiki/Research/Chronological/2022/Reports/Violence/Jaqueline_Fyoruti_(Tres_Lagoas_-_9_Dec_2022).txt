Jaqueline Fyoruti was stabbed in her home in Três Lagoas, Mato Grosso do Sul (Brazil) on 9 Dec 2022.

She was 49.


A 37 year-old man was suubsequently arrested after documents identifying him were found at the scene of the crime.


> According to unofficial information, the murderer of the transgender, Emerson Aparecido de Lima, known as Jaqueline Fyoruti, 46 years old was arrested in the city of Ilha Solteira, in the state of São Paulo.
> The transgender was killed with multiple stabbings last night, Friday, at her residence in the Interlagos neighborhood, in Three Lagoas.
