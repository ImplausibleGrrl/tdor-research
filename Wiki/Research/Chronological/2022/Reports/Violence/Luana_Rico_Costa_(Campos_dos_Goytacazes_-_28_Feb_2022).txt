> Suspect of involvement in the murder of a transvestite is detained in Campos
> The suspect was not arrested as there was no longer the act, he was heard and released from the police unit.
> March 02, 2022 | 17:31
> 
> A homeless man suspected of murdering a transvestite was detained by Civil Police and Municipal Civil Guard (GCM) agents on Avenida José Alves de Azevedo, close to Hospital Manoel Cartucho, in the center of Campos dos Santos. Goytacazes.
> 
> According to information from GCM, in diligence the accused and four more witnesses were taken to the 134th Police Station of the Center.
> 
> According to information gathered by  NF Notícias , together with the Civil Police, the suspect was not arrested because there was no longer the act, he was heard and released from the police unit. The next steps will now be to hear witnesses who attend the site.
> 
> Police officers from the 134th Precinct of the Center, investigate the crime.
> 
> The body of the victim, identified as Luana Riço Costa, who lives on the streets, was found last Monday (28) with signs of violence and blood marks, also at José Alves de Azevedo near Hospital Manoel Cartucho. The death would have occurred after the disagreement.


> VIDEO: Police find suspect in transvestite's death
> Victim lived on the streets, as did the accused; body of a transvestite, who was in a wheelchair, was found last Monday (28)
> 02/03/2022, 14:28, Photo: Filipe Lemos/Campos 24 Hours .
> 
> The Civil Police, with the support of the Campos Municipal Guard, arrested in the early afternoon of this Wednesday (02), a man suspected of involvement in the death of a transvestite identified as Luana Riço Costa, after a disagreement . The body of the transvestite, who was also a wheelchair user, was found on the morning of last Monday (28), on the banks of Beira Valão, on Avenida José Alves de Azevedo, in the Central area of Campos.
> 
> According to police information, the body was found without signs of violence, but many blood marks were found on the sidewalk. The body was removed to the Legal Medical Institute (IML), confirming the identification to be Márcio Riço Costa, a transsexual named Luana Riço Costa. The determining cause of death was due to beatings. (read more below)
> 
> After investigations and surveys, the police arrived at the man who was possibly involved in Luana's death. Victim was homeless, as was the accused. (read more below)
> 
> The man was found this afternoon, being sent to provide clarification at the 134th DP / Center. Man was released after testifying. The motive for the crime has not yet been released by the police.
