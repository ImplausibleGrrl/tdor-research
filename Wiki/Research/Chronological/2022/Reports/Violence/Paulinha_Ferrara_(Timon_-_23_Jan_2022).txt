Paulinha das Plantas was brutally killed in the centre of Timon, Maranhão (Brazil) on 23 Jan 2022.

She had been beaten around the head with stones and stabbed. Her body was found naked from the waist down and with a piece of wood stuffed into her mouth.

A 30 year old suspect was subsequently arrested.

> A transvestite identified only as Paulinha was brutally murdered with stones and stab wounds this Sunday afternoon, the 23rd, on Rua Firmino Pedreira, near Praça Higino Cunha, in the center of Timon, in Maranhão.
> 
> According to Captain Tyciane Ibiapina, of the 11th Military Police Battalion, the victim was found without her underwear and with a piece of wood in her mouth. The crime scene was cordoned off by the military police garrison.
> 
> The Criminal Forensics carried out the survey of the first information at the crime scene and the body was removed by the Institute of Legal Medicine (IML).
> 
> The police have no clues about the perpetrators of the murder and the motivation for the crime. The case is being investigated by the Department of Homicide and Protection of Persons of the Civil Police of Maranhão.




> Paulinha's body was found naked from the waist down.
> 
> Known as Paulinha das Plantas, she was brutally killed this Sunday (23) in a place known as “Rua dos Cachorros”, located in the center of the city of Timon (MA).
> 
> According to popular reports, Paulinha was stoned to death, with most of the aggressions to the head and a piece of wood placed in her mouth. The body was found naked from the waist down.
> 
> The Military Police responded to the incident, isolated the place and called in a team from the Legal Medical Institute (IML), which has already removed the body. So far it is not possible to say the reason for the crime, if it was committed by one or more criminals. The case will be investigated by the Civil Police of Maranhão.
> 
> Taking into account the brutality of Paulinha's death, it is understood as a hate crime. According to the National Association of Transvestites and Transsexuals (Antra), in the first half of 2021 alone, about 80 transgender people were killed.

Paulinha sold plants for a living.
