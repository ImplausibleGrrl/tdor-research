The body OF Gabrielly Monteiro was found with stab wounds in an apartment in São Luís, Maranhão (Brazil) on 22 Feb 2022.


> Police search for suspect in murder of transvestite inside kitchenette in São Luís
> The body of the victim, Gabrielly Monteiro, identified late yesterday afternoon, was found on Tuesday morning (22).
> 
> Gabrielly Monteiro's body was found on Tuesday (22), inside a kitchenette (Photo: Disclosure)
> 
> The Civil Police, through the Department of Femicide of Maranhão, is looking for Edmundo Silva Pereira, the main suspect in the murder of a transvestite , in the Jardim das Margaridas, in São Luís. Gabrielly Monteiro's body was found inside the kitchenette neighborhood where the man lived, on the morning of last Tuesday (22).
> 
> According to delegate Wanda Moura, head of the Department and responsible for investigating the case, the arrest of the author has already been requested and steps are also being taken, in order to locate him more quickly. He has not been seen at the scene since the crime.
> 
> According to the delegate, through security cameras in the area, the police obtained an image of the victim going to the kitchenette on Sunday (20). Gabrielly was found naked and with her body in an advanced state of decomposition.
> 
> In addition to the victim, the police found a knife smeared with blood and a bag, but without any documents. No one in the place knew her.
> 
> The police only managed to find out her name this Friday afternoon (25), with the help of photos that circulated on social networks and which made it possible to contact a family member.
> 
> To Jornal Pequeno, the delegate informed that among the lines of investigation is a possible drug debt that the victim allegedly had and may have motivated the crime. “We will also investigate this aspect”, she pointed out.




The victim was found dead inside a quitinete (a small apartment) on the morning of Tuesday 22nd February. Her identity was not reported.

> The body of a [trans woman] was found at the end of the morning of this Tuesday, 22, in a [quitinete](https://pt.wikipedia.org/wiki/Quitinete) on Rua Projetada, in the Jardim das Margaridas neighborhood of São Luís.
> 
> According to the police, a woman found the body covered in blood, in a room that belongs to a resident identified as Edmundo Pereira. The unidentified victim was not recognized by anyone at the scene. Popular reported that the resident has not been seen in the quitinete since last Saturday, the 19th.
> 
> According to the police, a check revealed that the resident has a criminal record for domestic violence dated 06/17/2020 and a crime of extortion, registered on 06/13/2020, both at the Raposa police station.
