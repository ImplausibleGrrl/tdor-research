> A 45-year-old transvestite, identified as Jerry, was shot dead when she was sitting in front of her family's residence in the city of Coreaú, in the interior of Ceará, on Thursday night (10).
> 
> According to the Secretariat of Public Security and Social Defense (SSPDS), teams from the Civil Police and the Military Police were called to the incident. Officers search the area to try to locate the suspects.
> 
> According to the folder, the victim has a history of threat and traffic crime. The case is investigated by the Coreaú Municipal Police Station.
