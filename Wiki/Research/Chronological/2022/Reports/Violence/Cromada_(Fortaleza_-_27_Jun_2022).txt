Chromada was shot in Fortaleza, Ceará (Brazil) on 27 Jun 2022.

She was 32.



> Transvestite friend of Silvero Pereira is murdered in Fortaleza on the eve of LGBTQIA+ Pride Day
> Crimes occurred, respectively, in the Messeja and Álvaro Weyne neighborhoods. 'Rarely have I met such a good person, so dear', said the actor Silvero Pereira in a social network.

> Transvestite known as "Chromada" was murdered in Fortaleza.  — Photo: Reproduction
> 
> A transvestite was shot dead in Fortaleza on Monday night (27), the eve of LGBTQIA+ Pride Day, a date celebrated this Tuesday (28) that aims to raise awareness of the importance of combating homophobia. The victim was a friend of actor Silvero Pereira, who mourned the death in a message posted on a social network.
> 
> The crime took place in a bar on Avenida Washington Soares, in the Messejana neighborhood. The victim, known as "Chromada", 32, was sitting in a chair on the sidewalk of the establishment when she was hit by the shots and died at the scene.
> 
> According to the Secretary of Public Security and Social Defense (SSPDS) of Ceará, teams from the Military Police and the Forensic Expertise of the State of Ceará (Pefoce) were at the scene. The case will be investigated by the 3rd Precinct, Department of Homicide and Protection of the Person (DHPP), of the Civil Police.
> 
> Silvero Pereira, who plays Zaquieu in the soap opera Pantanal, published messages of condolence for his friend's death.
> 
> "They killed my friend Cromada. Transvestite, black, poor, peripheral. We haven't seen each other for a while, we haven't talked to each other, but few times I've met such a good person, so dear. Cromada was a victim of a racist, transphobic, unequal system. if she had been socially accepted, she would never have made decisions that would lead to such tragedy," Silvero wrote.
> 
> The actor also shared images of the music video for the song "Lady Cromada", by the band Veronica Decides to die, made in honor of a few years ago, in honor of the victim.
> 
> In another case, also on the night before the LGBTQIAP+ Pride Day, another transvestite, whose identity was not informed, was the victim of a beating on Rua Teodomiro de Castro, in the Álvaro Weyne neighborhood, also in the capital. information about the motive of the crime.
> 
> The Secretary of Security says the police are investigating the case. "According to the first police investigations based on the reports of witnesses, the victim was allegedly thrown from a moving vehicle by three individuals", the folder says in a statement.



> Transvestite friend of Silvero Pereira is murdered in Fortaleza
> Crime happened the day before LGBTQIA+ Pride Day
> Published June 28, 2022
>   
> A 32-year-old transvestite, identified as "Cromada", died after being shot on Monday night (27) in a bar in the Messejana neighborhood, in Fortaleza, Ceará. The crime happened a day before the LGBTQIA+ Pride Day celebrated this Tuesday (28). 
> 
> Cromada was a personal friend of actor Silvero Pereira, who plays Zaquieu in TV Globo's soap opera Pantanal. Through her social media, Pereira, who fights for the fight against homophobia, regretted what happened and criticized the social shelter system for LGBTQIA + people.
> 
> They killed my friend Chrome. Transvestite, black, poor, peripheral. It's been a while since we've seen each other, nor spoken to each other, but rarely have I met such a good person, such a dear one. Chrome was the victim of a racist, transphobic, unequal system. Certainly if she had been socially accepted, she would never have made decisions that would lead to such tragedy.
> Silvero PereiraActor
>   
> According to information, Cromada was sitting in a chair on the sidewalk of the bar when she was hit by shots, dying on the spot. Teams of the Military Police and Forensic Experts of the State of Ceará (Pefoce) were called to the scene. The case is being investigated by the 3rd Precinct, Department of Homicide and Protection of the Person (DHPP), of the Civil Police. 
> 
> Also on Monday (27), another transvestite, whose identity was not revealed, was the victim of a beating in Fortaleza. The victim would have been thrown from a moving vehicle by three individuals, according to the Municipal Security Department.
> 
> The number of cases of intentional homicide against LGBTQIA+ people increased by 7.2% in 2021 compared to 2020, according to the Brazilian Public Security Yearbook, released this Tuesday (28).
> 
> There were 179 deaths recorded last year against 167 in 2020. The number of intentional bodily harm rose by 35.2%, growing from 1,271 to 1,719, and rape increased to 88.4%, from 95 to 179 cases.
> 
> The Yearbook also showed that cases of racism against homosexuals and transsexuals rose 147% in 2021 compared to 2020. Homophobia was included as a crime of racism in 2019, after a decision by the Federal Supreme Court (STF). There are 448 assaults, 84 rapes and 12 homicides more than in 2020.
> 
> According to researcher at the Brazilian Forum of Public Security (FBSP) Dennis Pacheco, the increase in notifications can mean greater confidence on the part of victims in making a complaint. 
> 
> One of the hypotheses is that the increase in public debate on the subject has led to an increase in registrations, because of a feeling that there could be adequate processing of the complaint by public security institutions.
>  
> The states of São Paulo, Rio de Janeiro, Minas Gerais, Bahia, Maranhão and Rio Grande do Sul did not present data for 2020 and 2021.

> 
> According to information from the police teams that attended the event, the 32-year-old victim was hit by firearm shots, on a public road, and could not resist the injuries. Teams from the Military Police of Ceará (PMCE) and the Forensic Expert of the State of Ceará (Pefoce) were also at the scene. A police procedure was initiated in the DHPP and investigations of the case are in charge of the 3rd Precinct of the DHPP.
