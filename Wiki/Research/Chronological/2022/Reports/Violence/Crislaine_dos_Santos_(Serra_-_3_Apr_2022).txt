Crislaine dos Santos was shto in Serro, Espirito Santo (Brazil) on 3 Apr 2022.

She was 45.

> The trans woman was found dead on Leôncio Monjardim Street, in the Guaraciaba neighborhood, in Serra. Credit: Oliveira Alves/TV Gazeta
> A trans woman was found dead with several gunshot marks on her face, on Leôncio Monjardim Street, in the Guaraciaba neighborhood, in  Serra . The incident took place on Sunday night (3). As determined by reporter Kaique Dias, from TV Gazeta , victim Crislaine dos Santos, 45, lived alone in the community.
> 
> Residents reported hearing about five shots. A housewife who owns a farm on the street where the woman was found said that her grandchildren were playing in the backyard at the time of the incident.
> 
> “My granddaughter started screaming for her mother, the little one too. I was terrified, I've never seen it around here”, reported Maria Valda de Andrade.
> 
> Residents of the community called  the Military Police  and passed on characteristics of the suspect who had shot. According to an investigation by TV Gazeta , when they arrived at the scene of the crime, the military found a man similar to the one narrated by the people. At the approach, he showed restlessness and was taken to the Regional Police Station of Serra, to provide explanations.
> 
> The victim had lived in the neighborhood for about 10 years. An acquaintance of hers who did not want to be identified described the woman as a wonderful person who, recently, had started drinking and using a lot of drugs. 
> 
> Also according to the source heard by the report, Crislaine had returned to identify herself as Cristiano and even altered documents, but had regretted it.
> 
> “She found out that she had a son in Colatina and it seems that she saw this son. He seems to have charged her that he didn't want her as a woman. But she said she wasn't happy. I told her to do what would make her feel happy," said the source, who declined to be identified.
> 
> In a note sent to the TV Gazeta report , the Civil Police reported that the suspect who was 18 years old was heard and released, as the police authority understood that there were not enough elements to carry out the act in the act. The case will be forwarded to the Police Homicide and Protection of Women (DHPM).



> Woman shot to death in Sierra
> 
> A woman was found dead with gunshot wounds to her face , on the night of this Sunday (3), on a corner in Serra , in Grande Vitória .
> 
> The crime took place on Rua Leôncio Monjardim, in the Guaraciaba neighborhood. The victim was identified as Crislaine dos Santos, 45 years old. She lived alone in the same neighborhood . Residents said they heard about five shots.
> 
> The housewife Maria Valda Paris Andrade, who has a farm on the street where the crime took place, said that her grandchildren were playing in the backyard at the time of the action .
> 
> "My granddaughter almost had a stroke, she was screaming, crying. 'Grandma, I've never seen that! I want my mother'. She started screaming. I was terrified. I've never seen that around here", said the housewife. .

> Residents called the Military Police and reported the characteristics of the man who had shot. When they arrived at the crime scene, in addition to finding the woman's body and the residents involved, the military saw a man in a red cap and gray shirt with the same characteristics described by the residents .
> 
> The man was approached and became restless, according to police. He was taken to provide clarification at the Serra Regional Police Station .
> 
> According to the Civil Police, the suspect is 18 years old, was heard and released after the police authority understood that there were no elements for arrest in the act .
> 
> Neighbors said Crislaine had lived in the neighborhood for about 10 years.
> 
> "Crislaine is a wonderful person. I met her in 2011, I welcomed her, and she was always running after her, working. But she came to the end, now, drinking a lot, using a lot of drugs, doing these crazy programs of hers", he reported.
> 
> Crislaine was a trans woman and had transitioned 10 years ago . Recently, she would have re-identified herself as Cristiano, came to alter the documents, but regretted it, according to the woman who knew her.
> 
> The case will be investigated by the Specialized Division of Homicide and Protection of Women (DHPM). No suspects were arrested .
