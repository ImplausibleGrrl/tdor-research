> A young trans man was shot to death in El Bagre, The victim known as: "Riqui pa Riqui" died in a hospital in Montería
> 
> A young transgender man was murdered from several gunshot wounds in a rural area of the El Torbe sector in the municipality of El Bagre, in Bajo Cauca, Antioquia. 
> 
> The victim known to his friends: "Riqui pa Riqui", was transferred to the Nuestra Señora de El Carmen hospital, where due to injuries he was sent to the city of Montería, where he died. 
> 
> Regarding the facts, the authorities reported that investigations have already begun in order to find out who is responsible for the crime.
> 
> On the other hand, the Mayor's Office of Medellín did not hide its concern for the homicide, in these three months, of eight members of this population. President Daniel Quintero assured that the investigations would intensify
