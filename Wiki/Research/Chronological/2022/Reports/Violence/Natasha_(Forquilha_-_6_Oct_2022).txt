> Transvestite is murdered in the interior of the state
> Natasha, 27 years old, was murdered in Forquilha, 229 km from Fortaleza. The crime happened last Thursday (6), Natasha was found dead in a pubic street.
> 
> According to the Secretariat of Public Security and Social Defense, teams from the Military Police, the Civil Police and a team from the Forensic Expertise were called to the scene and carried out the first surveys on the case.
> 
> A witness told the Military Police that he heard gunshots around 3 am and, when he left the house to leave the woman at work, around 6 am, he found the victim's body on the street. The crime is investigated by the Municipal Police of the municipality.
