Calle Cardenal Caro con Puente Patronato, Parque Forestal
Recoleta
Santiago
Chile
stabbed
 

> Person is killed with a knife in the vicinity of the Forest Park
> A homicide was recorded during the early hours of this Tuesday in the Parque Forestal, commune of Santiago.
> 
> The fact, according to information from Carabineros, originated in Cardenal Caro Street with Patronato Bridge.
> 
> In that place, police personnel found an adult woman, "apparently a street situation", with a stab wound to the back. She would be a trans woman.
> 
> Carabineros, who realized that the person had low vital signs, requested the presence of Samu. However, the victim died at the scene due to the severity of his injuries.
> 
> The prosecution ordered that the Labocar and the OS9 of Carabineros investigate the case. Prosecutor Luis Salazar, from the North Central area, was established at the scene of the event.
> 
> A knife was found at the scene that would have been the weapon used in the crime, according to Channel 13.
