Valeria was shot in Almoloya de Juárez, Estado de México (Mexico) on 29 Sep 2022.



Valeria was shot multiple times by a group of armed men on the night of Thursday 29th September.

> This Thursday afternoon-night, a [trans woman] known as Valeria was shot dead in Rancho San Juan, Almoloya de Juárez. Neighbors reported a shooting to the municipal authorities, on the outskirts of this subdivision.
> 
> According to witness accounts, armed men fired at least five times at a person who was walking by the entrance to the well-known urban complex, when she received bullet impacts.
> 
> The merchants reported the events to the emergency numbers, arriving municipal security elements to comb the area, in addition to the emergency services, who transferred the victim to the hospital with serious injuries.
> 
> The assailants fled in an unknown direction, leaving her lying on the street. Valeria was dedicated to the promotion of telephone companies and she lived in this subdivision, according to her own neighbors de ella who recognized her immediately.
