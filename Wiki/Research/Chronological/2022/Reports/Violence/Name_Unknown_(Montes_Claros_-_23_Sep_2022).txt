> Transvestite is found dead inside her house, in Montes Claros, with several head injuries
> According to the PM, a man identified himself as the author of the crime; he was arrested.
> By Michelly Oda, g1 Grande Minas
> 09/23/2022 22:39
> 
> A transvestite was found dead inside her house, in Montes Claros , with several head injuries this Friday (23). A 30-year-old man was arrested.
> 
> “We received the information from the author himself, he committed the crime and went to the police station, where he confessed to the crime. With the information in hand, the Military Police carried out the arrest in the act and made the report", explained Major Reginaldo Ribeiro de Aguiar.
> 
> According to the police officer, the man stated that he had been living in the victim's house for two weeks, after leaving his parents' residence. There is no information on how they met.
> 
> “The victim insisted on having sex with him and he refused. In possession of a pestle hand, he made several blows against the victim, hitting him in the head ", completes the major.
> 
> The PM called Samu, who found the transvestite's death. The Civil Police expertise was also called. The case will be investigated.
