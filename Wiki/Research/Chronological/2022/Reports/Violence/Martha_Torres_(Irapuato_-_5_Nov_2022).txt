> Security in Irapuato: They find a trans woman murdered in Las Heras; they suspect a partner
> Lomelí T., a trans woman, was found dead inside her house in the Las Heras neighborhood, in Irapuato; it is suspected that she could have been killed by her partner
> Security in Irapuato: They find a trans woman murdered in Las Heras;  they suspect a partner
> Lomelí was found dead inside her house in the Las Heras neighborhood, in Irapuato. Photo: Special
> Irapuato
> Sunday, November 6, 2022 22:48
> Irapuato, Guanajuato.- This Sunday afternoon, a trans woman identified as Lomelí T. was found dead inside a house in the Las Heras neighborhood , in Irapuato ; it is suspected that her partner could have murdered her.
> 
> Relatives of Lomelí went to her house to look for her at her house on Calle Nardo, in the Las Heras neighborhood, since they had not heard from her for several days and she did not answer their calls either.
> 
> Inside the house they noticed a fetid odor, so they immediately called 911 to request the support of the authorities.
> 
> Municipal police officers accompanied the relatives to enter the house, where they found the deceased trans woman under a staircase, so they immediately secured the house.
> 
> The family had to leave the place that was cordoned off for several meters around by municipal police.
> 
> Paramedics arrived at the scene, but could only confirm the death of the trans woman identified as Lomelí T.
> 
> They suspect that she could have been murdered by a partner
> Versions in the place suspect that Lomelí could have been murdered by her partner, since they consider him an aggressive person and a consumer of some drugs.
> 
> However, authorities reported that so far the cause of the trans woman's death is unknown.
> 
> Experts in criminology and personnel from the Criminal Investigation Agency arrived at the site to begin the corresponding proceedings.
> 
> The victim's body was transferred in an ambulance to perform the necropsy of law and determine exactly the causes of death.
> 
> The Regional State Prosecutor's Office itself will be in charge of clarifying the facts and reporting on the progress of the investigations.
