The body of Renata Ferraz/Renata Cavalcante was found on a side road near the PB-272 highway in São José de Espinharas, Paraíba (Brazil) on the afternoon of 19 Apr 2022.

She was 16.



> A 16-year-old transsexual teenager was found dead on the afternoon of this Tuesday (19), on a side road, on the banks of PB-272, outside the city of São José de Espinharas, in the Patos region, in the Sertão of Paraíba. According to the Civil Police, the body was found around 2 pm. 
> 
> Renata Ferraz had been missing since last Saturday (16th) and the family registered a report at the police station this Monday (18th). The victim's body was recognized by her father. Due to the advanced state of putrefaction, the teenager's body was taken to the municipality of Cajazeiras, where an expertise will be carried out. 
> 
> According to delegate Carlos Seabra, the motivations have not yet been established, but it was possibly a crime. Still according to the delegate, it is still not possible to say if it would have been a homophobic crime, as it still needs to wait for the cadaveric report to know the causes of death.
> 
> "Since the fact was reported, the Patos Homicide and Narcotics Police Station has been investigating, listening to witnesses and seeking other evidence to quickly be able to elucidate the crime, the motives, the authorship and the circumstances that occurred. the crime" , said the delegate of the region, Carlos Seabra. 
> 
> In a note, the LGBTQIA+ representative of Patos, Sheyla Ferreira, mourned the death of Renata and said that the class deserves respect and deserves to live.
