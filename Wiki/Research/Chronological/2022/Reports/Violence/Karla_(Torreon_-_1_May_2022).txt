Karla was stabbed in her home in Torreón, Coahuila (Mexico) on 1 May 2022.


> Torreón, Coahuila.- This Saturday the Coahuila State Prosecutor's Office confirmed the death of a trans woman, whose official documents identify him as Carlos "N", inside her home in the Pedregal del Valle neighborhood of this city.
> 
> Through social networks, the outrage caused by the violent death of Karla went viral, who, following the information provided by authorities, had multiple cuts caused by a sharp weapon.
> 
> The victim's body was found in a room on a half-naked chair in the dorsal decubitus position, that is, lying down with his head looking at the ceiling.
> 
> The remains of the deceased today were transferred to the Forensic Medical Service (Semefo) to determine the causes of death through a necropsy of law.
> 
> Through social networks, members of the LGBT+ community demand the investigation of the case and justice for Karla.
