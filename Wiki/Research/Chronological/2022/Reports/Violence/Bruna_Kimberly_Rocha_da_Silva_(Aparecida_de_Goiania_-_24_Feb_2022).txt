Bruna Kimberly Rocha da Silva was shot in Aparecida de Goiânia, Goiás (Brazil) on 24 Feb 2022.

She was 27.



Bruna was shot in the street.

> A [trans woman] was shot dead in Aparecida de Goiânia on the morning of this Thursday (24).
> 
> The victim was found dead on Rua Pará, in the Nossa Senhora de Lourdes sector, in the motels region, and the Military Police were called to secure the scene.
> 
> According to information the corpse had 4 gunshot wounds through the body, chest, neck and back. Witnesses reported that the perpetrators arrived on a motorcycle. As the vehicle approached the victim, the passenger fired shots and the driver accelerated as they left.
