> A trans woman is stabbed to death in Largo do Tanque, in Salvador
> Essay,
> 26/07/2022 | 3:07 pm
> Student Deyse Anne Diógenes França Santos, a 32-year-old transsexual woman, was stabbed to death in the late morning of Tuesday (26), in the Largo do Tanque neighborhood, in Salvador.
> 
> The victim participated in a preparatory course for completing high school at the Center for the Promotion and Defense of LGBT Rights in the State of Bahia (CPDD-LGBT).
> 
> According to friends of the victim, her boyfriend did not agree with Deyse returning to the classroom. Also according to her friends in the class, she was on her way to class when she was murdered by two strangers who were on a motorcycle.
> 
> After being hit, she was taken to the Ernesto Simões Filho General Hospital (HGESF), in the Pau Miúdo neighborhood, but she arrived dead at the health unit.
> 
> The CPDD-LGBT, equipment of the Secretariat of Justice, Human Rights and Social Development of Bahia (SJDHDS), run by the Conceição Macedo Benefit Institution (IBCM), is providing support to the victim's family, in addition to calling the Civil Police and the Public Ministry. to handle the case.

> A trans woman was brutally murdered with a knife at a bus stop in Largo do Tanque, Salvador.
