Larissa was shot in Vitória da Conquista, Bahia (Brazil) on the night of 14 Feb 2022.



Larissa was shot while walking through a residential area on the night of Monday 14th February.

> A [trans woman] was shot dead in Vitória da Conquista, in the South Center of Bahia, on Monday night (14). According to information collected by the ADNERSON BLOG, the victim, known as Larissa, was walking through Residencial Lago Azul in the Campinho neighborhood, when she was attacked. After being shot with a revolver she fell to the ground and was rescued by the Mobile Emergency Care Service, but she couldn't resist her injuries.
