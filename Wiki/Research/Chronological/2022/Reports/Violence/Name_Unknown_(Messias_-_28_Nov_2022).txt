> Video: Transvestite and companion are charred in bed
> The family did not know the possible motive for the death of the two.
> Tucumã newsroom
> 11/30/2022
> 
> Brazil – Last Monday (28), the Military Police of Alagoas was called to attend to the case of two bodies, including a transvestite, charred in a house at Fazenda Prazeres, in the rural area of ​​the municipality of Messias. The victims were a transvestite and his partner, found near the bed burned by the fire.
> 
> The victims were identified as Adriano Cardoso and José Amaro, and witnesses point out that the transvestite was with his partner when they were found completely charred on the spot in the morning.
> 
> The suspicion is that the room was set on fire during the night and because it is an isolated place, it was not reported at the time of the event, but only the next day. The family did not know how to inform the possible motive for the death of the two.
> 
> A video recorded on site and shared on social media shows the situation in which the room was left after the incident, with the walls and floor in ashes due to the fire at the site. The body of one of the victims without clothes appears burned as well.
