> On the afternoon of this Friday (13/05) another scene of violence was recorded in the city of Lagoa Grande, in the Sertão de Pernambuco.
> 
> According to information passed on to the Blog Notícias em Destaque, a young transsexual was murdered in the invasion, known as Lixão, in the Red District.
> 
> According to popular reports, the victim identified as Bruna, was shot by firearms, and died on the spot.
> 
> The Military Police and the IML (Forensic Medical Institute) were called to attend the scene and carry out all the usual procedures.
> 
> For now, the authorship and motivations of the crime are unknown.
> 
> The case must be investigated by the Civil Police.
