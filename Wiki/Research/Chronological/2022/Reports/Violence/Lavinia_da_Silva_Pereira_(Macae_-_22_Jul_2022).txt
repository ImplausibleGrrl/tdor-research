> TRANS IS MURDERED BY SHOT IN ASSAULT IN RJ
> NEWS
>  On Friday, July 22, 2022
> Trans is shot dead during robbery in Macaé
> 
> A transvestite was shot dead during an attempted robbery in the early hours of this Friday (22) in the Aroeira neighborhood, in Macaé.
> 
> According to information, the victim, identified as Layo Wenderson da Silva Pereira, 25, tried to run away from the criminals on Rua Clodomiro Faustino da Cruz, but ended up being shot at.
> 
> The young woman was rescued to the Municipal Public Hospital (HPM), but could not resist the injuries, going to death.
> 
> According to the Legal Medical Institute (IML), the cause of death was internal abdominal hemorrhage due to injury to the great vessels of the abdomen and a transfixing wound, caused by blunt force action.
> 
> The occurrence was registered at the 123rd Police Station (123rd DP) and no one was arrested.
