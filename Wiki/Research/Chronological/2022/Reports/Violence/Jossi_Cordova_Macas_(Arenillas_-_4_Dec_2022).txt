Jossi Córdova Macas was shot in Arenillas, El Oro (Ecuador) on 4 Dec 2022.

She was 21.



> Hooded subjects murdered a trans woman while she slept.
> 
> The event occurred in the Arenillas canton , El Oro province , in the early hours of Sunday, November 4, 2022.
> 
> The criminals, who were traveling in a van, broke into the home of Ronaldo Rubén Córdova Macas, 21, known as Jossi, around 3:00 a.m., according to what the Police learned.
> 
> Córdova, who identified as a transgender girl , received two bullet wounds , one to the head and the other to the neck, according to a police report.
> 
> In the violent event, another person was injured and was transferred to a health home.
> 
> Dinased agents carried out the procedure for the removal of the corpse.
> 
> Police are investigating the fact. They do not rule out that the victim had personal problems.
> 
> Jossi was well known in the San Francisco de Asís neighborhood of Arenillas.
> 
> Another similar fact
> 
> Daniela Demera Zambrano, approximately 37 years old, was murdered while she was sleeping at a sister's house.
> 
> This event occurred on July 28 at around 2:00 a.m., in El Carmen, Manabí province.
> 
> Three subjects entered the house violently to kill the woman.
