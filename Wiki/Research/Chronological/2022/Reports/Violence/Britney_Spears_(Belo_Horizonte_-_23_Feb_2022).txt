> Body of transvestite is found in the Nazaré neighborhood.
>
> A travesti was stabbed to death and left on the banks of Anel Rodoviario, in the region of the Nazaré 
neighborhood, in Belo Horizonte. The victim, known as Britney Spears, was found this morning Wednesday (23), but the suspects and the motive for the crime are still unknown. According to witnesses, the person who abandoned the body on the spot was in a red vehicle.
>
> "Preconception is a social problem and, often, it causes people like transvestites to fail regular jobs and travel to life at night to ensure their livelihood. They need opportunities so they don't have to expose themselves to so much violence! ”, commented @alvarodamiao .
