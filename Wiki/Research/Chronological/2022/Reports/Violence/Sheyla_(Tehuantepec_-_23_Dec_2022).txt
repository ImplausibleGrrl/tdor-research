> It was a trans girl, the deceased of Casablanca |  The Impartial of Oaxaca
> After the macabre discovery of the lifeless body on a dirt road located on the side of the transisthmian highway, Tehuantepec Juchitán section, right in the place known as Casablanca, it was determined that the corpse corresponds to a trans girl.
> 
> As will be recalled, the events took place last Friday morning when peasants passing through the area noticed the body thrown away, it was itself handcuffed, for which they immediately reported it to the corresponding authorities.
> 
> After the personnel of the State Investigation Agency (AEI) carried out the corresponding procedures, the body was identified as Víctor AL, originally from the municipality of Salina Cruz, residing in Santo Domingo Tehuantepec, who was known by the nickname of Sheyla, who claimed to be a transsexual girl.
> 
> According to the first reports, the now deceased remained missing for several days, and it was until yesterday that his relatives managed to identify him, who will request the delivery of his body to bury him according to their religious beliefs.
> 
> In the case, an investigation file was opened against the person or persons responsible for the crime that caused consternation among the lesbian-gay community of the Isthmus of Tehuantepec.
