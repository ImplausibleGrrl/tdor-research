> July 11, 2022.  This morning, July 11, 2022, a trans woman was murdered in the Guineo township, in the rural area of ​​Chigorodó. Antioch. The victim was a visible trans woman, 27 years old, recognized in the territory as a dancer; Her body was found with apparent signs of torture, according to preliminary information.

This is the second femicide against a trans woman that occurs in Antioquia in less than a week, since on July 7 in the afternoon the lifeless body of Isabella Peña was found on the banks of a river, who had been reported missing. from July 3.
