Júlia dos Santos Silva was shot in the street in Palmares, Pernambuco (Brazil) on the night of 11 Feb 2022.

She was 23.



> A transvestite was shot dead on Friday night (11), in the Santo Rosa neighborhood, in the municipality of Palmares, in the Mata Sul region of Pernambuco.
> 
> According to the police, victim Julio César dos Santos Silva, 23, known as “Júlia”, was on Alves da Silva street when an unknown person on a motorcycle with an unmarked color and license plate fired several shots from firearm against Júlia who could not resist the injuries and died on a public road.
> 
> The team from the Special Division of Homicide Investigation (DEAH) of the Civil Police was at the scene to make the first surveys. It was found that the victim is an ex-convict and is responding to several criminal proceedings, including a sentenced for drug trafficking and suspected of dealing drugs in the locality.
> 
> After carrying out the cadaveric survey, the body was sent to the Institute of Legal Medicine (IML) in Recife.



> A transvestite was shot dead in Palmares, in the Mata Sul region of Pernambuco, on Friday night (11).
> 
> According to the Civil Police, the victim, known as Julia, was on Rua Alvez da Silva, in the Santa Rosa neighborhood, when a suspect approached and shot her. She did not resist her injuries and died at the scene.
> 
> The motivation and authorship are unknown, at the time of this publication. The Civil Police is investigating the case.
