> The Civil Police informs that the body of the young transsexual Amani Frieden was found. She had been missing since April 3rd.
> 
> According to police information, a body was found on the stones of the Coroa do Meio neighborhood on April 5 and was taken without identification to the Legal Medical Institute. After several steps, Depatri handed over documents and other information for the experts to carry out tests that could identify the body.
> 
> The expert report pointed to the identification of the victim and showed that Amani's cause of death was drowning. The disappearance of the young trans woman has mobilized social networks in recent days. The Civil Police carried out several steps in an attempt to find her, but unfortunately, the young woman had died two days after she disappeared.
