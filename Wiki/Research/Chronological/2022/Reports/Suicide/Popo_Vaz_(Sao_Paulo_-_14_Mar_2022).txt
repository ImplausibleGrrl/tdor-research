> Civil police officer Paulo Vaz, 36, died on Monday night (14), according to the National Association of Transvestites and Transsexuals (Antra). Paulo became known for being one of the few transgender men who worked in the police.
> 
> The causes of death were not reported.
> 
> Share on WhatsApp
> Share on Telegram
> "We just found out that @popo_vaz left us. Unfortunately we lost another one of us who couldn't stand to continue in such a violent and inhuman society. Thank you for everything! We will continue in mourning, in the fight. It's not time to speculate about Paulo's death . Respect the pain of those who lost a friend, husband, son and brother. It's time to silence and reflect. We need to think of ways to build a world where people want to live", said the association on a social network.
> 
> Paulo Vaz was one of the security agents who used social media to support military police officer Leandro Prior, the PM who was the target of homophobic attacks for appearing in a video in uniform kissing another man on the mouth in the São Paulo Metro in 2018. married to youtuber PedroHMC.
> 
> Non-binary, transvestite and more: members of the LGBTQIA+ community explain terms
> 
> He was a Civil Police investigator since April 2018, in the Greater São Paulo region. In 2018, he gave an interview to g1 about the importance of supporting and inspiring more trans men to enter the police career. "I thought I would encounter many barriers, but I was quite happy and surprised with the reception of my colleagues from the beginning. I already knew that there is a difference between the Military Police and Civil Police institutions, but I was quite surprised".
> 
> "I started the hormoneization process, which is taking testosterone, two years ago. There are some ways to take testosterone, in the form of gel, ointment, oral and injected. The body needs a peak of testosterone to masculinize. injected because it makes a difference faster. Beard characteristics begin to appear, voice changes. And I also had breast removal surgery", he said at the time.
> On social media, politicians mourned the policeman's death. "With great sadness I receive the news of the departure of dear Popó Vaz. Activist and transsexual influencer, he made a decisive contribution to the LGBT fight against all forms of prejudice. My hugs to family and friends. May God comfort us", said Senator Fabiano Contact (PT).
> 
> Trans people live under 'fragile tolerance' , says researcher who accounted for 140 deaths in 2021
> Councilor Erika Hilton (PSOL) also commented on the influencer's death. "I have just received the news of the death of Paulo Vaz and I am devastated. He was very dear and it is a sadness that he has left us. I wish a lot of strength and solidarity for the whole family and everyone at this moment".


> Paulo Vaz, a trans man who is a reference in the LGBTQIA+ community and a civil police officer , died on the night of this Monday, 14th . Paulo was married to Pedro HMC founder of the famous LGBTQIA+ channel, “ Põe Na Roda ”. Popó's death, as he was known, was publicized on social media by the National Association of Transvestites and Transsexuals (Antra).
> 
> Paulo Vaz, a trans man who is a reference in the LGBTQIA+ community, dies
> “With great sadness we received the news that Paulo Vaz @popo_vaz left us”, began the association in a note published on Facebook.
> 
> “Paul was dear and loved by everyone around him. An activist engaged and dedicated to the trans struggle, he has always made a point of building bridges, acting in the fight against transphobia and in defense of transmale people. Gay trans man, civil police officer, he always approached questions about sexuality and gender in a light and relaxed way. He was such a sweet soul and a beautiful heart,” ANTRA said.
> 
> “Unfortunately we lost another one of us who could not bear to continue in such a violent and inhumane society. ANTRA sympathizes with Paulo's family and friends, especially dear Pedro HMC”. "Thank you for everything! We will continue in mourning, in the struggle”, concluded the association.




> Popó Vaz, police officer and Brazilian trans influencer was found dead
> 
> The Brazilian influencer and police officer known as Popó Vaz was found dead at his residence, in what - for now - is headed as a possible suicide.
> 
> The 37-year-old - whose real name was Paulo Vaz - was married to Pedro HMC. Regarding his death, the Mosaico agency explained: "Mosaico is GRIEVING for the departure of our hero PAULO VAZ."
> 
> “A gay trans man from the Civil Police of SP, an LGBTQIA+ activist and hero who shared his story, inspired and encouraged so many people,” can be read in the middle of the publication.
> 
> It is worth mentioning that the news about the death of the young man in uniform has generated a great impact on Brazilian social networks .
> 
> In this regard, the National Association of Transvestites and Transgender tweeted a note: "We have lost another person who could not bear to continue in such a violent and inhumane society."
>
