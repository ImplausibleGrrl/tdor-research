> Coimbatore: Transgender woman killed in accident
> 
> COIMBATORE: A transgender woman was killed after she fell off a bus while trying to board it near the Town Hall on Saturday evening. The deceased was identified as Sahana, 22, daughter of Kaja Hussain of Mettupalayam. She was staying at Gandhi Park with her friends.
> 
> “She was attempting to board a private bus plying between Gandhipuram and Seerapalayam, when she lost balance and fell on the road from the footboard. She sustained serious head injuries and died on the spot,” a police officer said. Police sent the body to the Coimbatore Medical College Hospital for postmortem.
