> Trans woman dies after car rolls over on ES-484
> The car stopped with its wheels up in a stream, on the side of the highway.
> Clovis Rangel / De Paula Communication | 11/14/2022 18:28 h | Updated on 11/14/2022, 21:11
>  
> A 44-year-old transgender woman died this Sunday afternoon (14) after the car she was in overturned on ES-484, in Limoeiro, in the interior of São José do Calçado, in the southern region of the state. The car stopped with its wheels up in a stream on the side of the highway.
> 
> Vander Paulo Silva de Jesus, who went by the social name Vander Paula, died at the scene.
> Vander Paulo Silva de Jesus, who went by the social name Vander Paula, died at the scene | Photo: Reproduction / Social Networks
>  
> Vander Paulo Silva de Jesus, who went by the social name Vander Paula and lived in São Jesus de Itabapoana, was in the passenger seat, got out of the car alone and asked for help. But, she fell dead beside the vehicle minutes later.
> 
> The 19-year-old driver and two women, 26 and 31, who were in the back seat, had injuries and were taken to hospitals in the region, where they were treated by teams from the Mobile Emergency Care Service (Samu).
> 
> Vander's body was sent to the Legal Medical Service of Cachoeiro de Itapemirim.
