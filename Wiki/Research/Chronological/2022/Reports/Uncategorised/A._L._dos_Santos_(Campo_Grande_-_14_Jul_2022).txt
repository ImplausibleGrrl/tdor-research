> Military Police and Samu were at the scene - (Photo: Reproduction, Do Leitor)
> A 39-year-old transvestite was found dead in the house where she lived, in Vila Carvalho, in Campo Grande. The case took place on the night of this Thursday (14) and the suspicion is that the death was due to natural causes.
> 
> According to the Military Police , friends of the victim were surprised by her disappearance and asked the owner of the property - where she lived on rent - to enter the place to see if everything was okay.
> 
> Upon entering the residence, the owner came across the victim's body in the room, already lifeless. The Samu (Mobile Emergency Care Service) was triggered and found the death. Police and forensic teams are at the scene. Information from acquaintances is that the victim suffered from high blood pressure.
