> They investigate the death of a trans woman in V. Carranza; there is a detail
> The Attorney General's Office (FGJ) of Mexico City is investigating the murder of a transsexual woman who was found dead on public roads.
> 
> The victim's body was located around 6:50 am this Sunday, in the middle of calle Grabados, almost on the corner of Peñón, in Venustiano Carranza.
> 
> The official part indicates that field police elements were alerted to a person lying on the public highway and upon arrival at the scene they observed a transsexual woman between 30 and 35 years old who was unconscious.
> 
> Paramedics certified that the victim no longer showed vital signs apparently derived from a head injury; later the corpse was lifted by experts from the capital prosecutor's office.
> 
> One person referred to the elements of the Mexico City Police that the trans woman was traveling on board an ATV that was driven by a subject and that apparently fell from said vehicle.
> 
> A few streets ahead the agents managed to locate the suspect and arrested him, they also secured a black four-wheeler.
> 
> The driver of the vehicle identified as José "N", 35 years old, was presented to the Public Ministry, where an investigation folder was started for the crime of manslaughter.
> 
> Through the expert reports and proceedings, the authorities will determine in the next few hours if the victim fell from the vehicle or if he was thrown by the arrested person.
