> They find a lifeless person on Plan de Ayala avenue
> Writing Monday, July 4
> Reading 1 - 2 minutes
> The find was reported at half past seven yesterday morning.
> Photographer @/ Alejandro López Arce
> Police assume she was run over Sunday morning.
> 
> A person was fatally run over on avenida Plan de Ayala, at the height of the town of Amatitlán, on Sunday morning.
> 
> The Police reported that at 07:30 a.m. they received a report announcing that there was a person lying on the corner of avenida Plan de Ayala Avenue and calle Carnero, at the height of the aforementioned town.
> 
> Paramedics confirmed that this person had passed away.
> 
> The internal information of the Police on the incident indicates: “Report of a person run over, the facts on Avenida Plan de Ayala Colonia Amatitlán, in the place of transit unit of the economic municipality 1757 official Joaquín Figueroa, and reports of an unconscious male on the asphalt , arrival of the rescue unit confirming the male with the absence of vital signs, the appropriate is carried out”.
> 
> It was later confirmed that it was a transsexual woman.
> 
> The body was described as having a slim build, dark complexion, and medium height.
> 
> The person was wearing a black blouse and blue shorts and white tennis shoes.
> 
> One of the lines of investigation indicates that she was run over by a vehicle, but so far there is no official information beyond that cited above.
