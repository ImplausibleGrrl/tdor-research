> Guwahati: Eunuch Found Dead In Hatigaon
> G Plus News (Jun 22, 2022)
> Dead body found in Hatigaon Guwahati
> 
> Guwahati: An eunuch was found dead at her rented accommodation at Sijubari in the Hatigaon area of Guwahati on Wednesday morning, June 22. 
> 
> The deceased has been identified as Bijulee Kinnar. 
>  
> The incident took place under the Hatigaon Police Station. According to Police, Bijulee had foam coming out of her mouth and her death is suspected to be a result of food poisoning. Some bottles of beer were found at the site of the incident, police said.
> 
> According to the Sub Inspector (SI) Bipul Das, the landlord of the house informed the Police that Bijulee was found lying unresponsive on her bed. 
> 
> Bijulee was last seen with her husband Sulaiman on Tuesday, a day before the incident took place. 
> 
> The Police further said that Sulaiman was missing during the whole investigation.
> 
> The body was later sent to the Gauhati Medical College and Hospital (GMCH) for post-mortem.
