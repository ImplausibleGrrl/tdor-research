> Man found dead in Lagarto village
> May 4, 2022
> 
> On Tuesday night (3), a 39-year-old man was found dead inside his house in the village of Quilombo, rural Lagarto.
> 
> It was around 11 pm when a military police garrison was called to investigate the information, arriving at the scene and finding the truth. According to the 7th Battalion, they immediately requested the SAMU, which confirmed the death and also requested the CIOSP Criminalistica and IML, due to the victim having recently suffered physical aggression according to SAMU.
> 
> The victim's brother informed the Military Police that he spent a few days in the hospital but could not specify the hospital or how many days it was. A Police Occurrence Report (ROP) was sent to the Lagarto Regional Police Station (DRL) for the necessary measures.
