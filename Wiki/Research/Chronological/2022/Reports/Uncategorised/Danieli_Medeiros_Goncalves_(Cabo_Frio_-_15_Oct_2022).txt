Danieli Medeiros Gonçalves died in hospital after being hit by two vehicles, the first of which fled the scene.

> The Civil Police of Cabo Frio , in the Lagos Region of Rio, is investigating the death of a [trans woman] who was run over in the early hours of this Friday (14).
> 
> The case was on Avenida Wilson Mendes, in the Porto do Carro neighborhood. The victim was identified as 26-year-old Danieli Medeiros Gonçalves. She was rescued by the Fire Department, but did not survive.
> 
> According to the NGO Grupo Iguais, which defends the rights of the LGBTQIA+ community, the victim's mother went to the IML to recognize the body this Friday afternoon. According to the NGO, the cars involved in the hit-and-run fled the scene. It is not yet known whether the crash was intentional or accidental.
