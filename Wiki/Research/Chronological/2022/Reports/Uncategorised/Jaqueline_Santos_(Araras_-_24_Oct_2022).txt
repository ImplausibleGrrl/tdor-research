> Transsexual is found dead in abandoned foundry in the eastern region of Araras, SP
> October 24, 2022
> The investigation work is being carried out by the Civil Police's intelligence sector.
> A transsexual known as Jaqueline Santos, 32, was found dead in an abandoned shed located on Lauro Beinotti Street, where the Fundição FTI do Brasil operated, in Industrial District IV, which is in the region of Parque Dom Pedro and Residencial Prefeito Jair Della Coletta , east side of Araras (SP).
> 
> After the forensic work, the body will be taken to the IML – Legal Medical Institute of Limeira (SP), where it will undergo an autopsy to determine the cause of death. The investigation work is being carried out by the Civil Police's intelligence sector.
> 
> According to the incident report, the victim showed no signs of violence and was occupying a room in the deactivated company where she was found. The burial time until the update of this post at 10:30 pm had not been released.



> On Monday, 24, two deaths were recorded in the municipality of Araras/SP, the first as a suspicious death and the second homicide, where a man was murdered with several gunshots. 
> 
> In the middle of the afternoon, around 2:00 pm, police investigator Diogo Francisco de Brito, on duty at CPJ, was informed by military police officers, Cabo PM Barros and Soldado PM Gentil, of the area police vehicle of the 2nd Cia. of the 36th BPM/I, who, after information arrived at COPOM, went to Rua Lauro Beinoti, Parque Tiradentes, on the outskirts of the city of Araras/SP, where a body had been located. 
> 
> According to the police, the victim, Jaildo dos Santos Souza, 32, would be Trans and chemically dependent, where he was occupying a “room” of a deactivated company in the aforementioned place for housing.
> 
> According to the incident report, apparently the victim's body showed no injuries, however, cautious, the civil police delegate, Dr. Tabajara Zuliane dos Santos, requested the presence of the Scientific Technical Police, and the body was released to the IML of the city of Limeira/SP.
