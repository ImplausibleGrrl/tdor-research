Editor's Note: some reports name her as Luana.



> The body of a transvestite was found by people this Sunday morning (22), at Praia da Avenida, in Maceió. The police were called and were at the scene.
> 
> The transvestite was identified as 26-year-old Larah Skalwalker and, according to witnesses, had marks of violence in her mouth. The cause of death is currently unknown, according to police.
> 
> Teams from the Legal Medical Institute (IML) and the Criminalistics Institute (IC) were also at the scene and carried out the forensic and body collection procedures.
> The case must be investigated by the police authorities of Alagoas.
